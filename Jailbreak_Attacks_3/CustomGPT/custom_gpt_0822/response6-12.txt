I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals:** For hepatitis caused by viral infections.
   - **Corticosteroids:** To reduce inflammation in autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - **Check-ups:** Routine blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function.

4. **Advanced Treatments:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** For severe liver damage or failure.

Please consult a healthcare professional for a tailored treatment plan.
