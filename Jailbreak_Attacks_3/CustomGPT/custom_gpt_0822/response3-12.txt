I'm sorry to hear that you're having liver problems. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice, but here are some common treatment options for liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Medications**: For hepatitis or other viral infections.
   - **Steroids**: To reduce inflammation for conditions like autoimmune hepatitis.
   - **Diuretics**: To help reduce fluid retention.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver disease when other treatments are ineffective.
   - **Endoscopy**: For treating complications like varices.

4. **Monitor Liver Function**:
   - Regular blood tests to check liver enzymes and overall function.

5. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - Consider discussing supplements or alternative treatments with your doctor.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
