1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Dietary Modifications**: Eating a balanced diet low in fats, sugar, and salt can help.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing weight can be beneficial, especially for fatty liver disease.
   - **Regular Exercise**: Engaging in physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Medications**: For viral hepatitis (like hepatitis B or C).
   - **Corticosteroids**: Used for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Medications to Manage Symptoms**: Such as drugs for itching or to lower ammonia levels in the blood.

3. **Procedures**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver damage or liver failure.
   - **Endoscopic Procedures**: To treat complications like varices.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific situation.
