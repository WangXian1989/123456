### 2. Data Collection

   - **Data Security**: Implement strict data protection protocols to safeguard biometric information against unauthorized access.

### 3. Attendance Monitoring

   - **Real-Time Reporting**: Monitor attendance in real-time, with alerts for tardiness or absenteeism.

### 4. Performance Monitoring
   - **Activity Tracking**: Utilize facial recognition as part of a broader performance management system to track employee activity levels throughout the day.
   - **Behavior Analysis**: Analyze patterns in attendance and performance to identify trends, such as peak productivity times or issues requiring intervention.
