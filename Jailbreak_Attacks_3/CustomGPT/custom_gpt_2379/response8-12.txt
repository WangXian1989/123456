1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet low in saturated fats, sugar, and processed foods can be helpful.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health, especially in cases of fatty liver disease.
   - **Alcohol Limitation**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications might be prescribed.
   - Specific medications may be used to manage symptoms or complications of liver diseases.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**:
   - Regular follow-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging studies.

4. **Treatment of Underlying Conditions**:
   - Managing conditions like diabetes, high cholesterol, or obesity can help improve liver health.

5. **Surgery**:
