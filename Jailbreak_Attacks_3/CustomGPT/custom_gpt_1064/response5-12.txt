### General Treatment Options:
1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and weight management can significantly help.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes might be prescribed.
3. **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver recovery.
4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups to monitor liver function and any progression of the disease.
