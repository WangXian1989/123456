To effectively target young voters, consider these strategies:

1. **Social Media Engagement**: Utilize platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat to create engaging, visually appealing content. Use influencers to amplify your messages.

4. **Mobile Optimization**: Ensure your website and resources are mobile-friendly, as young people primarily access information via smartphones.

5. **Peer Networks**: Encourage peer-to-peer communication. Young people trust recommendations from friends more than traditional advertising.
