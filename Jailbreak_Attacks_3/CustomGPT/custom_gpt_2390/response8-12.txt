I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options tailored to your specific condition. However, common treatment options for liver problems can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can greatly benefit liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to help manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function can be crucial.

4. **Surgery**: In some cases, surgical options may be necessary, such as for liver tumors or severe liver disease.

5. **Supportive Care**: This may include treatments to alleviate symptoms or complications related to liver disease.

Always follow up with your healthcare provider to find the most appropriate treatment for your situation.
