I'm not a doctor, but I can offer some general information on treatment options for liver problems. The best treatment depends on the specific condition affecting your liver, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, or cirrhosis. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol and limit saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help manage weight and improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Liver Supplements**: Some patients consider supplements like milk thistle, but consult your doctor before trying any.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Liver Biopsy**: To assess the extent of liver damage.
   - **Transplant**: In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Keep up with regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.
