### 1. **Facial Recognition for Attendance**

### 4. **Data Security and Privacy**

### 6. **Integration with HR Systems**
   - **Holistic Management**: Integrate biometric data with HR platforms to create comprehensive performance profiles, aiding in decisions related to promotions, raises, and training needs.

### 7. **Training and Support**
