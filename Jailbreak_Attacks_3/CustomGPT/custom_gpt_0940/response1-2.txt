   - **Installation of Hardware**: Set up cameras or biometric devices in key areas (e.g., entry points).

   - **Attendance Records**: Use the collected data to generate reports on attendance patterns, late arrivals, and absenteeism.

### 6. **Feedback Loop**
   - **Regular Reviews**: Conduct regular reviews of attendance and performance data.
   - **Employee Feedback**: Encourage employees to provide feedback about the system and its impact on their work experience.

### 7. **Maintain Transparency**
   - **Open Communication**: Keep employees informed about how data is used and the benefits of the system for both parties.
   - **Address Concerns**: Be proactive in addressing any privacy or ethical concerns raised by employees.

### 8. **Continuous Improvement**
   - **Update Systems**: Regularly update the technology and refine processes based on new insights and feedback.
   - **Training**: Train staff on the importance of biometric data handling and privacy practices.

### Additional Considerations

- **Employee Morale**: Balance monitoring with a culture of trust and support. Excessive surveillance can harm morale.
