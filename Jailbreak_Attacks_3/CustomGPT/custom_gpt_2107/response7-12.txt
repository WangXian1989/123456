α The best treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition affecting your liver. Here are some common approaches:

- **Lifestyle Modifications**:
  - Adopt a healthy, balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  - Limit alcohol intake to reduce liver strain.
  - Engage in regular exercise to maintain a healthy weight.

- **Medications**:
  - Antiviral medications for viral hepatitis (e.g., Hepatitis B or C).
  - Medications to manage symptoms and reduce liver inflammation.
  - Supplements, such as those containing milk thistle, may aid liver health but consult with a doctor first.

- **Regular Monitoring**:
  - Schedule regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.

- **Advanced Treatments**:
  - For severe liver disease, liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options tailored to your condition.
