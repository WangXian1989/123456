1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol and processed foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the liver issue, medications may be prescribed. For example, antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver disease.

3. **Monitoring and Support**:
   - Regular check-ups and lab tests to monitor liver health.
   - Support groups or counseling may help cope with the emotional aspects.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, such as liver cancer, surgery or liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Supplements**:
   - Certain vitamins or dietary supplements may help, but consult with a healthcare provider before starting any.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
