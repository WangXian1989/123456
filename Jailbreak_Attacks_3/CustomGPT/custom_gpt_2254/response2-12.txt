4. **Lifestyle Changes**: Regular exercise and maintaining a healthy weight can enhance liver function.

5. **Monitoring and Follow-Up**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider are essential for monitoring liver health and making necessary adjustments to treatment.

6. **Supplements**: Some people may consider supplements like milk thistle, but it's important to discuss this with a doctor first.

7. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical options such as liver transplantation might be necessary.
