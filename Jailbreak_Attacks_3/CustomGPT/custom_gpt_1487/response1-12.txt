It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for liver issues. Treatment depends on the specific condition, but common options may include:

1. **Lifestyle changes:** Diet modifications, avoiding alcohol, and regular exercise.
2. **Medications:** Depending on your specific liver disease (like antivirals for Hepatitis).
3. **Monitoring:** Regular check-ups to track liver function.
4. **Procedures:** In some cases, procedures like liver biopsy or even transplant may be necessary.

Always seek personalized advice from a doctor to address your unique situation effectively.
