6. **Data Security**: Ensure that biometric data is encrypted and stored securely to protect employee privacy. Implement strict access controls.

7. **Feedback and Adaptation**: Regularly review the data collected and solicit employee feedback to refine the system. Transparency about how data is used can help mitigate concerns.

8. **Ethical Considerations**: Be aware of the ethical implications. Ensure the system is used to support employees, not just monitor them. Promote a culture of trust and openness.
