1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats, refined sugars, and salt can help.

2. **Medications**:

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: 
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, liver surgery or liver transplantation may be necessary.

6. **Supplements**: 
   - Some supplements may support liver health, but always consult a healthcare provider.
