I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some information on common treatment options for liver issues. Treatment choices depend on the specific liver condition you're facing (like fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, etc.). Here are some general options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and sugars and high in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing weight if overweight can significantly benefit liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms, reduce inflammation, or treat infections (like antivirals for hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**:
   - Regular liver function tests to monitor liver health and detect any progression of disease.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**:
   - Abstaining from alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

5. **Natural Supplements**:
   - Some people consider supplements like milk thistle, but speak with your doctor before starting any new supplements.

6. **Surgery or Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.
