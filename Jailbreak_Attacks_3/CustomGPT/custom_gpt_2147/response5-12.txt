I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver health. For liver problems, treatment options vary based on the specific condition (like fatty liver disease, hepatitis, or cirrhosis). Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, reducing alcohol intake, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly improve liver function.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the diagnosis, medications may help treat viral hepatitis or reduce inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular blood tests and imaging can help track liver health and guide treatment.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation might be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some herbal remedies and supplements may help, but consult with a healthcare provider before trying them.
