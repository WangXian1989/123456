I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Medications:** 
   - Antiviral drugs for hepatitis
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune hepatitis

2. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - Avoid alcohol and drugs
   - Eat a balanced diet low in fats and sugars
   - Maintain a healthy weight

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional to determine the best treatment for your specific condition.
