### 5. **Performance Feedback**
- **Real-Time Feedback**: Use the collected biometric data to facilitate real-time performance feedback sessions, providing employees with insights into their attendance patterns and how it impacts their overall performance.

### 6. **Regular Reviews and Adjustments**
- **System Evaluation**: Regularly assess the effectiveness of these technologies and adjust processes based on employee feedback and performance metrics.
