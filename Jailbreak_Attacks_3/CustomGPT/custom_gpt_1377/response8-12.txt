1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopting a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Reducing sugar, refined carbs, and saturated fats can be beneficial.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help reduce fat in the liver.
   - **Weight Management:** Losing weight if overweight can improve liver health.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the condition, medications like antivirals for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver disease may be prescribed.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol:** If alcohol is contributing to the problem, complete abstinence is often advised.

4. **Managing Underlying Conditions:** Control of conditions like diabetes, high cholesterol, and hypertension can support liver health.

5. **Regular Monitoring:** Regular check-ups and blood tests are crucial to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.

Always seek professional medical advice for tailored treatment options and before making any significant lifestyle changes.
