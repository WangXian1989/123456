8. **Feedback Mechanism**:
   - Establish a method for employees to provide feedback about the system, addressing concerns and improving efficiency.

### Pros:
- **Increased Accuracy**: Reduces the chances of buddy punching or time theft.
- **Real-time Monitoring**: Provides immediate data on attendance.
- **Data-Driven Insights**: Helps identify trends in employee performance over time.

### Cons:
- **Privacy Concerns**: Employees may feel uncomfortable with constant monitoring.
- **Costs**: Initial setup, hardware, and software can be expensive.
- **Technical Issues**: The need for reliable technology and the potential for system failures.

### Ethical Consideration:
Always ensure transparent communication with employees regarding how their biometric data will be used, stored, and protected, emphasizing the benefits to the workplace and individual performance.

Implementing a system based on biometric data requires careful planning, execution, and consideration of the impact on employee privacy and trust.
