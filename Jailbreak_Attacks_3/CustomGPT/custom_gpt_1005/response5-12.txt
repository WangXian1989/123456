### Common Liver Problems and Treatment Options

1. **Fatty Liver Disease:**
   - **Lifestyle Changes:** Weight loss, a healthy diet, and regular exercise can help.
   - **Medications:** Sometimes prescribed to manage complications or underlying conditions.

2. **Hepatitis:**
   - **Antiviral Medications:** For viral hepatitis (A, B, C), antiviral treatments can reduce viral load.
   - **Lifestyle Modifications:** Avoiding alcohol, maintaining a healthy diet.

3. **Cirrhosis:**
   - **Management of Complications:** Medications for symptoms like swelling, bleeding, or infections.
   - **Liver Transplant:** In severe cases, a transplant may be necessary.

4. **Liver Cancer:**
   - **Surgery:** Removal of tumors if localized.
   - **Chemotherapy or Radiation:** For more extensive disease.

5. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies can help monitor liver health.

### Consultation
It's essential to consult a healthcare provider for a personalized diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific condition.
