1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopting a balanced diet low in fats and sugars can help. Foods rich in antioxidants, like fruits and vegetables, are beneficial.
   - **Alcohol Reduction**: Limiting or eliminating alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver, or cirrhosis), medications may be prescribed. For example, antiviral drugs for hepatitis or steroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and track the progress of treatment.

4. **Weight Management**:
   - If overweight, losing weight can significantly improve liver health, especially in cases of fatty liver disease.

5. **Exercise**:
   - Regular physical activity can support overall health and liver function.

Always follow up with your healthcare provider for personalized advice and treatment plans.
