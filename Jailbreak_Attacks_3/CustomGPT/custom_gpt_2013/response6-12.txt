1. **Lifestyle Modifications**:
   - **Dietary Changes**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can support liver health. Avoid fatty foods, added sugars, and alcohol.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Corticosteroids**: They may be prescribed for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**:
   - **Regular Blood Tests**: These can help monitor liver function and detect any changes early.
   - **Imaging Tests**: Ultrasounds or CT scans may be used to assess the liver's condition.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or, in extreme cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.
