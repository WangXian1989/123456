1. **Utilize Digital Platforms**:

   - Highlight topics such as climate change, education reform, and social justice that matter deeply to this demographic.
   - Use storytelling to connect real-life impacts of these issues, making them feel personal and urgent.

   - Organize virtual town halls, Q&A sessions, or live discussions that encourage participation and dialogue.
   - Incorporate gamification elements in campaigns to make engagement more appealing.
