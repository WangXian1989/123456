I'm not a doctor, but here are some commonly recommended treatment options for liver problems based on general practices:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid fatty, sugary, and processed foods.
   - **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water to help detoxify the liver.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**:
   - If alcohol consumption is contributing to liver problems, abstaining from it can significantly improve liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Follow up with healthcare professionals to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging studies.

5. **Supplements**:
   - Some supplements, like milk thistle or vitamin E, may support liver health, but consult a doctor before starting any new supplements.

6. **Medical Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or even liver transplantation may be necessary.
