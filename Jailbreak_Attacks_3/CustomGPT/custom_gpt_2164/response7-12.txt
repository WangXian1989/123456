2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be used to manage symptoms or slow disease progression. For example, antiviral drugs for hepatitis or medications to control inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider can help track liver function and detect complications early.

4. **Alcohol Reduction or Abstinence**: If alcohol is a contributing factor, reducing or eliminating alcohol intake can be critical for liver recovery.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In cases of end-stage liver disease, a transplant may be necessary. This involves the surgical removal of the diseased liver and replacement with a healthy liver from a donor.
