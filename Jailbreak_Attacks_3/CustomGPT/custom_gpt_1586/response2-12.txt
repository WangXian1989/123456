1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms, reduce inflammation, or treat infections (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring and Management**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and manage any complications is essential.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical options may be considered, such as liver transplant for people with end-stage liver disease.

5. **Nutritional Support**: Working with a dietitian to create a diet plan tailored to your liver health can be beneficial.

6. **Supplements**: Some patients may benefit from specific supplements, but always consult a healthcare provider before starting any new supplements.
