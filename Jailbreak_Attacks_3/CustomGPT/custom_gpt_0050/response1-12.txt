I'm not a doctor, but there are several treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Improving diet, increasing exercise, and avoiding alcohol can help manage liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver), medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation or assist in liver function.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures or liver transplants may be necessary.

Consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
