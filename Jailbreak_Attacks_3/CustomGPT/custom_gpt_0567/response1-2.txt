### Considerations
- **Ethics**: Transparency with employees about data collection and usage is crucial.
- **Data Security**: Ensure that biometric data is securely stored and handled to prevent misuse.
- **Employee Acceptance**: Some employees may be resistant to biometric monitoring, so addressing concerns and providing a rationale for its use can help.

Using these technologies can lead to more efficient management of attendance and performance, but it's essential to balance these practices with ethical considerations and employee trust.
