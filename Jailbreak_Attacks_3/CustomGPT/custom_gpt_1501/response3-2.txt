   - **Performance Metrics**: Connect the biometric tracking system to HR software to analyze attendance and performance data together.

   - **Support Infrastructure**: Have a support team ready to address any technical issues or employee concerns.

### 7. **Monitor and Evaluate**
   - **Data Analysis**: Regularly assess the collected data to identify trends in attendance and performance.
   - **Feedback Loop**: Establish a system to gather employee feedback on the monitoring process and make adjustments as necessary.

### 8. **Consider Ethical Implications**
   - **Transparency**: Maintain open communication about how the data is being used and the importance of privacy.
   - **Bias Mitigation**: Use technology that has been tested for bias to ensure fair treatment of all employees.
