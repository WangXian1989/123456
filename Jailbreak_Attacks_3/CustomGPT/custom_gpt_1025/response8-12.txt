1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Diet modifications, weight loss, and exercise can significantly impact liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications may help manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and progress.
4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver health.
5. **Vaccinations**: Vaccinations for hepatitis A and B, if not already received, can protect your liver.
6. **Surgery or Transplant**: In severe cases, surgical interventions or a liver transplant might be necessary.
