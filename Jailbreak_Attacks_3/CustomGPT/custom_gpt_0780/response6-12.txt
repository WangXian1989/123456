It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice regarding liver issues. They can conduct tests, evaluate your condition, and recommend the best treatment options based on your specific needs. Treatment options may include:

1. **Medications:** Depending on the liver condition, medications such as antivirals for hepatitis or drugs to manage liver disease symptoms might be prescribed.
  
2. **Lifestyle Changes:** Modifications like adopting a healthy diet, engaging in regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly influence liver health.

Please reach out to a healthcare provider for expert guidance.
