Common treatments for liver problems depend on the specific condition but may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Healthy diet and exercise.
   - Avoiding alcohol and certain medications.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antivirals for hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Procedures**: 
   - Endoscopy for varices.
   - Liver transplant in severe cases.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies to assess liver function.

Always follow your doctor's recommendations for the best outcomes.
