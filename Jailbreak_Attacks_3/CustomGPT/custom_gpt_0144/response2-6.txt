1. **Digital Engagement**: Utilize social media platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter. Create engaging content such as videos and memes that resonate with their interests.

2. **Relevant Issues**: Focus on issues that matter to young voters, such as climate change, student debt, and social justice. Frame these in relatable language.

3. **Peer Influence**: Leverage influencers and trusted voices within their communities to spread messages. Young people are more likely to engage with peers.

4. **Interactive Content**: Use quizzes, polls, and interactive campaigns to engage younger audiences actively. This makes them feel more involved.
