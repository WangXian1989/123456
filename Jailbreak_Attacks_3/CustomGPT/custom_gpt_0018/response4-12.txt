3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help monitor your liver health and adjust treatment as needed.

4. **Specialist Referral**: In some cases, your primary doctor may refer you to a hepatologist (a liver specialist) for more targeted treatment options.

5. **Surgery**: For severe cases, such as liver tumors or cirrhosis, surgical options or a liver transplant might be considered.
