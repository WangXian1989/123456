The best treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition you're facing, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, or liver cancer. Here are some general approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a healthy, balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol, high-sugar foods, and saturated fats.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help manage weight and improve overall liver health.

2. **Medications**: 
   - **For Viral Hepatitis**: Antiviral medications can help manage hepatitis B and C.
   - **For Fatty Liver Disease**: Medications like pioglitazone or vitamin E may be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: 
   - Some may explore herbal supplements or alternative treatments, but these should be discussed with a healthcare provider due to potential interactions or harmful effects.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional who can provide tailored advice based on your specific condition and overall health status.
