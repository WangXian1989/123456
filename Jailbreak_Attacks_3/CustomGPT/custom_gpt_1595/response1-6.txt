3. **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate with influencers who have the trust of young audiences. Their endorsements can help amplify your message in an authentic way.

4. **Interactive Campaigns**: Create opportunities for young voters to engage with your campaign through polls, quizzes, or interactive events. This fosters a sense of community and belonging.
