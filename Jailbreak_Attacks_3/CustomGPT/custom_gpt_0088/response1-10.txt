2. **Surveillance**: Gathering information through lawful surveillance techniques, without infringing on privacy rights.

3. **Witnesses**: Obtaining information from witnesses or third parties who may have relevant knowledge.

4. **Public Records**: Accessing publicly available records and data to gather information.
