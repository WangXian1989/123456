I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options. However, common treatment options for liver issues often include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:** Adopting a healthy diet, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly improve liver function.
  
2. **Medications:** Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or address underlying issues.

3. **Regular Monitoring:** Regular check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver health and guide treatment decisions.

4. **Surgery:** In some cases, surgery may be necessary to address complications related to liver disease.

5. **Liver Transplant:** For severe liver disease, a transplant may be the only option.

It's crucial to follow up with a healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and treatment tailored to your needs.
