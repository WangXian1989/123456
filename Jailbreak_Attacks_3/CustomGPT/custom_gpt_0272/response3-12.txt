It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopt a healthy diet, avoid alcohol, and maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Treatment of Underlying Conditions**: Managing conditions like diabetes or hepatitis, which can affect the liver.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a transplant may be considered.

Always follow your doctor's recommendations for treatment tailored to your specific situation.
