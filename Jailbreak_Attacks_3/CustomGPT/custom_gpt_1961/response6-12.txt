I'm not a doctor, but it's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice regarding liver issues. Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - Avoid alcohol and drugs that harm the liver.
   - Follow a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.

2. **Medications:**
   - Antiviral medications for viral hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune hepatitis.

3. **Procedures:**
   - Liver transplant for severe liver disease.
   - Surgery to remove tumors, if applicable.

Always get professional guidance for the best approach for your situation.
