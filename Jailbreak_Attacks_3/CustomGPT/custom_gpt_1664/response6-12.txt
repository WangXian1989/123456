I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver problems that you might consider discussing with a healthcare professional:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Emphasize a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein. Minimize fatty and sugary foods.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and support liver function.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), certain medications may be prescribed to help manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Regular blood tests and imaging may be necessary to monitor liver function and track any changes in your condition.

4. **Avoid Alcohol and Drugs:**
   - Eliminating alcohol and avoiding certain medications that can harm the liver is crucial.
