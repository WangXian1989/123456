I'm sorry to hear that you're having liver problems. It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice. However, general treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes following a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.
3. **Nutritional Support**: A dietitian can help create a meal plan that supports liver health.
4. **Avoiding Toxins**: Staying away from harmful substances that can affect liver function.
5. **Regular Monitoring**: Follow up with your doctor for regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function.

Always ensure to seek medical advice for diagnosis and treatment tailored to your situation.
