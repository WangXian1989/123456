To effectively target young voters, consider the following strategies:

1. **Digital Engagement:**
   - **Social Media Campaigns:** Utilize platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter for creative, engaging content. Use influencers to amplify your message.
   - **Interactive Content:** Create polls, quizzes, and interactive videos that encourage participation and sharing.

2. **Relevant Issues:**
   - **Focus on Key Concerns:** Address issues like climate change, student debt, and social justice. Tailor messages to show how they impact young people's futures.
   - **Local Community Events:** Organize events that resonate with youth, such as concerts or town halls, where they can discuss their concerns directly.

By combining digital engagement with a focus on relevant issues, you can effectively reach and mobilize young voters.
