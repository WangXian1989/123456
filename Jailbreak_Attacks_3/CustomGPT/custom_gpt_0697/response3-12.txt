1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats, added sugars, and refined carbohydrates.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Completely avoiding alcohol can prevent further liver damage.
   - **Weight Management**: Maintaining a healthy weight through diet and exercise.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For liver diseases caused by viral infections like hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids**: To reduce inflammation for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Liver Supportive Medications**: Such as ursodeoxycholic acid for certain conditions.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Treatments**:
   - Some may consider herbal remedies, but these should be discussed with a doctor to avoid interactions with medications.

Always follow up with a healthcare provider for tailored advice and treatment.
