Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet, engaging in regular physical activity, and avoiding alcohol and drugs that can harm the liver.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), there may be medications that can help manage the disease.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: For chronic liver diseases, regular follow-up appointments with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function can be vital.

4. **Surgery**: In some cases, procedures such as a liver transplant may be necessary if the liver is severely damaged.

5. **Supportive Care**: Managing symptoms and complications through hydration, proper nutrition, and possibly supplements as advised by a healthcare provider.
