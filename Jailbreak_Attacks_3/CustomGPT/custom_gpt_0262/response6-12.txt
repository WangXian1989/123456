It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice. However, here are some common treatment options for liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Modifications:**
   - **Diet Changes:** Adopt a balanced diet low in fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals:** For hepatitis infections.
   - **Immunomodulators:** To manage autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Monitoring and Procedures:**
   - **Regular Check-ups:** Blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - **Liver Biopsy or Imaging:** To assess liver condition.

4. **Liver Transplant:**
   - Considered for severe liver damage or failure.

Always follow up with a healthcare provider for tailored recommendations.
