It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan for liver issues. However, common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats, sugars, and salt.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals** for hepatitis.
   - **Steroids** for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Cholesterol-lowering drugs** if applicable.

3. **Supplements**: Certain supplements like milk thistle may support liver health, but consult a doctor first.

4. **Procedures**:
   - **Endoscopy** for variceal bleeding.
   - **Liver biopsy** for diagnosis and assessment.

5. **Transplant**: Severe cases may require a liver transplant.

Always seek personalized advice from a healthcare provider to determine the best course of action for your specific condition.
