1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can help improve liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to manage inflammation, treat infections, or alleviate symptoms.
3. **Monitoring and Follow-up**: Regular check-ups and imaging tests to monitor liver function may be advised.
