### Steps to Implement

2. **Data Capture**:

5. **Reporting and Analytics**:

6. **Feedback Mechanism**:

### Considerations

- **Legal Compliance**: Ensure that your use of biometric data complies with local laws regarding privacy and data protection.
- **Employee Consent**: Obtain informed consent from employees before implementing biometric monitoring.
- **Ethical Concerns**: Consider the ethical implications of monitoring employee performance closely. Strive for a balance between oversight and trust.
- **Transparency**: Clearly communicate to employees how data will be used and the benefits of such monitoring for both the company and employees.
