### Lifestyle Changes
- **Diet Modification**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars can help improve liver health.
- **Weight Management**: Achieving and maintaining a healthy weight may reduce fat accumulation in the liver.

### Medical Treatments
- **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications such as antivirals for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases may be prescribed.
- **Supplements**: Some supplements (like milk thistle) may support liver health, but consult your doctor first.

### Procedures
- **Endoscopy**: For conditions like varices, an endoscopic procedure may be necessary to prevent bleeding.
- **Liver Transplant**: For advanced liver disease, transplantation may be the only option.

### Regular Monitoring
- **Routine Check-ups**: Regular blood tests and imaging studies help monitor liver function and assess disease progression.

Consult a healthcare provider for a tailored treatment plan based on your specific liver condition.
