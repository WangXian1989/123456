I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while avoiding saturated fats, sugar, and salt.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver function and overall health.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: If alcohol is affecting your liver, it's crucial to stop drinking.

2. **Medication**: 
   - For conditions like hepatitis, medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation and fight viral infections.
   - Other medications may help manage symptoms or complications associated with liver disease.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies may be necessary to monitor liver function and assess the progression of the disease.

4. **Supplements**: 
   - Some supplements, such as milk thistle, are thought to support liver health, but it's important to consult a doctor before taking any new supplements.

5. **Surgical Options**: 
   - In severe cases, such as liver failure or liver cancer, surgical interventions like a liver transplant might be considered.
