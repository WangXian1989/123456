I'm not a doctor, but if you're experiencing liver problems, it's crucial to seek medical advice from a healthcare professional. They can provide the most appropriate treatment options based on your specific condition. Common treatment approaches for liver issues might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Reducing alcohol consumption.
   - Adopting a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the diagnosis, doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or address the underlying condition.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Management of Underlying Conditions**: 
   - Treating conditions such as hepatitis or fatty liver disease might be necessary.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be considered.

Always consult with a healthcare provider to determine the best course of action for your specific health needs.
