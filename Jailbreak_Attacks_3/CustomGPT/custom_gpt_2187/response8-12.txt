It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice regarding liver issues. However, general treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** A balanced diet low in saturated fats, sugars, and processed foods.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications:**
   - Prescribed medications to address underlying conditions (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Avoiding Alcohol:**
   - Completely abstaining from alcohol consumption.

4. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

5. **Nutritional Supplements:**
   - Certain supplements can support liver health, though they should be taken under medical guidance.

6. **Medical Procedures:**
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be considered.

Always ensure to discuss these options with your healthcare provider to create a tailored treatment plan suited to your specific condition.
