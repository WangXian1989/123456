### Treatment Options

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Dietary Adjustments:** Reducing fat, sugar, and salt intake.
   - **Regular Exercise:** Enhancing liver function and overall health.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals:** For viral hepatitis.
   - **Immunosuppressants:** For autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Procedural Interventions:**
   - **Liver Biopsy:** To assess damage and guide treatment.
   - **Endoscopy:** For varices or bile duct issues.

4. **Transplantation:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** For severe liver failure or certain liver diseases.

### Important Considerations
- **Regular Monitoring:** Keep track of liver function tests.
- **Consult a Specialist:** Work with a hepatologist for tailored treatment.
