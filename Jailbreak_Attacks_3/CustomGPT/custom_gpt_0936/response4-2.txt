1. **Compliance**:
   - Familiarize yourself with laws regarding biometric data collection (e.g., GDPR in Europe, CCPA in California) to ensure compliance.
   
2. **Transparency**:
   - Communicate clearly with employees about how their biometric data will be used, stored, and protected. Obtain informed consent.

3. **Privacy Protection**:
   - Implement robust security measures for data storage and processing to protect against breaches.
