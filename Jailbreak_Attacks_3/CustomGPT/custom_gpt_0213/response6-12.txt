For liver problems, treatment options vary depending on the specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral drugs**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids**: To reduce inflammation (like autoimmune hepatitis).

2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Dietary modifications**: Low-fat, high-fiber diet.
   - **Avoid alcohol**: Essential for liver health.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Blood tests**: To track liver function and monitor conditions.

4. **Surgery**:
   - **Liver transplant**: In severe cases or liver failure.

Always consult a healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
