Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:

   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.
   - **Avoiding Toxins**: Steering clear of certain chemicals and substances that can harm the liver.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver issue (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), doctors may prescribe antiviral medications, corticosteroids, or other drugs that target the underlying condition.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular monitoring of liver function through blood tests and imaging can help track the progression of liver disease and response to treatments.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures such as liver biopsy or even liver transplantation may be necessary.
