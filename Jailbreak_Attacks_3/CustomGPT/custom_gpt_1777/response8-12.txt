I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice, as they can provide guidance based on your specific condition. Generally, treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a balanced diet, reducing alcohol intake, and maintaining a healthy weight.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, certain medications may be prescribed.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular liver function tests to monitor your condition.
4. **Avoiding Toxins**: Staying away from substances that can worsen liver function.
5. **Surgery or Procedures**: In severe cases, surgery or procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.
