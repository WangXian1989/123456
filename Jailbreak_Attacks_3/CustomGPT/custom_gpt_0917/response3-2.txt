### 6. **Security Measures**
   - **Data Protection:** Implement strong cybersecurity measures to protect biometric data from breaches.
   - **Regular Audits:** Conduct regular audits of the systems and data usage to maintain integrity and compliance.

### 7. **Feedback Loop**
   - **Employee Feedback:** Establish processes for employees to provide feedback on the use of biometric monitoring tools.
   - **Continuous Improvement:** Use feedback and data analytics to enhance the monitoring systems and policies continuously.
