I'm sorry to hear you're dealing with liver issues. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. However, general treatment options can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a balanced diet, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight.
  
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.

4. **Possible Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

Discuss these options with your doctor to determine the best approach for your situation.
