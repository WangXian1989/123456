3. **Taxable Accounts**:
   - **Diversify Investments**: Use this account for short-term goals or as a complement to your retirement accounts. Focus on investments with favorable tax treatments, like long-term capital gains or tax-efficient funds.
   - **Cash Management**: Keep some cash available for emergencies or upcoming expenses so you're not forced to sell investments at a loss.

- **Rebalance Regularly**: Ensure your portfolio stays aligned with your goals as market conditions change.
