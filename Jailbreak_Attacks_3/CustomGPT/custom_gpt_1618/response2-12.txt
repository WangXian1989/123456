I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver treatment options. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Common treatment options for liver problems might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - A balanced diet low in fats and sugars.
   - Regular exercise to maintain a healthy weight.
   - Avoiding alcohol and harmful substances.

2. **Medications**:
   - Antiviral medications for hepatitis.
   - Medications to manage symptoms or complications associated with liver disease.
   - Supplements or vitamins as recommended by a physician.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - Treatments like endoscopy for varices in case of portal hypertension.
   - Liver biopsy to assess the degree of damage.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

Make sure to speak with a healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
