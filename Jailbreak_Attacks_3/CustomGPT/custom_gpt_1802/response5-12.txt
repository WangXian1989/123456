I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on liver problems and treatment options. The best treatment depends on the specific liver condition you're dealing with. Here are a few common liver issues and their treatment options:

1. **Fatty Liver Disease**:
   - Lifestyle changes: Weight loss, balanced diet, and regular exercise.
   - Medications: Sometimes, medications are used to control associated conditions like diabetes and cholesterol.

2. **Hepatitis**:
   - Antiviral medications: For hepatitis B and C, antiviral drugs can help manage the disease.
   - Lifestyle changes: Avoiding alcohol and maintaining a healthy diet.

3. **Cirrhosis**:
   - Treating the underlying cause: This could involve managing alcohol consumption, viral hepatitis, or addressing metabolic disorders.
   - Medications: To reduce symptoms and complications.
   - Liver transplant: In severe cases.

4. **Liver Cancer**:
   - Surgical options: Removal of the tumor or a liver transplant.
   - Chemotherapy and radiation: To target cancer cells.
   - Targeted therapies: Newer treatments that specifically target cancer cells.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare provider for a proper diagnosis and to discuss the best treatment plan tailored to your condition.

For personalized advice, please seek a healthcare professional.
