I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on treatment options for liver problems. It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and tailored advice. 

Treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle changes:**
   - Alcohol abstinence
   - Healthy diet, low in fat and sugar
   - Regular exercise

2. **Medications:**
   - Antiviral medications for hepatitis
   - Corticosteroids to reduce inflammation
   - Medications to manage symptoms

For more personalized and definitive treatment, please see a healthcare provider.
