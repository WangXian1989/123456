I'm not a doctor, but it's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet and regular exercise.
2. **Medication**: Depending on the condition (e.g., antivirals for hepatitis).
