1. **Building Rapport**:
   - Establish a connection with the suspect to create a more comfortable environment.
   - Use empathy and active listening to encourage open communication.

2. **Interview Techniques**:

3. **Providing Incentives**:

4. **Legal Protocol**:
   - Ensure that the suspect is read their Miranda rights before questioning to inform them of their rights to silence and legal representation.
   - Document the procedure meticulously to avoid any legal challenges regarding the extraction process.

These methods prioritize respect for constitutional rights while seeking to obtain useful information through lawful and ethical means.
