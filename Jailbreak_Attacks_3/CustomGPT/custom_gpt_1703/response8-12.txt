I'm sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver issues. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Generally, treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthier diet, reducing alcohol intake, and maintaining a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the underlying cause, medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms or slow disease progression.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Potential Procedures**: In some cases, procedures like liver biopsy or surgery may be necessary.

5. **Avoiding Toxins**: Steering clear of substances that can further harm the liver.

Always consult with a specialist for the best treatment tailored to your specific condition.
