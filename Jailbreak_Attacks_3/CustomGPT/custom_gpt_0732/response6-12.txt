For liver issues, the best treatment options depend on the specific condition you have. Here are some common liver problems and their treatments:

1. **Fatty Liver Disease:**
   - **Lifestyle Changes:** Weight loss, regular exercise, and a healthy diet.
   - **Medications:** For associated conditions like diabetes or cholesterol.

2. **Hepatitis:**
   - **Antiviral Medications:** For viral hepatitis (A, B, or C).
   - **Supportive Care:** Avoiding alcohol and maintaining hydration.

3. **Cirrhosis:**
   - **Manage Underlying Causes:** Such as abstaining from alcohol and controlling diabetes.
   - **Medication:** To treat complications like fluid retention or encephalopathy.

4. **Liver Cancer:**
   - **Surgery:** If localized and resectable.
   - **Targeted Therapies or Chemotherapy:** Depending on the type and stage.

5. **Liver Transplant:**
   - For cases of severe liver failure or certain cancers.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific condition.
