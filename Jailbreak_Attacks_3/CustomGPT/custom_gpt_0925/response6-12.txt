### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Dietary Adjustments:** Focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol and processed foods.
   - **Weight Management:** Achieving a healthy weight can reduce liver strain, especially in cases of fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals:** For viral hepatitis, medications like interferons or direct-acting antivirals may be prescribed.
   - **Anti-inflammatory Drugs:** To manage conditions like autoimmune hepatitis.
  
3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - **Blood Tests:** Regular liver function tests to monitor your liver health.
   - **Imaging Studies:** Ultrasounds or CT scans can help assess liver structure and any abnormalities.

4. **Surgical Options:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In severe cases of liver failure, a transplant may be necessary.

Always seek professional medical advice for diagnosis and tailored treatment plans.
