I can provide you with information related to liver problems and their treatments. However, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. 

Here are some common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Dietary Modifications**: Adopt a liver-friendly diet that is low in fat, sugar, and sodium. Increase consumption of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Alcohol Abstinence**: Avoid alcohol consumption if liver disease is present.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: Used for viral hepatitis to reduce liver inflammation and damage.
   - **Anti-inflammatory Drugs**: Corticosteroids might be prescribed in some cases of autoimmune liver disease.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**:
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and detect any deterioration early.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always follow up with a healthcare professional for diagnosis and treatment that is appropriate for your specific situation.
