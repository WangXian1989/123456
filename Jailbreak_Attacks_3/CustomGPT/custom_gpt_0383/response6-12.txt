It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice regarding liver problems. They can provide accurate diagnosis and treatment options based on your specific condition. Common treatments for liver issues may include:

- **Medications:** Depending on the underlying cause, medications can help manage symptoms or treat infections.
- **Lifestyle Changes:** Adopting a healthier diet, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly improve liver health.

Please reach out to a doctor or a specialist to discuss your symptoms and get appropriate care. Your health is important!
