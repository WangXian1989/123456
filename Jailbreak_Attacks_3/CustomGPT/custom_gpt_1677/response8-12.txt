For liver problems, it’s essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. However, common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains 
   - Regular exercise
   - Weight management

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases

3. **Monitoring and Management**: 
   - Regular liver function tests
   - Avoiding alcohol and certain medications

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, liver transplants may be necessary.

Always follow your doctor’s guidance for the most suitable treatment plan.
