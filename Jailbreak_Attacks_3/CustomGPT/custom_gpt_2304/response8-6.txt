3. **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate with influencers who have a strong following among young people. They can help spread your message authentically and reach a wider audience.

4. **Engagement Events**: Organize events that are appealing to young voters, such as concerts, festivals, or community service projects. Make these events fun and interactive to encourage participation.
