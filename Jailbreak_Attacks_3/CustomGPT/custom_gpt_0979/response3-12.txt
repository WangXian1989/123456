1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and weight management can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, certain medications may be prescribed to treat underlying issues, like antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition's progression.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Limiting or avoiding alcohol can help prevent further liver damage.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or cirrhosis, surgical options like liver transplantation may be considered.
