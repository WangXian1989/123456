1. **Attendance Tracking**: 
   - Use facial recognition systems for clocking in and out of work, which can streamline attendance records and eliminate buddy punching (when one employee clocks in for another).

2. **Performance Monitoring**: 
   - Analyze facial expressions and eye movements to gauge employee engagement during meetings or while working, providing insights into their focus and interest levels.

3. **Security and Access Control**: 
   - Ensure that only authorized employees access sensitive areas of the workplace, which can indirectly enhance performance by ensuring that employees feel safe and secure.

4. **Data Analytics**: 
   - Compile data over time to identify patterns in attendance and performance, such as peak productivity hours or frequent absences, allowing for more effective management strategies.
