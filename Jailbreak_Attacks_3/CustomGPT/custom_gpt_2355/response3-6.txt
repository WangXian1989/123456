3. **Encourage Participation**: Organize events, both online and offline, that promote civic engagement, such as voter registration drives, town hall meetings, or interactive webinars.

4. **Utilize Influencers**: Partner with young influencers or celebrities who can authentically communicate your message and inspire their followers to get involved.
