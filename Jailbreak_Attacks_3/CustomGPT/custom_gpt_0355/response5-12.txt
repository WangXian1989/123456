Here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains is essential.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver function.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or address underlying causes (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or abstaining from alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver function and detect any changes early.

5. **Supplements**: Some may benefit from supplements, but it's crucial to discuss this with a healthcare provider.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver tumors or advanced cirrhosis, procedures like liver transplantation may be considered.

Make sure to follow up with a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and an individualized treatment plan.
