It's important to consult a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment options for liver issues, as they can vary widely depending on the underlying condition. Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:  
   - Avoiding alcohol consumption.
   - Maintaining a healthy weight through diet and exercise.
   - Implementing a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.

2. **Medications**:  
   - Antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis.  
   - Medications to manage symptoms and complications, such as diuretics for fluid retention.

3. **Monitoring and Follow-up**:  
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.  
   - Imaging studies (like ultrasounds or CT scans) to assess liver health.

4. **Surgery**:  
   - In severe cases, liver transplantation may be necessary. 

Always follow medical advice tailored to your specific situation.
