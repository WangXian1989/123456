3. **Implementation**:
   - Install necessary hardware (cameras, scanners) in key areas like entry points.
   - Integrate biometric systems with existing HR or attendance management software.

4. **Data Collection**:
   - Establish protocols for collecting and storing biometric data securely. Ensure data encryption and compliance with privacy legislation.

5. **Monitoring Attendance**:
   - Use facial recognition systems to log employee attendance automatically. Employees could simply walk into the workplace, and the system registers their presence.

6. **Performance Monitoring**:
   - Track employee performance through metrics linked to attendance patterns (e.g., absences, tardiness) and productivity (e.g., hours logged).
