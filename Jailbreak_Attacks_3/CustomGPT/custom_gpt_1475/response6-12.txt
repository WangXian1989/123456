   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms and improve liver function.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver health.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: 
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption to prevent further liver damage.

5. **Treating Underlying Conditions**: 
   - Managing conditions such as hepatitis or fatty liver disease.
