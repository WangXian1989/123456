1. **Medication:** Depending on the type of liver problem, medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms or reduce inflammation.

2. **Lifestyle Changes:** Adopting a healthy diet, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly benefit liver health.

3. **Regular Monitoring:** Keeping track of liver function through regular check-ups and blood tests can help manage your condition.
