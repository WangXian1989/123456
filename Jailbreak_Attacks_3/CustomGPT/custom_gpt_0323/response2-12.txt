1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats, sugars, and refined carbs can help. Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water to aid liver function.
   - **Alcohol**: Limit or eliminate alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on your specific liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to treat the underlying issue.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Periodic blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and health.

4. **Exercise**:
   - Regular physical activity can improve liver health and reduce fat accumulation.

5. **Supplements**:
   - Some may benefit from supplements like vitamin E or milk thistle, but consult your doctor first.

6. **Manage Underlying Conditions**:
   - Control conditions like diabetes or high cholesterol, as they can affect liver health.
