It's important to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific liver problem. However, general treatment options for liver conditions may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Avoid alcohol and drugs that can harm the liver.
   - Maintain a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Exercise regularly to manage weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation, manage symptoms, or treat underlying causes.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Vaccinations**: 
   - Vaccines for hepatitis A and B if at risk.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, surgical interventions such as a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always discuss your symptoms and options with a healthcare provider for the most appropriate advice.
