1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver), specific medications may be prescribed.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**:
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake to prevent further liver damage.

5. **Weight Management**:
   - Losing weight if overweight can improve liver health, especially for non-alcoholic fatty liver disease.

6. **Viral Hepatitis Treatment**:
   - Antiviral medications may be necessary for viral hepatitis.

7. **Potential Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be considered.

Always seek personalized advice from a healthcare provider.
