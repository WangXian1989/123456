It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice, but generally, treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Avoid alcohol and drugs.
   - Maintain a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Exercise regularly to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation, manage symptoms, or treat underlying infections.

3. **Medical Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures such as liver biopsy or surgery may be necessary. 

4. **Monitoring and Management**: Regular check-ups and monitoring liver function through blood tests are critical to managing liver health.
