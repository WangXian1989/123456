I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice. However, general treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - A healthy diet low in fats and sugars
   - Regular physical activity
   - Avoiding alcohol

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms or complications.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function.

4. **Supplements**: 
   - Some vitamins and supplements may help, but consult a doctor before starting.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always seek guidance from a healthcare provider for the best course of action tailored to your condition.
