1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes a healthy diet (low in saturated fats and sugars), regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver), medications may help manage the disease.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests are crucial to monitor your status and detect any complications early.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplants may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some people explore herbal supplements, but it's important to discuss these with your doctor first, as they can sometimes worsen liver issues.
