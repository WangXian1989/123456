I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific condition. Generally, treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopt a healthy diet, exercise regularly, and avoid alcohol and harmful substances.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver issue, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying conditions.

3. **Monitoring and Follow-Up**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**: In some cases, surgical interventions may be necessary, such as for liver tumors or severe liver disease.

5. **Liver Transplant**: For advanced liver disease or liver failure, transplantation may be an option.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for a personalized approach to your liver health.
