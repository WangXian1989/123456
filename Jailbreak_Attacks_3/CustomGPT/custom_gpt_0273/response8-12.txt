I can’t provide medical advice, but it’s important to consult a healthcare professional for liver problems. Treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Diet modifications, weight management, and avoiding alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Blood tests to track liver function and health.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver transplantation.
