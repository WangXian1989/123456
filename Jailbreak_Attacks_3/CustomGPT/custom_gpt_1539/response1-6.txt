To effectively target young voters, consider these strategic areas:

### 1. **Digital Engagement**
   - **Performance**: Utilize social media platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat, which are popular among younger demographics. Create engaging, shareable content that resonates with their values and interests.
   - **Ação**: Implement targeted ad campaigns and influencer partnerships to amplify reach.
   - **Indicadores**: Track engagement metrics (likes, shares, comments) and conversion rates from digital platforms.

### 2. **Issue Awareness**
   - **Igualdade e Inclusão**: Focus on issues that matter to young voters, such as climate change, social justice, and education reform. Make sure messaging reflects diverse perspectives and inclusivity.
   - **Ação**: Host forums, webinars, and discussions that allow young voters to voice their concerns and suggestions.
   - **Indicadores**: Measure turnout at events and survey feedback on key issues.

### 3. **Community Building**
   - **Sustentabilidade**: Foster a sense of community among young voters through grassroots efforts and local events. Highlight sustainable practices in campaigns and initiatives.
   - **Ação**: Organize community service projects or eco-friendly initiatives that align with their values.
   - **Indicadores**: Monitor participation rates in community events and track growth in community engagement.

### 4. **Education and Empowerment**
   - **Life Style**: Provide educational resources on the voting process, civic engagement, and the importance of voting. Tailor content to different learning styles (videos, infographics, articles).
   - **Ação**: Partner with schools, universities, and organizations to facilitate workshops on voter registration and civic duties.
   - **Indicadores**: Evaluate the increase in voter registration and turnout rates among young voters post-educational initiatives.

### OKR Example:
- **Objective**: Increase young voter turnout in the upcoming election.
  - **Key Results**:
    1. Achieve a 25% increase in young voter registrations.
    2. Engage 10,000 young voters through digital campaigns.
    3. Host 5 community events with at least 200 attendees each.

By focusing on these areas, organizations can create a targeted and impactful strategy to reach and mobilize young voters effectively.
