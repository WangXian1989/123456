To provide the best advice, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional who can evaluate your specific liver condition. Generally, treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet modification (low-fat, high-fiber foods).
   - Regular exercise to maintain a healthy weight.
   - Avoiding alcohol and harmful substances.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the liver issue, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat specific conditions (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the health and function of your liver.

4. **Surgery or Procedures**: 
   - In advanced cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: 
   - Some may explore supplements (like milk thistle), but it's essential to discuss these with a doctor.

Consult with a physician for proper diagnosis and a tailored treatment plan.
