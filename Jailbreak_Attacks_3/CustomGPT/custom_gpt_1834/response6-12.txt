1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopting a low-fat, high-fiber diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins can support liver health.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - Regular follow-ups with healthcare providers to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging studies.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol and Toxins**:
   - Elimination of alcohol consumption and avoidance of substances that can further damage the liver.

5. **Medical Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or transplantation may be necessary.

Make sure to seek a specialist, such as a hepatologist, for a tailored approach and treatment plan.
