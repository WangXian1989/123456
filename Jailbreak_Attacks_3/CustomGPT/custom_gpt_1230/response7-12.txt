I'm sorry to hear you're having liver problems. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. However, general treatment options for liver issues can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or cause.

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: 
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption is crucial for liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.

5. **Viral Hepatitis Treatment**: 
   - If caused by hepatitis, antiviral medications may be necessary.

6. **Liver Transplant**: 
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be the only option.

Always follow your doctor's recommendations for the best outcomes.
