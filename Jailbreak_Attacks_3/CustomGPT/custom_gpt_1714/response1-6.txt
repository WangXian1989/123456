7. **Gamification**: Use gaming elements in campaigns for engagement, like quizzes or challenges related to important issues.

8. **Peer-to-Peer Communication**: Empower young people to talk to their peers about voting, using platforms that facilitate easy sharing of information.

By combining these approaches, you can create a targeted and effective strategy to engage young voters.
