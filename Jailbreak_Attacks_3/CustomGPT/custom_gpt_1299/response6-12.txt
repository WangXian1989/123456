1. **Lifestyle Modifications**:
   - Healthy Diet: Incorporate fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while avoiding alcohol and high-fat foods.
   - Regular Exercise: Engage in physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

   - In more severe cases, procedures such as liver biopsies or even liver transplantation might be discussed.
