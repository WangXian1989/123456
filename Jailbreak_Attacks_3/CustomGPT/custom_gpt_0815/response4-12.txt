1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Incorporating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains can support liver health. Reducing fats, sugars, and processed foods is also beneficial.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat infections.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**:
   - Completely abstaining from alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging.
