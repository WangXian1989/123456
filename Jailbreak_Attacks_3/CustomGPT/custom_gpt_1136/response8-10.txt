1. **Interrogation Techniques**: Officers can use non-coercive interrogation techniques, such as building rapport, active listening, and open-ended questions.

2. **Miranda Warnings**: Suspects must be informed of their rights to remain silent and to an attorney before any interrogation. This ensures that any information obtained is admissible in court.

3. **Voluntary Statements**: Officers can encourage suspects to provide information voluntarily, ensuring that they understand they are not under obligation to speak.
