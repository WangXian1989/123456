I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems that you might consider discussing with your healthcare provider:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet and regular exercise can help improve liver function. Reducing alcohol intake is also crucial.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or cirrhosis), various medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Dietary Supplements**: Certain supplements (like milk thistle and vitamin E) may support liver health, but consult with a doctor before starting any.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures such as liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver function and progress.
