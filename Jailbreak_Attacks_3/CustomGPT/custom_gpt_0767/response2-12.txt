I'm not a doctor, but I can share some common approaches for managing liver issues. Treatment options typically vary based on the specific liver condition you have:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid high-fat and sugary foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver stress.
   - **Alcohol**: Avoid alcohol entirely, as it can worsen liver damage.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, specific medications may be prescribed. For instance:
   - **Antivirals** for viral hepatitis.
   - **Diuretics** for fluid retention in cases of cirrhosis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant might be necessary.

5. **Complementary Therapies**: Some patients explore herbal supplements, but it's crucial to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any alternative treatments.
