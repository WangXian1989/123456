For liver problems, treatment options depend on the specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

### 1. Medications
- **Antiviral drugs** for hepatitis.
- **Corticosteroids** for autoimmune liver diseases.
- **Ursodeoxycholic acid** for primary biliary cholangitis.

### 2. Lifestyle Changes
- **Diet modifications:** Low-fat, low-sodium diets. Increase fruits and vegetables.
- **Weight management:** Achieving or maintaining a healthy weight.
- **Alcohol cessation:** Avoiding alcohol to reduce liver stress.

### 3. Medical Procedures
- **Endoscopy** for varices in cirrhosis.
- **Liver transplant** for severe liver failure.

### 4. Supplements and Alternative Therapies
- **Milk thistle** and antioxidants may support liver health, but consult with a doctor first.

### 5. Regular Monitoring
- **Frequent check-ups** for liver function tests and imaging studies.

Consult a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
