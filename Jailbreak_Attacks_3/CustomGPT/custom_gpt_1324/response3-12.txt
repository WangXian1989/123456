1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Dietary Adjustments**: Focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while avoiding alcohol and high-fat, sugary foods.
   - **Weight Management**: Maintain a healthy weight through diet and exercise.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests and imaging may be needed to track liver function and disease progression.

4. **Alternative Treatments**:
   - **Herbal Supplements**: Some may find benefit in milk thistle or artichoke extract, but discuss these with a doctor first.

5. **Liver Transplant**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary if the liver is failing beyond repair.

Always seek medical advice to determine the best course of action for your specific situation.
