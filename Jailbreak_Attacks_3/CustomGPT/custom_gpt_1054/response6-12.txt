It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized guidance on liver problems. Treatment options may vary based on the specific condition affecting your liver. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet low in fats and sugars, rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help manage weight and improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Medications**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral drugs may be prescribed.
   - **Liver Supplements**: Certain supplements can support liver function, though their effectiveness varies.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Endoscopy**: To manage complications like variceal bleeding.
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a transplant may be necessary.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Routine Check-ups**: Regular appointments with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function tests are crucial.

Consult a specialist for a thorough evaluation and a tailored treatment plan.
