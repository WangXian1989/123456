It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver issues. Common treatment options might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may help manage symptoms or underlying causes.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.
4. **Avoiding Toxins**: Steering clear of substances harmful to the liver, like certain medications or chemicals.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical options such as a liver transplant might be considered.

Always follow your doctor's recommendations for the best outcome.
