### Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - **Alcohol Reduction**: Limit or eliminate alcohol consumption to reduce liver strain.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications like tenofovir or entecavir may be prescribed.
   - **Steroids and Immunosuppressants**: In cases of autoimmune hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Blood Tests**: To monitor liver function and enzyme levels.
   - **Imaging Tests**: Such as ultrasounds or MRIs to assess liver structure.

4. **Surgical Options**:
   - **Liver Transplantation**: In severe cases of liver failure or cirrhosis.
   - **Biliary Surgery**: To relieve obstructions or complications.

5. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - **Herbal Remedies**: Such as milk thistle, but consult your doctor before trying these.
