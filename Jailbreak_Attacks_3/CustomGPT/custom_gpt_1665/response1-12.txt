1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Avoiding alcohol and drugs.
   - Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Maintaining a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), your doctor may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Supplements**: 
   - Some may benefit from certain vitamins or supplements, but this should only be done under the guidance of a doctor.

5. **Surgery or Transplant**: 
   - In severe cases, surgical options or organ transplant may be considered.

6. **Support Groups**: 
   - Joining support groups can also help you cope with the emotional aspects of liver disease.
