I’m sorry to hear that you’re dealing with liver issues. It’s essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Generally, treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Implementing a healthy diet, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed.
