   - **Alerts and Notifications**: Set up automated alerts for irregular attendance patterns to address performance issues promptly.

### 6. **Training and Support**
   - **Employee Training**: Educate employees on how the system works and its benefits to mitigate concerns around surveillance.
   - **Technical Support**: Provide ongoing technical support to address any issues with biometric devices or software.

   - **Monitoring Limitations**: Ensure that monitoring is justified and used to support employee development rather than punitive measures.
