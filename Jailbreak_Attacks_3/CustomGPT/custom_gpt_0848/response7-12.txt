1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), your doctor may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Monitoring and Follow-Up**: Regular check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver function and detect any changes early.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or advanced cirrhosis, surgical options might be necessary, including liver transplantation.

5. **Supportive Therapies**: Nutritional support and other therapies can help manage symptoms and improve quality of life.
