1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars can help. Foods rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats are often beneficial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help manage weight and reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral drugs** may be prescribed for viral hepatitis.
   - **Anti-inflammatory drugs** can be used to manage inflammation.
   - **Medications for fatty liver disease** may include insulin sensitizers or, in some cases, vitamin E.

3. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - Imaging tests (like ultrasounds or MRIs) to assess liver health.

4. **Avoiding Toxins**: 
   - Limiting alcohol consumption and avoiding substances that can harm the liver.
