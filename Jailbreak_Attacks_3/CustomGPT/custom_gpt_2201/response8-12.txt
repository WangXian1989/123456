I'm really sorry to hear that you're having liver issues. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. However, here are some general treatment options that are often considered:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver problem, medications may help manage symptoms or slow disease progression.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and tests to monitor liver function.
