When addressing liver problems, the best treatment options depend on the specific condition and its severity. Here are key approaches:

- **Lifestyle Modifications**:
  - Adopt a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  - Avoid alcohol and limit exposure to toxins.
  - Maintain a healthy weight through regular exercise.

- **Medical Treatments**:
  - **Medications**: Follow prescribed medications to manage conditions like hepatitis or fatty liver disease.
  - **Monitoring**: Regular liver function tests and imaging studies to monitor progress.
  - **Interventions**: For severe conditions, options may include liver surgery or transplantation.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a tailored treatment plan.
