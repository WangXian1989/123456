### 5. **Employee Feedback and Involvement**

   - **Voluntary Participation:** Where possible, make participation in biometric monitoring voluntary to promote a sense of agency among employees.

### 6. **Ethical Considerations**
   - **Avoiding Misuse:** Establish clear policies on the appropriate use of biometric data to prevent misuse or discrimination.
   - **Building Trust:** Foster a workplace culture that values trust and transparency, reassuring employees that the data is used to support their success, not to micromanage them.
