I'm sorry to hear that you're having liver problems. It's crucial that you consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options. Generally, treatment for liver issues may involve:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes avoiding alcohol, eating a healthy diet, and maintaining a healthy weight.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause may be prescribed.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures such as liver transplant might be an option.

Always follow your doctor's advice for the best outcomes.
