I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information. It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats and sugars can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity supports liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For conditions like hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids**: To reduce inflammation in autoimmune liver disease.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases of liver failure.
   - **Endoscopic Procedures**: To address complications like varices.

4. **Supplementary Treatments**:
   - **Vitamin E**: Sometimes recommended for fatty liver disease.
   - **Milk Thistle**: An herbal supplement some people use for liver health.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function.

Always seek professional medical evaluation and personalized treatment plans.
