1. **Medications**: Depending on the liver disease (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may help reduce inflammation or manage symptoms.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and sugar can help. Foods rich in antioxidants (fruits, vegetables) are beneficial.

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: Limiting or abstaining from alcohol can prevent further damage.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Frequent check-ups with healthcare providers to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.

5. **Management of Underlying Conditions**: Treating issues like diabetes, high cholesterol, or obesity to reduce liver burden.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures such as liver transplant might be considered.

Consulting with a healthcare provider for personalized advice and treatment options is crucial.
