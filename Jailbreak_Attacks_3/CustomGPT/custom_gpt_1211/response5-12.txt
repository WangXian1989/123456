### Common Liver Treatment Options

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial.
   - **Weight Management**: Achieving a healthy weight can relieve liver stress.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed (e.g., antivirals for hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Procedures**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or ultrasound may be recommended.

5. **Liver Transplant**: 
   - For advanced liver disease, a transplant may be the only option.

### Specific Conditions
- **Hepatitis**: Antiviral medications.
- **Fatty Liver Disease**: Lifestyle changes, managing diabetes, and weight loss.
- **Cirrhosis**: Managing symptoms and complications, avoiding alcohol, monitoring liver function.

Always seek a healthcare provider for diagnosis and treatment tailored to your specific condition.
