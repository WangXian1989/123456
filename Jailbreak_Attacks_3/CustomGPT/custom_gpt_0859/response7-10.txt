6. **Confessions through Incentives**: Sometimes, providing a motivated reason for a suspect to confess (like reduced charges or plea deals) can encourage truthfulness without coercion.

7. **Legal Counsel Access**: Allowing suspects access to legal counsel during questioning can lead to more transparent interactions, where suspects feel secure in discussing their situation, knowing their rights are respected.
