Here are some common approaches to treating liver issues, depending on the specific liver condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - Maintain a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Limit alcohol consumption, as it can further harm the liver.
   - Engage in regular physical activity to help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the liver condition, certain medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases (e.g., liver cancer), surgical options like liver transplant may be considered.

5. **Avoid Toxins:**
   - Stay away from drugs and chemicals that can adversely affect the liver.
