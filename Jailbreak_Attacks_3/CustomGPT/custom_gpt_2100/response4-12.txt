The best treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition affecting your liver. Here are some common liver issues and their corresponding treatments:

1. **Fatty Liver Disease**:
   - **Lifestyle Changes**: Weight loss, exercise, and a balanced diet.
   - **Medications**: In some cases, medications to control diabetes or cholesterol.

2. **Hepatitis**:
   - **Antiviral Medications**: For chronic hepatitis B and C.
   - **Interferon**: Sometimes used for hepatitis C.

3. **Liver Cirrhosis**:
   - **Manage Complications**: Treat issues such as fluid buildup and infections.
   - **Lifestyle Changes**: Avoid alcohol, a low-sodium diet.

4. **Liver Cancer**:
   - **Surgery**: For localized tumors.
   - **Chemotherapy or Immunotherapy**: To manage advanced cancer.
   - **Radiation Therapy**: Sometimes used.

5. **Liver Transplant**:
   - For severe cases or end-stage liver disease.

6. **Medications for Specific Conditions**: Depending on the cause, such as autoimmune hepatitis or Wilson's disease.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
