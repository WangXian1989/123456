It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver problems. That said, common treatment options for liver issues can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes, such as antivirals for hepatitis.

3. **Avoiding Toxins**:
   - Limiting alcohol consumption and avoiding certain medications that can be harmful to the liver.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.

5. **Specifically for Conditions**:
   - If diagnosed with conditions like fatty liver disease or cirrhosis, tailored treatment plans might be necessary.
