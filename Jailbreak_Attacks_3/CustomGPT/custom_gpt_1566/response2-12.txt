1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats can help. Avoid alcohol and high-fat, processed foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve overall liver health and help with weight management.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis (e.g., hepatitis B or C).
   - **Corticosteroids**: To reduce inflammation for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Diuretics**: To manage fluid retention in conditions like cirrhosis.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function and detect any progression of liver disease.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases (like cirrhosis), liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Supplements**:
   - Some may benefit from supplements, but consult a healthcare provider first.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a personalized diagnosis and treatment plan based on your specific condition.
