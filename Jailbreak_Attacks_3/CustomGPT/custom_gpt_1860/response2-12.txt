1. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, options may include antiviral medications for hepatitis, corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases, or drugs to manage symptoms.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

3. **Weight Management**: For conditions like fatty liver disease, losing weight can help reduce fat in the liver.
