   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can support liver health. Reducing salt intake and avoiding alcohol is also crucial.

3. **Monitoring and Follow-up**:
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests are crucial to monitoring the condition and adjusting treatment as necessary.

4. **In advanced cases**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver damage or liver failure, a transplant may be the only option.
