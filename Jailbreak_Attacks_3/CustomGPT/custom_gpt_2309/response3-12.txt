For liver problems, treatment options vary based on the specific condition. Here are some common liver issues and their corresponding treatments:

1. **Fatty Liver Disease**:
   - **Lifestyle Changes**: Weight loss, healthy diet, and regular exercise.
   - **Medications**: Insulin sensitizers or vitamin E supplements may be recommended.

2. **Hepatitis**:
   - **Viral Hepatitis**: Antiviral medications (e.g., for Hepatitis B and C).
   - **Autoimmune Hepatitis**: Corticosteroids and immunosuppressants.

3. **Liver Cirrhosis**:
   - **Treating the Underlying Cause**: Alcohol cessation, antiviral therapy for hepatitis.
   - **Medications**: Diuretics for fluid retention, and beta-blockers for portal hypertension.

4. **Liver Cancer**:
   - **Surgery**: Resection or liver transplant.
   - **Other Treatments**: Chemotherapy or targeted therapy.

5. **General Support**:
   - **Diet**: Low-fat, high-fiber diet; limit alcohol.
   - **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with liver function tests.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice based on your specific condition.
