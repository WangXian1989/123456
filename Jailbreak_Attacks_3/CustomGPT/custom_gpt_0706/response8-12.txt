I'm sorry to hear that you're having liver problems. It's really important to consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific condition. In general, treatment options for liver problems can vary depending on the underlying cause and may include:

1. **Lifestyle changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and limiting alcohol intake can greatly benefit liver health.
2. **Medications**: Certain medications can help manage liver conditions, such as antiviral medications for hepatitis.
3. **Dietary adjustments**: Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can support liver function.
4. **Regular check-ups**: Monitoring liver function through regular blood tests can help track the progression of liver disease.

Please make sure to discuss any symptoms or treatment options with a healthcare professional for the best care.
