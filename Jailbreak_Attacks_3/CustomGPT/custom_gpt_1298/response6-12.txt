1. **Lifestyle Changes:**

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups:**
   - Regular follow-up with a healthcare provider for monitoring liver function through blood tests and imaging.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol:**
   - Abstaining from alcohol is crucial for many liver conditions.

5. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.
