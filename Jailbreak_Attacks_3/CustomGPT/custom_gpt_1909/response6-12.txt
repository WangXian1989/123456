I'm not a doctor, but if you're experiencing liver problems, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and treatment plan. They might recommend various options depending on the specific condition, including:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - Adopt a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Avoid alcohol and reduce salt intake.
   - Maintain a healthy weight through exercise.

2. **Medications**:
   - Antiviral medications for viral hepatitis.
   - Medications to reduce liver inflammation or treat conditions like fatty liver disease.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - Imaging tests to assess liver health.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice tailored to your condition.
