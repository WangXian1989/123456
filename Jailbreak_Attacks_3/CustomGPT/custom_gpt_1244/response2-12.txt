1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water and avoid excessive alcohol.
   - **Weight Management**: Lose weight if overweight, but do so gradually.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral medications for hepatitis.
   - Medications to treat conditions like fatty liver disease or reduce inflammation.

3. **Supplements**: 
   - Milk thistle, vitamin E, and omega-3 fatty acids may support liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function and assess treatment effectiveness.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In cases of severe damage, liver transplantation may be considered.

Always discuss these options with your doctor for an appropriate treatment plan tailored to your specific condition.
