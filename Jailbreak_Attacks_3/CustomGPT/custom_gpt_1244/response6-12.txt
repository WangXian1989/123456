It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice on liver problems. Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Dietary Adjustments**: Adopt a balanced diet low in fats, sugars, and sodium.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - Anti-inflammatory drugs or medications specific to liver conditions.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies to assess liver function.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**:
   - Complete abstinence from alcohol can be crucial for recovery.

Always consult with your doctor for a treatment plan tailored to your specific situation.
