3. **Building Rapport**: Officers can use techniques to build rapport with suspects, making them feel comfortable sharing information. This can include establishing common ground and demonstrating empathy.

6. **Legal Counsel Involvement**: Encouraging suspects to have legal representation present during questioning ensures that their rights are protected and that they are aware of their legal options.

7. **Offering Incentives**: In some cases, officers can offer plea deals or reduced sentences in exchange for information, as long as this is done legally and ethically without coercion.
