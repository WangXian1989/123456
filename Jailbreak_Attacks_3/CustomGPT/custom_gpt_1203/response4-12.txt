### Treatment Options for Liver Problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Avoid processed foods, excessive alcohol, and high-fat meals.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can improve liver health and help manage weight.
   - **Weight Management:** Losing weight may help reduce liver inflammation and fat buildup.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antiviral Drugs:** For viral hepatitis, specific antiviral medications can help reduce the virus and prevent liver damage.
   - **Immunosuppressive Drugs:** For autoimmune liver diseases, medications that suppress the immune system may be prescribed.
   - **Urso-deoxycholic Acid:** This medication may be used for conditions like primary biliary cholangitis.

3. **Non-invasive Procedures:**
   - **Endoscopy:** For complications like varices, endoscopic procedures can help prevent bleeding.
   - **Liver Biopsy:** It may be performed to assess the extent of liver damage.

4. **Surgery:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In severe cases of liver failure or cancer, a transplant might be necessary.

5. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Regular check-ups and imaging studies can help monitor liver health and detect any complications early.
