I'm not a doctor, but I can give you some general information about treatment options for liver problems. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet: Focus on a balanced diet low in fat and high in fruits and vegetables.
   - Alcohol: Limit or eliminate alcohol consumption.
   - Exercise: Aim for regular physical activity.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the condition, medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.

4. **Weight Management**: 
   - For fatty liver disease, losing weight can significantly improve liver health.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, such as cirrhosis, a liver transplant might be necessary.
