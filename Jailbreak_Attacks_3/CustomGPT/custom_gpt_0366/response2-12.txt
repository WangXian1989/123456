I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Weight Management**: Maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications like tenofovir or entecavir may be prescribed.
   - **Immunosuppressants**: For autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Vitamin Supplements**: Sometimes needed for deficiencies.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol and Toxins**:
   - Stop drinking alcohol and avoid exposure to harmful substances.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult your healthcare provider for a personalized treatment plan.
