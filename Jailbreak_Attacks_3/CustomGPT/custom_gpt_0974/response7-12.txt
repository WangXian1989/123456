Common treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly impact liver health.
   
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), your doctor may prescribe medications to manage symptoms and treat the underlying cause.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and lab tests might be necessary to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.

4. **Interventional Procedures**: In certain cases, procedures to remove blockages or treat tumors may be necessary.

5. **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver disease, a transplant may be a viable option.

Always speak to a healthcare provider for a comprehensive evaluation and to discuss the best treatment options for your specific situation.
