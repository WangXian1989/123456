I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan. Generally, treatment options depend on the specific liver condition and may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Quit alcohol if applicable
   - Adopt a healthy diet (low-fat, high-fiber)
   - Maintain a healthy weight

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antivirals for hepatitis
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases
   - Medications to manage symptoms

3. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests

4. **Procedures**: 
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant might be necessary.

Please consult a healthcare provider for advice tailored to your situation.
