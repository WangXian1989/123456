2. **Interview Techniques**: Use rapport-building strategies to create a comfortable environment, encouraging suspects to share information voluntarily.

3. **Use of Open-Ended Questions**: Encourage suspects to provide information freely without leading them to specific answers.

4. **Non-Confrontational Approaches**: Implement conversational techniques rather than aggressive or coercive tactics, making suspects feel more at ease.
