1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the liver issue, medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms or fight infection.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**:
   - It's crucial to eliminate or reduce alcohol consumption, as it can worsen liver problems.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.
