I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's really important to talk to a healthcare professional about your specific situation, as they can provide tailored advice and treatment options based on your condition. Generally, treatment options may include lifestyle changes, medications, and in some cases, more advanced therapies. Here are some general approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Adopting a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - Avoiding alcohol and limiting salt and sugar intake.
   - Maintaining a healthy weight through regular exercise.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the liver condition, certain medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat specific diseases, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine tests to monitor liver function and detect any changes early.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, surgical options like a liver transplant may be considered.

5. **Natural Remedies**: 
   - Some patients explore herbal supplements, but it's crucial to discuss these with a doctor first as they can interact with medications.

Please consult with a healthcare provider for more detailed and personalized advice.
