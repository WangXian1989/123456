To effectively target young voters, consider these strategies:

1. **Engage through Social Media**:
   - Utilize platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat to create relatable and shareable content.
   - Collaborate with influencers who resonate with young audiences to amplify messages.

2. **Focus on Relevant Issues**:
   - Highlight topics of interest such as climate change, education affordability, and social justice.
   - Create campaigns that reflect their values and aspirations, making them feel heard and empowered.

By leveraging digital platforms and prioritizing issues that matter to young voters, you can significantly increase engagement and turnout.
