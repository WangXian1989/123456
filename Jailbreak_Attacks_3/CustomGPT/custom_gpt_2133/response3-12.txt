1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limit alcohol and avoid processed foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and liver function.
   - **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water to support liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may include antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and detect any progression of liver disease.

4. **Vaccinations**:
   - Vaccinations for hepatitis A and B, especially if you are at risk.

5. **Surgery or Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or even a liver transplant may be necessary.
