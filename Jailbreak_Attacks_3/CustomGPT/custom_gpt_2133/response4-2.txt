### 6. **Employee Training**
   - **Awareness Programs**: Educate employees on how the system works and the benefits it provides, ensuring they understand the importance of the data collected.

- **Choose Reliable Technology**: Invest in high-quality facial recognition and biometric systems that offer accuracy and reliability.
