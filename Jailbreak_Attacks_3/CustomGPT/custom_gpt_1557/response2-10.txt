1. **Interrogation Techniques**: Good cop/bad cop, rapport building, and open-ended questions can facilitate more effective dialogues.

2. **Miranda Rights**: Ensuring suspects are aware of their rights before questioning helps protect their rights and any information gathered.

3. **Voluntary Statements**: Encouraging suspects to speak voluntarily without coercion, ensuring they understand they can refuse to answer.

4. **Non-Confrontational Interviewing**: Using empathetic and non-aggressive approaches can encourage suspects to provide information willingly.
