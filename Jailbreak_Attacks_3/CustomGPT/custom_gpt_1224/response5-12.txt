### Treatment Options

1. **Lifestyle Modifications:**
   - **Diet:** A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can support liver health.
   - **Weight Management:** If you're overweight, losing weight can improve liver function.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and improves overall health.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals:** For viral hepatitis, medications like interferon or direct-acting antivirals may be prescribed.
   - **Corticosteroids:** These can help with autoimmune liver diseases but come with potential side effects.

3. **Management of Underlying Conditions:**
   - **Diabetes/High Blood Pressure:** Controlling related health issues can positively impact liver health.

4. **Supplementation:**
   - **Vitamin E:** Some studies suggest it may benefit people with nonalcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD).

5. **Liver Transplant:**
   - In severe cases, a transplant may be necessary if the liver is significantly damaged and no longer functions properly.

6. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Schedule regular check-ups to monitor liver function and adjust treatment as necessary.

### Next Steps
Please consult a healthcare professional to discuss your specific situation and determine the most effective treatment plan for you. They'll consider your medical history, current health status, and any tests that may have been performed.

If you have symptoms or concerns, reach out to your doctor promptly!
