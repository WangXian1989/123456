### Considerations:

- **Privacy Concerns**: Employee monitoring must be balanced with privacy. Clear policies should be communicated to staff.

- **Employee Sentiment**: Monitor how employees feel about biometric monitoring. Surveys can help gauge their attitudes and suggest adjustments if needed.

Overall, while biometric data can enhance attendance and performance-monitoring processes, careful implementation and consideration of ethical aspects are crucial.
