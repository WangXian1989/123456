I'm sorry to hear that you're facing liver issues. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for the most accurate diagnosis and treatment options tailored to your specific condition. Treatments can vary widely depending on the underlying issue, such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, cirrhosis, or liver tumors. 

Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes a balanced diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, there may be antiviral drugs for hepatitis or medications to reduce liver inflammation.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary.

Always follow the guidance of your medical provider.
