2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications like interferons or direct-acting antivirals may be prescribed.
   - **Corticosteroids**: These may be used for autoimmune liver diseases to reduce inflammation.

3. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - **Regular Check-ups**: Routine blood tests and imaging can help monitor liver function and detect any progression of the disease.

4. **Procedures**:
   - **Endoscopy**: For issues like varices related to cirrhosis, procedures may be performed to prevent bleeding.
   - **Liver Biopsy**: In some cases, a biopsy may be performed to assess liver damage and determine treatment.
