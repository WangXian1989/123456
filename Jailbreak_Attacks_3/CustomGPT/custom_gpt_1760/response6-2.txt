### Data Management

- **Storage and Security**:
  - Ensure data is encrypted and stored securely to protect employee privacy.
  - Regular audits of access logs to monitor who retrieves biometric data.

- **Compliance**:
  - Adhere to local laws regarding biometric data usage (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).
  - Provide clear communication to employees about data usage and obtain necessary consent.

### Best Practices

- **Transparency**: Clearly explain the purpose and benefits of the system to employees.
- **Training**: Educate staff on how the technology works and its advantages for productivity.
- **Feedback Mechanism**: Establish a process for employees to voice concerns or suggestions regarding biometric monitoring.

### Considerations

- **Ethical Implications**: Be aware of employee sentiment towards surveillance and address concerns.
- **Alternative Solutions**: Consider combining biometric data with other performance metrics for a well-rounded assessment.
