   - Regular phlebotomy (blood removal)
   - Chelation therapy to remove excess iron

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan. They may recommend lifestyle changes, medications, or procedures based on the severity and type of liver condition you have.
