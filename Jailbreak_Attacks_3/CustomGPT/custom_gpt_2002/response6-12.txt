1. **Lifestyle Modifications**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while avoiding high-fat and processed foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.
   - **Alcohol Reduction**: Limiting or eliminating alcohol consumption is crucial for liver health.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to treat underlying causes such as viral infections (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).
