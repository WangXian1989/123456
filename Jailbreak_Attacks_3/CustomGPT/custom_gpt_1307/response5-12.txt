### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** A healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance:** Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake.
   - **Weight Management:** Losing weight if overweight can benefit liver health.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals:** For viral hepatitis, medications like interferon and direct-acting antivirals may be prescribed.
   - **Anti-inflammatory Drugs:** To reduce inflammation in the liver.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases or tumors, procedures like liver transplants or tumor removal may be necessary.

5. **Supplements:**
   - Some may find supplements like milk thistle beneficial, but consult your doctor before taking any.

Always follow your healthcare provider's recommendations for the best course of action tailored to your specific condition.
