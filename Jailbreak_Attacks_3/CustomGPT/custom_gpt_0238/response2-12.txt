It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugar; increasing fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids**: To reduce inflammation in autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Procedures**:
   - **Endoscopy**: For complications like varices.
   - **Liver Biopsy**: To assess the extent of liver damage.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, liver transplantation may be necessary.

6. **Avoid Alcohol**: Complete abstinence is crucial for liver health.

Discuss these options with your doctor to determine the best course of action for your specific condition.
