I'm not a doctor, but liver issues can be serious, and it's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Some common treatment options for liver problems might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antiviral drugs:** For hepatitis infections.
   - **Corticosteroids:** To reduce inflammation in conditions like autoimmune hepatitis.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol:**
   - Abstaining from alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

4. **Regular Check-Ups:**
   - Monitoring liver function through blood tests and ultrasounds.

5. **Surgery or Procedures:**
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always discuss treatment options with your healthcare provider to determine the best course of action for your specific situation.
