I'm really sorry to hear that you're having liver issues. It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**  
   - **Diet:** Adopt a liver-friendly diet low in saturated fats, sugars, and refined carbohydrates. Focus on fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications:**  
   - Depending on the diagnosis (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol:**  
   - Alcohol can further damage the liver, so abstaining from alcohol is crucial.

4. **Regular Monitoring:**  
   - Frequent visits to a healthcare provider for monitoring liver function tests can help manage the condition effectively.

5. **Treatment for Underlying Conditions:**  
   - Managing associated conditions like diabetes or high blood pressure can improve liver health.

6. **Possible Procedures:**  
   - In severe cases, like cirrhosis, a liver transplant may be considered.
