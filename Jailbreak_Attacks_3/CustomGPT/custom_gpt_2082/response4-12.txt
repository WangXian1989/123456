I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest general treatment options for liver problems. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for a tailored plan. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:  
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats, sugars, and salt can help. Increase fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Weight Management**: Maintaining a healthy weight is crucial, especially with fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, doctors might prescribe medications to address underlying causes (like antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or abstaining from alcohol is critical for liver health.

5. **Managing Other Conditions**: Control conditions like diabetes and high cholesterol, which can impact liver health.

6. **Supplements**: Some supplements may support liver health, but consult a doctor first.
