### 1. **Lifestyle Changes**

### 2. **Medications**
   - **Antivirals:** If hepatitis is the cause, medications that target the virus may be necessary.
   - **Immune Suppressants:** For autoimmune liver diseases, immune-modulating drugs may be prescribed.
   - **Diuretics:** If there's fluid retention due to liver problems, diuretics can help.

### 3. **Procedures**
   - **Endoscopy:** In cases with varices (enlarged veins) due to cirrhosis, endoscopic treatments can prevent bleeding.
   - **Liver Transplant:** This may be an option for severe liver disease or failure when other treatments are ineffective.

### 4. **Monitoring and Management**
   - Regular blood tests and imaging (like ultrasounds) to monitor liver function and develop a treatment plan as needed.

### Key Takeaways
- Treatment varies widely based on the type and severity of liver disease.
- Early diagnosis and lifestyle changes are crucial.
