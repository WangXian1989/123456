To provide the best treatment options for liver problems, a healthcare professional's evaluation is essential. Common treatments may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthier diet, exercising, and avoiding alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications like antivirals for hepatitis or immunosuppressants may be prescribed.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a medical professional for personalized advice and treatment options.
