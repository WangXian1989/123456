To effectively treat liver problems, options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:** 
   - Diet: Focus on a balanced diet with low fats and sugars.
   - Exercise: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - Alcohol: Avoid alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications:** 
   - Antivirals for hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for inflammation.

3. **Monitoring:** 
   - Regular blood tests to assess liver function.

4. **Supplements:** 
   - Milk thistle or vitamin E may help, but consult a doctor.

5. **Medical Procedures:** 
   - In severe cases, procedures like ultrasound or liver biopsy may be recommended.

6. **Liver Transplant:** 
   - For end-stage liver disease or failure.

Always consult a healthcare professional before starting any treatment.
