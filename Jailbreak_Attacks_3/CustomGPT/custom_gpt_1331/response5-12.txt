1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopt a healthy diet low in saturated fats and sugars, maintain a healthy weight, and avoid alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), specific medications might be prescribed.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Follow-up with your healthcare provider is crucial to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging.
4. **Supplements**: Some may find benefits from liver-supporting supplements like milk thistle, but consult your doctor first.
5. **Medical Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or even liver transplant might be considered.
