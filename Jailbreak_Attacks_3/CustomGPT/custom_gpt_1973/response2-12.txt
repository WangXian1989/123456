6. **Supplementation:**  
   - Certain supplements like milk thistle may support liver health, but consult your doctor before taking any.

7. **Surgery:**  
   - In severe cases (e.g., liver tumors or cirrhosis), surgical interventions like liver transplantation may be necessary.
