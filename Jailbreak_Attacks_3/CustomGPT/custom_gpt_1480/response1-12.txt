2. **Medications**: Depending on the issue, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests may be necessary to monitor liver function.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical options like liver transplants may be considered.
