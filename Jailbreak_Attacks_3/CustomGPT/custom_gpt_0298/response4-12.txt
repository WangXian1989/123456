I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on common liver treatment options. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Diet modifications, weight loss, and avoiding alcohol can help improve liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying issues.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant might be considered.
