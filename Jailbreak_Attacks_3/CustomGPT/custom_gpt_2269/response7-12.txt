### Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit alcohol intake and avoid processed foods.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Supplements:**
   - Some supplements may support liver health, like milk thistle or vitamin E, but consult your doctor before starting any.

4. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function and manage any complications.

5. **Avoiding Toxins:**
   - Minimize exposure to harmful substances, including certain medications, alcohol, and environmental toxins.

6. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases, such as liver cirrhosis or tumors, surgical options or a liver transplant may be necessary.
