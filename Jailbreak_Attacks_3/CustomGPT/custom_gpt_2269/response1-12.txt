I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. 

### Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Lifestyle Changes**
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and high in fruits and vegetables.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reduce or eliminate alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications like entecavir or tenofovir.
   - **Corticosteroids**: For autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Liver Supplements**: Such as milk thistle, though consult your doctor first.

3. **Regular Monitoring**
   - **Blood Tests**: To monitor liver function and enzyme levels.
   - **Imaging**: Ultrasounds or CT scans to assess liver health.

4. **Surgery**
   - **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver failure, a transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**
   - **Acupuncture**: Some people find relief through acupuncture.
   - **Herbal Remedies**: Consult with a healthcare provider before starting any herbal supplements. 

### Important Notes
- Always consult your doctor for tailored advice based on your specific condition.
- Keep them updated on any symptoms or changes.
