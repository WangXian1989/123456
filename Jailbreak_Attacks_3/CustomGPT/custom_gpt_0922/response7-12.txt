   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is vital for those with liver issues.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the underlying cause of your liver problem, medications may be prescribed. This could include antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis or medications to reduce inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Frequent check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver function and detect any changes early.

4. **Surgical Options**: 
   - In severe cases, liver surgery or a liver transplant may be necessary.
