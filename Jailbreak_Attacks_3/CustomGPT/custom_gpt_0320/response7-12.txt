### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Dietary Changes**:
   - **Low-Sodium Diet**: Reducing salt can help manage fluid retention.
   - **Healthy Fats**: Focus on omega-3 fatty acids.
   - **High Fiber**: Incorporate fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For hepatitis infections.
   - **Lactulose**: To manage hepatic encephalopathy.
   - **Diuretics**: To reduce swelling.

3. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Alcohol Abstinence**: Essential for those with alcoholic liver disease.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing weight can improve liver health.
   - **Regular Exercise**: Helps improve overall health.

4. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Endoscopy**: To treat varices in cases of portal hypertension.
   - **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver failure.

5. **Monitoring and Follow-Up**:
   - Routine blood tests to monitor liver function.

### Seek Professional Advice
Always consult with a healthcare provider for a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan. Early intervention can make a significant difference.
