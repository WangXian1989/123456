I'm sorry to hear about your liver problem. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Generally, treatment options may include lifestyle changes, medications, and in some cases, surgery, depending on the specific liver condition. Here are a few common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes maintaining a healthy diet, regular exercise, avoiding alcohol, and managing weight.
2. **Medications**: Certain medications may be prescribed to address specific liver conditions, such as hepatitis or fatty liver disease.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help manage the condition.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplants may be necessary.

Always seek professional medical advice to determine the best treatment plan for your particular situation.
