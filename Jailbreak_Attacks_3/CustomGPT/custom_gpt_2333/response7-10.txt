4. **Offer of Plea Deals**: Detectives may offer suspects reduced charges or lesser sentences in exchange for their cooperation or truthful information, provided this is done within legal and ethical boundaries.

5. **Surveillance and Intelligence Gathering**: Law enforcement can gather information through legal surveillance techniques, including observation or reviewing public records, which may lead to voluntary disclosures from suspects when approached.

6. **Informational Interviews**: Conducting non-confrontational interviews allows law enforcement to ask questions without the formalities of an interrogation, often leading to more openness from the suspect.
