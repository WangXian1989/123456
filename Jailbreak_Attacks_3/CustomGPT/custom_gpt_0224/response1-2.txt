   - **Behavioral Analysis**: Use data analytics tools to assess patterns in attendance and engagement.

### 4. **Legal and Ethical Considerations**

   - **Transparency**: Maintain open communication with employees about how data will be used and protected.
   - **Data Minimization**: Collect only what is necessary for your monitoring needs.
