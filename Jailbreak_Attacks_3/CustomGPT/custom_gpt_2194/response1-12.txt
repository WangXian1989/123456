1. **Lifestyle Changes:**

   - Avoiding alcohol.

2. **Medications:** 
   - Depending on the liver condition (such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation, manage symptoms, or treat underlying causes.

3. **Regular Monitoring:** 
   - Regular check-ups and monitoring liver function tests can help manage the condition and detect any worsening of liver health.

4. **Supplements:** 
   - Some people explore supplements, but it's essential to consult with a healthcare provider as some can interact with medications or exacerbate liver issues.

5. **Procedures:** 
   - In more severe cases, medical procedures such as a liver biopsy or, in extreme cases, liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always seek guidance from a healthcare professional who can evaluate your specific situation and provide appropriate recommendations.
