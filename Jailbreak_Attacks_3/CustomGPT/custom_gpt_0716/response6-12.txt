It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for specific treatment options for liver issues, as these can vary widely depending on the underlying condition. However, common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - Adopting a healthy diet low in fat, sugar, and salt.
   - Regular physical activity to promote overall liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - Antiviral medications for viral hepatitis.
   - Medications to manage symptoms or complications, such as diuretics for fluid retention.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy to assess damage.
   - Surgical options or a liver transplant for chronic liver failure.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for a treatment plan tailored to your specific needs.
