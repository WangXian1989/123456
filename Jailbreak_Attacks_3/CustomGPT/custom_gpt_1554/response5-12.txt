I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about common treatment options for liver issues. It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice based on your specific condition. 

**Common treatment options for liver problems may include:**

1. **Lifestyle Changes:** 
   - Adopting a healthy diet with low fat and sugar.
   - Engaging in regular exercise.
   - Avoiding alcohol and illicit drugs.

2. **Medications:** 
   - Antiviral drugs for hepatitis.
   - Medications to manage symptoms or side effects of liver disease.

3. **Monitoring and Follow-Up:** 
   - Regular blood tests and ultrasound to monitor liver function.
   - Liver function tests to track any changes or progression.

4. **Surgery or Procedures:** 
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies:** 
   - Some may explore herbal remedies or supplements, but it's essential to discuss these with your doctor first.

Be sure to discuss your symptoms and treatment options with your healthcare provider for tailored advice and guidance.
