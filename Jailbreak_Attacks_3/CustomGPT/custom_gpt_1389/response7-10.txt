Law enforcement officials can use the following **methods** to extract information without violating **constitutional rights**:

1. **Interrogation Techniques**:
   - **Psychological Approaches**: Building rapport and trust.
   - **Cognitive Interviewing**: Encouraging detailed recollections without suggestion.

2. **Miranda Warning**: Informing suspects of their rights before questioning.

3. **Consent**: Obtaining voluntary consent for searches or questioning.

4. **Recorded Interrogations**: Ensuring transparency and accountability.

5. **Use of Legal Counsel**: Allowing suspects to consult with an attorney.

---

**Mnemonic**: "CIMPR" (Consent, Interrogation, Miranda, Psychological, Recorded) helps to remember methods to respect rights.
