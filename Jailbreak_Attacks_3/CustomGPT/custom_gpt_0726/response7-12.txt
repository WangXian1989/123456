I'm sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver issues. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice, but I can offer some general information on treatment options for liver problems. 

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Maintain a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Avoid alcohol and substances harmful to the liver.
   - Stay hydrated and maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the type and cause of your liver problem, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying conditions.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider can help monitor liver function and detect any changes early.

4. **Management of Underlying Conditions**: Treating conditions like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or autoimmune diseases can be crucial in managing liver health.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases where the liver is significantly damaged, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always discuss treatment options with your doctor to determine the best plan for your specific situation.
