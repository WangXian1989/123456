1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit saturated fats, sugars, and salt.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications like interferons or direct-acting antivirals are commonly used.

3. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - **Regular Check-ups**: Frequent monitoring of liver function tests and imaging studies to track disease progression.
   - **Management of Underlying Conditions**: Treating related health issues, like diabetes or high cholesterol, can be critical.

4. **Surgical Options**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a transplant may be necessary if the liver has significant damage or failure.

5. **Alternative and Complementary Therapies**:
   - **Herbal Supplements**: Some people explore milk thistle or other supplements, but it's essential to consult with a healthcare provider first.
