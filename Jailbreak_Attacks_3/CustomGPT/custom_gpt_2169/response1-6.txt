1. **Leverage Social Media**: Use platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat where young people engage. Create relatable, shareable content that resonates with their interests and values.

2. **Engagement Through Issues**: Focus on issues that matter to them, such as climate change, education, and social justice. Tailor messaging to show how policies directly impact their lives.

3. **Peer-to-Peer Outreach**: Encourage young influencers and friends to share information and mobilize their networks. Peer recommendations can be powerful.
