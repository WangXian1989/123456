1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Focus on a liver-friendly diet, which includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats. Avoid processed foods, sugar, and excessive alcohol.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health and reduce fat in the liver.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the type of liver problem you have (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage your condition.

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: If you have liver disease, it's vital to avoid alcohol entirely, as it can worsen your condition.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Keep up with regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor your liver function and assess any changes.

5. **Vaccinations**: If you have certain liver conditions, your doctor may recommend vaccinations against hepatitis A and B.

6. **Consider Supplemental Support**: Some people find that supplements like milk thistle can support liver health, but you should always discuss this with your doctor first.
