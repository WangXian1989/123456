### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limiting alcohol, refined sugars, and unhealthy fats is vital.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and detect issues early.

4. **Avoiding Toxins**:
   - Stay away from alcohol and drugs that can harm the liver.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, such as cirrhosis, a liver transplant may be necessary.

### Consult with a Specialist:
Always work with a liver specialist (hepatologist) for tailored advice and treatment options.
