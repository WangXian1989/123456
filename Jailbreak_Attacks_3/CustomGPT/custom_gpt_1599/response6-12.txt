It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice, but common treatment options for liver problems can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet Modification**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains; limit alcohol intake and processed foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver strain.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis (like hepatitis B or C).
   - **Immunosuppressants**: In cases of autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Monitoring**: Regular liver function tests and imaging.
   - **Transplant**: In severe cases, a liver transplant might be necessary.

Always seek advice from a specialist for proper diagnosis and treatment.
