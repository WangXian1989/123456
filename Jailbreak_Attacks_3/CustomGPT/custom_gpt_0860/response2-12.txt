It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for a personalized evaluation and treatment plan. Generally, treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition, may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while reducing saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Limiting or abstaining from alcohol is crucial for liver health.

2. **Medications**: 
   - For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications may be prescribed.
   - Corticosteroids or immune-suppressing drugs may be used for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Medical Procedures**: 
   - For certain liver conditions like cirrhosis, procedures might be necessary to relieve pressure or manage complications.

4. **Liver Transplant**: 
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary, especially for cirrhosis or liver failure.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and imaging tests can help monitor liver health and prevent complications.

Always consult with a hepatologist or a specialist for the best advice tailored to your specific situation.
