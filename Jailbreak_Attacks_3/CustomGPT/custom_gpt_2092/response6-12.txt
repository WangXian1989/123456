To provide the best guidance, it's important to know the specific liver condition you're dealing with. Common liver problems include fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. However, here are some general treatment options based on common liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Avoid high-fat, sugary, and processed foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help manage weight and reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral drugs** for hepatitis (e.g., interferon, direct-acting antivirals).
   - **Corticosteroids** for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - Supplements (e.g., vitamin E for nonalcoholic fatty liver disease) based on physician advice.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine liver function tests and imaging studies to track the progression of the disease.

4. **Specialized Treatments**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: Considered for severe liver damage or cirrhosis.
   - **Endoscopy**: For varices related to cirrhosis, to prevent bleeding.

5. **Avoid Toxins**:
   - Limit alcohol intake and avoid substances harmful to the liver.
