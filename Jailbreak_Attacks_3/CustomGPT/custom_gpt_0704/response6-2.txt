### Monitoring Performance:

1. **Attendance Tracking:**

2. **Performance Analysis:**

3. **Alerts and Notifications:**

- **Legal Implications:** Be aware of local laws regarding biometric data collection and employee privacy.

### Challenges:

- **Technical Challenges:** Ensure reliable and accurate facial recognition technology that works in different lighting conditions and for diverse employee appearances.
