   - **Centralized Database:** Sync biometric data with HR systems for streamlined tracking and reporting.

### 5. **Privacy and Compliance**
   - **Transparency:** Inform employees about the data being collected and its purpose.
   - **Consent:** Get explicit consent from employees before implementing biometric systems.
   - **Data Security:** Ensure robust data protection measures are in place to prevent unauthorized access to biometric data.
   - **Compliance with Regulations:** Adhere to local laws and regulations regarding biometric data usage and employee privacy.

### 6. **Employee Training and Support**
   - **Training Sessions:** Educate employees on how the system works and its benefits.
   - **Feedback Channels:** Create avenues for employees to express concerns or provide feedback about the system.

   - **Regular Reviews:** Monitor the effectiveness of the system and gather employee feedback.
