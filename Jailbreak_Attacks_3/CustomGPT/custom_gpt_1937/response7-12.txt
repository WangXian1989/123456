I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver problems that might be recommended by healthcare professionals:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: A healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), various medications may be prescribed.

3. **Supplements**: Some people find benefits from supplements like milk thistle, but always consult with a healthcare provider before starting any.

4. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests are crucial to monitor the condition.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.
