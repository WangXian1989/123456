Treatment options for liver problems can vary widely depending on the underlying cause (such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, or liver cancer) and can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eating a liver-friendly diet low in saturated fats and high in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Abstaining from alcohol can help prevent further liver damage.

2. **Medications**: 
   - For conditions such as hepatitis or autoimmune liver diseases, specific antiviral or immunosuppressive medications may be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function and manage any complications.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, liver transplantation may be necessary, especially in cases of liver failure or advanced cirrhosis.
