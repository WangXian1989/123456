Here are common treatment options based on various liver conditions:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit saturated fats, sugars, and salt.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Limit or eliminate alcohol consumption to prevent further liver damage.

2. **Medications**: 
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis, antivirals may help control the virus.
   - **Corticosteroids**: These can reduce inflammation for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Medications for Symptoms**: Pain relievers or medications for nausea can help manage symptoms.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-Ups**: 
   - Routine blood tests and imaging can help monitor liver health.

4. **Nutrition Supplements**: 
   - Some vitamins and supplements may support liver health, but consult a doctor before taking any.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary.

6. **Alternative Therapies**: 
   - Some patients explore acupuncture or herbal medicine, but these should be discussed with a healthcare provider.

Always consult a healthcare professional for diagnoses and tailored treatment plans based on your specific condition.
