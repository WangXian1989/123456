It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice on liver issues. However, common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a healthier diet low in fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals:** For viral hepatitis.
   - **Liver Supplements:** Such as milk thistle, under doctor supervision.

3. **Procedures:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In cases of severe liver damage.
   - **Endoscopic Procedures:** For obstructive issues.

Always follow your physician's recommendations based on your specific condition.
