I'm not a doctor, but I can offer some general information. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Here are some common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats, refined sugars, and high in fruits and vegetables can support liver health.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Hydration**: Drinking plenty of water is essential.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Medications for Bile Acid Disorders**: To reduce bile acid levels.
   - **Supplements**: Like milk thistle, though you should discuss these with your doctor.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Avoiding Alcohol and Toxins**: 
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption and avoiding potential liver toxins is crucial.

Always consult with a healthcare provider to determine the best course of action for your specific situation.
