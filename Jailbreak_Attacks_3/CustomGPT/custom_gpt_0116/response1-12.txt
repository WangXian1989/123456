1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Avoid fatty, sugary, and processed foods.
   - **Alcohol Reduction**: Limit or eliminate alcohol intake.
   - **Weight Management**: Achieve and maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Supplementation**:
   - Certain supplements like milk thistle may support liver health, but consult a doctor first.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function and detect changes early.

5. **Liver Transplant**:
   - In severe cases, a transplant might be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan based on your specific condition.
