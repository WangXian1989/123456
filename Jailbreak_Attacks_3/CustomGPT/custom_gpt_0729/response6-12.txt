2. **Lifestyle Changes**:

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, liver transplantation may be necessary if the liver is significantly damaged.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some individuals explore herbal supplements and other natural remedies, but it's vital to discuss these options with a healthcare provider, as they can interact with medications or cause harm.
