Law enforcement officials can employ several lawful methods to extract information from suspects while respecting their constitutional rights:

1. **Building Rapport:**
   - Establishing a connection to foster trust.
   - Using active listening techniques to encourage openness.

2. **Interview Techniques:**
   - Utilizing open-ended questions to gather information.
   - Implementing the PEACE model (Preparation and planning, Engagement and explanation, Account, Closure, and Evaluation).

3. **Incentives:**
   - Offering plea deals or leniency in exchange for cooperation.
   - Providing information on potential benefits, such as reduced charges or alternate sentencing.

4. **Miranda Rights and Waivers:**
   - Ensuring suspects are informed of their rights; legally obtaining waivers for interrogation.

5. **Non-Coercive Tactics:**
   - Avoiding physical or psychological coercion.
   - Using calm and respectful communication to encourage voluntary disclosure.
