It's important to consult a healthcare professional for specific liver problems, but common treatment options may include:

1. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals** for hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids** for autoimmune liver diseases.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Dietary Adjustments**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**:
   - Complete abstinence if alcohol-related damage is present.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies.

5. **Liver Transplant**:
   - Considered in severe cases of liver failure.

Always consult a liver specialist for personalized advice.
