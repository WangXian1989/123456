I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may include antiviral drugs (for hepatitis), corticosteroids (for autoimmune liver diseases), or medications to manage symptoms.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding alcohol can significantly help improve liver health.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and detect any changes early.

4. **Surgery or Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver biopsy or even liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Supportive Care**: Managing symptoms with medications, such as diuretics for fluid retention, and addressing any complications that arise.
