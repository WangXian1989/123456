   - **Supportive Therapies:** Depending on the condition, medications for symptom relief may be prescribed.

3. **Surgery:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In severe cases, a liver transplant may be the only option.

4. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - **Follow-ups:** Regular check-ups and tests to monitor liver function and overall health status are important.
