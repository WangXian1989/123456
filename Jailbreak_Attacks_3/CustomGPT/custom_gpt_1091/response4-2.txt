   - **Informed Consent:** Ensure that all employees are informed about the use of facial recognition and biometric data, obtaining their consent.
   - **Compliance with Regulations:** Follow local laws and regulations regarding biometric data usage to protect employee privacy.

### 6. **Feedback Mechanism**
   - **Regular Reviews:** Implement a process for regularly reviewing and discussing the data collected with employees. Develop performance improvement plans based on data insights.
