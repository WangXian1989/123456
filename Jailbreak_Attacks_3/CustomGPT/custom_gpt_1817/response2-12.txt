2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or fight infections.

4. **Avoiding Toxins**: Reducing exposure to harmful substances, including certain medications and chemicals, can help protect liver function.

5. **Monitoring and Management**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and manage any underlying conditions such as diabetes or high cholesterol.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or, in extreme cases, liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always talk to a healthcare provider for a thorough diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
