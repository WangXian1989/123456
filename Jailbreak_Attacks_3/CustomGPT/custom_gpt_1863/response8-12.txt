### 1. **Lifestyle Changes**

   - **Corticosteroids:** Can be used for autoimmune liver diseases.

### 3. **Supplementation**
   - **Vitamin E:** Sometimes recommended for non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD).

### 4. **Medical Procedures**
   - **Liver Biopsy:** To evaluate the extent of liver damage.
   - **Endoscopy:** For variceal bleeding in advanced liver disease.

### 5. **Surgery**
   - **Liver Transplant:** An option for severe liver failure or cirrhosis.

### 6. **Regular Monitoring**
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and adjust treatments as needed.

### 7. **Support Groups**
   - Connecting with groups for emotional support and sharing experiences.
