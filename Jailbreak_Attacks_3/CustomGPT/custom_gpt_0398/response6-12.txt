### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Incorporate a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid processed foods and high sugar intake.
   - **Alcohol Reduction:** Limit or eliminate alcohol consumption to reduce strain on the liver.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antiviral Drugs:** For viral hepatitis, medications like interferons or direct-acting antivirals may be prescribed.
   - **Liver Support Supplements:** Some may use supplements such as milk thistle or N-acetylcysteine, but consult a doctor before taking anything.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - **Routine Blood Tests:** Regular check-ups to monitor liver function and assess any potential progression of liver disease.

4. **Surgery:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In severe cases, where liver function is critically impaired, a transplant may be necessary.

Always discuss options with your healthcare provider to determine the best plan for your specific condition.
