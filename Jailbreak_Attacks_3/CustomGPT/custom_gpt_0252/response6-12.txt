For liver problems, it's essential to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis. Treatment options vary based on the specific condition, but generally include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - Adopt a healthy diet, low in fats and sugars.
   - Maintain a healthy weight through regular exercise.

2. **Medications**:
   - Antivirals for viral hepatitis.
   - Medications to manage symptoms or prevent further damage.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - Imaging tests as recommended by your doctor.

4. **Alcohol Cessation**:
   - Avoid alcohol completely if alcohol-related liver disease is present.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, liver transplant may be necessary.

Always follow your healthcare provider's recommendations tailored to your liver condition.
