It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. Lifestyle changes (diet, exercise)
2. Medications to manage symptoms or underlying conditions
3. Avoiding alcohol and toxins
4. Regular monitoring and check-ups
5. In severe cases, liver transplant may be considered.
