Law enforcement can use several methods to extract information from suspects while respecting constitutional rights, including:

1. **Interrogation Techniques**:
   - **Building Rapport**: Establishing a connection to encourage dialogue.
   - **Cognitive Interviewing**: Using open-ended questions to enhance memory recall without coercion.

2. **Legal Procedures**:
   - **Miranda Warnings**: Informing suspects of their rights to remain silent and to have an attorney present.
   - **Voluntary Statements**: Encouraging suspects to share information without coercive tactics.

3. **Negotiation Strategies**:
   - **Incentives**: Offering plea deals or reduced sentences for cooperation.
   - **Truthfulness Promises**: Assuring that honest information will be considered favorably.
