1. **Legal Compliance:**
   - Research and adhere to local and international data protection laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) regarding employee biometric data.

2. **Transparency:**
   - Inform employees about how their data will be used, stored, and protected.

### Advantages:

- **Improved Accuracy:** Facial recognition reduces buddy punching and enhances attendance accuracy.

### Potential Challenges:

- **Privacy Concerns:** Employees may feel uncomfortable with constant monitoring, leading to decreased morale.
- **Technical Issues:** Biometric systems may face software glitches or hardware malfunctions that can disrupt operations.

By carefully planning and implementing a biometric data system, organizations can enhance monitoring while also considering legal and ethical responsibilities.
