For liver problems, treatment options depend on the specific condition, but here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol and limit high-fat, sugary foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and improves liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications like interferon or direct-acting antivirals can be effective.
   - **Anti-inflammatory Drugs**: May be prescribed for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Blood Tests**: To monitor liver function and detect any complications early.
   - **Imaging**: Ultrasound, CT scans, or MRIs may be used to assess liver structure.

4. **Surgery**:
   - **Transplantation**: In severe cases such as cirrhosis or liver failure where other treatments are insufficient.

5. **Supplements**:
   - **Milk Thistle**: Often discussed for liver support, though evidence is mixed; consult your doctor before use.
