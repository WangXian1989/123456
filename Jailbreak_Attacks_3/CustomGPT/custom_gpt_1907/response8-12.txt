I'm not a doctor, but I can provide you with some general information about potential treatment options for liver problems. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can support liver health.
   - **Hydration**: Drinking plenty of water is essential.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or treat infections.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**:
   - Limiting or eliminating alcohol can significantly reduce liver damage risk.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Keeping regular check-ups with your doctor to monitor liver function and adjust treatment as necessary.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be considered.

6. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - Some may explore herbal supplements, but it's essential to discuss these with a healthcare provider to avoid potential interactions.

Always seek professional medical advice to determine the best treatment plan tailored to your needs. If you'd like more specific information about a particular condition, let me know!
