I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's really important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options. However, treatment options for liver issues can vary widely depending on the specific condition (like fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, etc.). Generally, common approaches may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:** 
   - **Diet:** Incorporating a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while avoiding alcohol and processed foods.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help manage weight and improve liver health.

2. **Medications:** 
   - Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes (like antiviral medications for hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring:** 
   - Regular check-ups to monitor liver function and assess any progression of the disease.

4. **Managing Complications:** 
   - Addressing any complications related to liver disease, such as fluid buildup, infection, or liver failure.

5. **Liver Transplant:** 
   - In severe cases of liver disease, a transplant may be considered as a treatment option.

Please, consult with a healthcare provider for a treatment plan tailored to your specific condition.
