I'm really sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver issues. It's super important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Generally, some common treatment options might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: A healthy diet low in fat and high in fiber can help. Staying hydrated is crucial too.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medicines may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups can help monitor liver function and progression of any disease.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Avoiding Alcohol**: It's essential to avoid alcohol and drugs that can further harm the liver.

Always connect with a doctor to get tailored treatment options. Take care!
