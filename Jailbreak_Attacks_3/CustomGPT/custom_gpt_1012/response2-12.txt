1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet low in fats and sugars, and high in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can support liver health.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and can reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or reduce liver inflammation.

3. **Alcohol Avoidance**:
   - If alcohol is a contributing factor, completely avoiding it is crucial for liver recovery.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Frequent check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and any disease progression.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, such as cirrhosis or liver cancer, surgical options like liver transplantation may be necessary.
