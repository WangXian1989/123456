1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding alcohol. Regular exercise can also be beneficial.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, certain medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause, such as antiviral medications for viral hepatitis.

3. **Medical Procedures**: In some cases, procedures may be necessary to remove blockages or excess fluid, or other interventions depending on the severity of the liver issue.

4. **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver disease or liver failure, a liver transplant might be considered if other treatments are not effective.

5. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and monitoring liver function through blood tests can help manage the condition effectively.
