I'm sorry to hear about your liver problem. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice. Generally, treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthier diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications like antivirals for hepatitis or corticosteroids for inflammation might be prescribed.
3. **Supplements**: Certain supplements like milk thistle may be recommended, but always check with a doctor first.
4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or even liver transplant may be necessary.

For tailored advice and treatment, please reach out to a healthcare provider.
