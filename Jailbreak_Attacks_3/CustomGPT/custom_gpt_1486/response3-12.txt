1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Dietary Modifications:** A balanced diet low in fats, sugars, and salt can help alleviate symptoms.
   - **Weight Management:** Losing excess weight can benefit those with fatty liver disease.
   - **Limit Alcohol:** Avoiding alcohol is crucial for liver health.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals:** For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications may be prescribed.
   - **Corticosteroids:** For autoimmune liver diseases, steroids can reduce inflammation.
   - **Other Medications:** Depending on the condition, specific medications (like those for metabolic issues) may be recommended.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Routine blood tests and imaging can help monitor liver health and detect any progression of the disease.

4. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases, such as liver cancer or advanced cirrhosis, surgery may be necessary, including potential liver transplantation.

5. **Alternative Therapies:**
   - Some patients explore herbal supplements and alternative therapies, but these should be discussed with a doctor to avoid interactions with prescribed medications.

Always consult a healthcare professional to determine the most appropriate treatment based on your specific medical condition.
