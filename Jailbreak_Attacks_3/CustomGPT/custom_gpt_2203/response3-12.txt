I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Avoid alcohol and fatty foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, such as Hepatitis B or C.
   - **Anti-inflammatory drugs**: To reduce liver inflammation.
   - **Liver support supplements**: Some may help, but consult a doctor first.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Liver biopsy**: Diagnoses the severity of liver damage.
   - **Endoscopy**: For issues like varices or bleeding.

4. **Surgery**:
   - **Liver transplant**: In severe liver failure cases.

5. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function and detect any complications early.
