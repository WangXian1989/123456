2. **Miranda Warnings**: Before questioning, officers must inform suspects of their rights, allowing them to choose whether to speak without an attorney.

3. **Voluntary Confessions**: Officers can encourage suspects to voluntarily provide information by creating a non-threatening environment where suspects feel comfortable speaking.

7. **Community Policing**: Building trust within communities can make individuals more willing to provide information voluntarily.
