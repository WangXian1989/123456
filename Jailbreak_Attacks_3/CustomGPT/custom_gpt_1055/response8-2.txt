- **Data Protection**: Use encryption and other security measures to protect biometric data against unauthorized access and breaches.

### 5. **Training and Transparency**

- **Employee Training**: Train employees on the usage of biometric systems, addressing concerns about privacy and data security.
- **Transparent Policies**: Create clear policies that outline how biometric data will be used, how it benefits employees, and the safeguards in place to protect their information.
