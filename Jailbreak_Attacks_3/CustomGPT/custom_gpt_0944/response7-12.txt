### 1. **Lifestyle Changes**
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet low in fats and sugar, and rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health and help with weight management.
   - **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver recovery.

### 2. **Medications**
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, specific antiviral medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Corticosteroids**: These may be prescribed for autoimmune liver diseases.

### 3. **Monitoring and Management**
   - Regular check-ups to monitor liver function and disease progression.
   - Blood tests to check liver enzymes and function.

### 4. **Procedures**
   - **Endoscopy**: To treat complications, such as varices in portal hypertension.
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases of liver failure, a transplant may be necessary.
