3. **Peer-to-Peer Outreach**: Leverage peer networks to reach young voters. Encourage them to engage their friends and family through grassroots campaigns, events, and online challenges.

6. **Event Participation**: Host or participate in events that attract young people, such as music festivals, campus activities, or community service projects. This gives you a chance to connect personally and discuss your platform.
