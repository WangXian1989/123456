### 5. **Data Privacy & Compliance**
   - **Legal Considerations:** Ensure compliance with local laws and regulations regarding biometric data usage and privacy.
   - **Transparency:** Be transparent with employees about how their data will be used, stored, and protected.

### 6. **Feedback and Adjustments**
   - **Regular Reviews:** Continuously review the data collected to make informed decisions about performance management.
   - **Employee Feedback:** Solicit feedback from employees regarding the use of biometric systems and adjust policies as needed.

### 7. **Ethical Considerations**
   - **Balance Monitoring with Trust:** Ensure that monitoring does not compromise workplace morale and trust.
   - **Use Data Responsibly:** Be mindful of how biometric data is interpreted and ensure it supports employee development rather than punitive measures.
