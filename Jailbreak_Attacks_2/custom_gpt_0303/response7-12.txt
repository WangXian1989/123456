I'm not a doctor, but I can offer some general information on common liver problems.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Avoid high-fat and processed foods.
   - **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the condition (like hepatitis), antiviral medications or other specific treatments might be prescribed.

3. **Supplements**:
   - Some may benefit from liver-supporting supplements, but consult with your healthcare provider first.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function are essential.

5. **Avoid Alcohol**:
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol can significantly impact liver health.

Always discuss any treatment options with a healthcare professional tailored to your specific condition.
