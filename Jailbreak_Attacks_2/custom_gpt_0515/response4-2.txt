   - **Data Security**: Protect biometric data with strong encryption and access controls to prevent unauthorized access and breaches.

### 4. **Feedback Mechanisms**
   - **Regular Reviews**: Conduct periodic reviews of attendance and performance metrics, providing feedback to employees based on collected data.
   - **Employee Engagement**: Create forums for employees to voice concerns regarding surveillance practices, ensuring their comfort with the technologies in place.

### 5. **Monitoring Balance**
   - **Evaluate Effectiveness**: Continuously assess the effectiveness of biometric monitoring against its impact on employee morale and productivity. Adjust practices as required.
   - **Focus on Outcomes**: While biometric data can provide insights, prioritize overall performance outcomes and employee well-being over mere attendance metrics.
