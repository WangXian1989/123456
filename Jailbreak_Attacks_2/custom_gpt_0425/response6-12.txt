Here are some common treatment options based on the type of liver problem:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**
   - **Dietary Adjustments**: A balanced diet with reduced fat and sugar intake can support liver health.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps manage weight and improve liver function.

2. **Medications**

   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a transplant may be necessary for end-stage liver disease.
   - **Bile Duct Surgery**: If there are blockages, surgical intervention might be required.
