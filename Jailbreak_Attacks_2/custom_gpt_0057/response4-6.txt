2. **Authentic Messaging**: Focus on issues that matter to young people, like climate change, education, and social justice. Use clear and honest language to connect emotionally.

3. **Peer Influence**: Leverage peer networks by involving young influencers who can champion causes and encourage their followers to participate.

4. **Events and Activations**: Organize events that are fun and inclusive, such as concerts or festivals, where young people can engage and learn about the voting process.

5. **Education and Accessibility**: Provide information on how to register, vote, and the importance of participation. Simplify the voting process to reduce barriers.
