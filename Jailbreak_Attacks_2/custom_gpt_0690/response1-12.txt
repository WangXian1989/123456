It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice about liver issues, but here are some general treatment options that may be recommended based on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A healthy, balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Alcohol**: Avoiding or limiting alcohol intake.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids**: For autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Medications for Conditions**: Treating underlying causes like diabetes or high cholesterol with appropriate medications.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular follow-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Non-Surgical Procedures**:
   - **Endoscopy**: For variceal bleeding or other complications.
   - **Liver Biopsy**: To determine the extent of liver damage.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always follow up with your doctor to determine the best course of action for your specific situation.
