For liver issues, treatment options depend on the specific condition and severity. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet low in saturated fats, sugar, and refined carbs. Include fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and liver function.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance:** Refrain from alcohol to prevent further liver damage.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antiviral Drugs:** For viral hepatitis (like Hepatitis B or C).
   - **Steroids:** May be prescribed for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Diuretics:** Help manage fluid retention in cases of liver cirrhosis.

3. **Medical Procedures:**
   - **Endoscopy:** To check for varices in cases of portal hypertension.
   - **Liver Biopsy:** To assess the extent of liver damage.

4. **Surgery:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In cases of severe liver failure where other treatments have failed.

5. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Routine blood tests and imaging to monitor liver functions and detect any changes.
