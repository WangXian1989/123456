1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the diagnosis (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), your doctor may prescribe medications to manage the condition.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests are essential to monitor liver function and assess any changes over time.
4. **Surgery or Procedures**: In severe cases, surgical interventions such as liver transplant or procedures to remove blockages may be necessary.
