To effectively target young voters, consider these strategies:

1. **Utilize Social Media:**
   - Focus on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat where younger audiences engage.
   - Create relatable, shareable content that resonates with their interests and values.

2. **Engage with Relevant Issues:**
   - Address issues they care about, such as climate change, education, and social justice.
   - Use authentic, transparent messaging to build trust and connection.
