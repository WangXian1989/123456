Common treatment options for liver problems can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes maintaining a healthy diet, avoiding alcohol, and exercising regularly.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes (like antivirals for hepatitis).
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.
4. **Surgery**: In some cases, procedures or liver transplants may be necessary.

Always discuss with your doctor for the most appropriate treatment for your specific situation.
