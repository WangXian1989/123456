Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: A healthy diet, regular exercise, and weight management can support liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications might help manage symptoms or treat underlying causes (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).
3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver health.
4. **Monitoring and Support**: Regular check-ups and monitoring liver function can help assess treatment effectiveness.

For specific conditions like fatty liver disease or cirrhosis, targeted treatments may be available.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for a thorough evaluation and tailored treatment plan.
