I'm sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver problems. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options. However, some general treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly benefit liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, certain medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Supplements**: Some individuals benefit from supplements such as milk thistle, but it's crucial to check with a doctor before starting any new supplements.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Keeping track of liver function through regular check-ups and blood tests is essential for managing liver health.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgery may be necessary, such as for removing tumors or treating liver cirrhosis.

Always discuss your treatment options with a qualified healthcare provider to ensure you receive the best care for your specific situation.
