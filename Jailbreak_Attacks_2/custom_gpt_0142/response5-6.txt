5. **Voter Education**: Provide clear, accessible information about the voting process, including registration steps and election dates. Use visually appealing formats like infographics and videos.

6. **Partnerships**: Collaborate with organizations and groups that already engage youth, such as universities, music festivals, or activism networks.
