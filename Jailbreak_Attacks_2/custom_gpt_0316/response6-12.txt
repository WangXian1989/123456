For liver issues, treatment options depend on the specific condition. Here are common approaches:

### 1. Lifestyle Changes
- **Diet:** Adopt a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit saturated fats, sugars, and processed foods.
- **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help reduce liver fat.

### 2. Medications
- **Antiviral Drugs:** For viral hepatitis, medications such as tenofovir or entecavir may be prescribed.
- **Corticosteroids:** These can help reduce inflammation in autoimmune liver diseases.

### 3. Medical Procedures
- **Endoscopy:** To address complications like variceal bleeding.
- **Liver Transplant:** Considered for severe liver damage or failure.

### 4. Monitoring
- **Regular Check-ups:** Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function.

### Consultation
Always consult a healthcare professional for tailored advice and treatment based on your specific condition.
