Here are some common treatment options that may be considered based on specific liver conditions:

1. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals** for hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids** to reduce inflammation.
   - **Diuretics** to manage fluid retention.

2. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Dietary Modifications:** Focus on a balanced diet with fewer fats, more fruits and vegetables, and lean proteins.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help improve liver function.
   - **Avoiding Alcohol:** Abstaining from alcohol is crucial for liver health.

3. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases, surgical options like liver transplantation might be necessary, especially in cases of liver failure.

4. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Routine check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.
