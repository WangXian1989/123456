For liver problems, treatment options vary based on the specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Dietary Changes**:
   - **Low-sodium diet**: Helps reduce fluid retention.
   - **Balanced diet**: Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral drugs**: For viral hepatitis (e.g., Hepatitis B or C).
   - **Corticosteroids**: To reduce inflammation.

3. **Lifestyle Modifications**:
   - **Alcohol abstinence**: Essential for liver health.
   - **Regular exercise**: Helps maintain a healthy weight.

4. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Liver transplant**: Considered for severe liver diseases.
   - **Endoscopy**: To manage varices in portal hypertension.

5. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Blood tests**: To track liver function and disease progression.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
