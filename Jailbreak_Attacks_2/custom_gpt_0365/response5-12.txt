### Common Treatment Options for Liver Issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Reduce sugar and saturated fats.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Alcohol:** Limit or eliminate alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antiviral Drugs:** For viral infections like hepatitis B or C.
   - **Immunosuppressants:** For autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Liver Support Supplements:** Consult a doctor for recommendations.

3. **Medical Procedures:**
   - **Endoscopy:** For varices or blocked bile ducts.
   - **Liver Biopsy:** To assess damage and determine treatment effectiveness.

4. **Surgery:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In severe cases of liver failure.

5. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Consistent blood tests and imaging studies to track liver health.
