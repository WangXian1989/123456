### 5. **Data Management**
   - **Ensure Compliance**: Adhere to local laws and regulations regarding data privacy and biometric data usage. Inform employees how their data will be used and stored.
   - **Secure Data Storage**: Implement robust cybersecurity measures to protect sensitive data from breaches.

### 6. **Feedback and Adaptation**
   - **Regular Reviews**: Monitor the system's effectiveness and gather feedback from employees. Make necessary adjustments to improve accuracy and user acceptance.
