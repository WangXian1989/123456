I'm not a doctor, but the best treatment options for liver problems often include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Healthy diet (low in fat and sugar).
   - Regular exercise.
   - Avoiding alcohol and drugs.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the type of liver disease (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests.

4. **Management of Conditions**: 
   - Treating underlying conditions like diabetes or high cholesterol.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases (e.g., liver transplant).

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment.
