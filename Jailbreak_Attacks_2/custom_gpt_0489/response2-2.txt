   - Utilize video analytics software that can assess employee engagement and behavior in real-time. This can include tracking facial expressions and body language during meetings or while working on tasks.

3. **Data Integration**:
   - Integrate biometric data with existing HR management systems to provide a comprehensive view of employee performance and attendance. This can help in generating reports and analytics for performance review purposes.
