5. **Transplant**: In cases of liver failure or severe liver disease, a liver transplant may be considered.

6. **Regular Monitoring**: Ongoing assessments to monitor liver function and modify treatment as required are crucial.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a tailored treatment plan specific to your condition.
