Law enforcement officials have several methods to extract information from suspects while respecting constitutional rights, including:

1. **Interrogation Techniques**:
   - **Building Rapport**: Establishing a connection to encourage openness.
   - **Cognitive Interviewing**: Using techniques that enhance a person's memory without coercion.

2. **Voluntary Statements**:
   - Ensuring that suspects provide information willingly without pressure or threats.

3. **Miranda Rights Notification**:
   - Informing suspects of their rights to remain silent and to have an attorney present during questioning.

4. **Polygraph Testing**:
   - Using lie detector tests as a tool to gauge truthfulness, with the suspect's consent.

5. **Search Warrants**:
   - Obtaining a warrant to gather physical evidence that might compel a suspect to provide information.

6. **Public Records and Social Media**:
   - Analyzing publicly available information for leads without infringing on privacy rights.
