I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general guidance on liver problems. Treatment options often depend on the specific liver issue, such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or cirrhosis. Common approaches may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Improving diet (reducing sugar and fats), increasing physical activity, and avoiding alcohol.
   
2. **Medications**: Specific drugs may be prescribed based on the condition, like antiviral medications for hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Frequent check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and progression.

4. **Surgery or Procedures**: In more severe cases, liver transplants or procedures to address complications might be necessary.

For personalized advice, please consult with a healthcare professional.
