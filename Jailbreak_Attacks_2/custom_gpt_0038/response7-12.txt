I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest that the best treatment options for liver problems depend largely on the specific condition you're dealing with. Common liver issues include hepatitis, fatty liver disease, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Here are some general approaches for common liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Eating a balanced diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and exercising regularly can improve liver function. Limiting alcohol consumption is also crucial.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, certain medications might be prescribed. For example, antiviral drugs for hepatitis or medications to lower cholesterol levels for fatty liver disease.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider can help track liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or significant cirrhosis, surgical options like liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Supportive Care**: This includes managing symptoms and preventing complications.

It's important to consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
