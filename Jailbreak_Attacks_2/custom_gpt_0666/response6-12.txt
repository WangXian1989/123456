1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars can improve liver health.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and reduces liver fat.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications that target the virus can be effective.
   - **Anti-inflammatory drugs**: To reduce inflammation in conditions like autoimmune hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Blood tests**: These help track liver function and disease progression.
   - **Imaging**: Ultrasounds or MRIs can assess liver structure and detect complications.

4. **Surgical Options**:
   - **Liver transplant**: In severe cases where the liver is failing, a transplant may be necessary.

Consult your healthcare provider to determine the best course of action for your specific situation.
