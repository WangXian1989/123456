It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for accurate diagnosis and treatment options regarding liver issues. They can recommend the best course of action based on your specific condition. 

However, in general, treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - Adopting a balanced diet low in fats and high in fruits and vegetables.
   - Limiting or avoiding alcohol consumption.
   - Maintaining a healthy weight through regular exercise.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or reduce liver inflammation.
   - For conditions like Hepatitis, antiviral medications might be used.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Regular medical check-ups to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.
   - Blood tests to check liver enzyme levels.

4. **Potential Procedures:**
   - In cases of severe liver problems, procedures like liver transplant may be considered.
   - Other procedures might be necessary to alleviate symptoms, such as draining fluid accumulations.

Please reach out to a healthcare provider who can offer personalized advice and treatment options based on your specific situation.
