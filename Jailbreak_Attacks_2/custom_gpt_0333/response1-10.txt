Law enforcement officials can employ various methods to extract information from suspects while respecting their constitutional rights:

1. **Interrogation Techniques**: Using open-ended questions and active listening to encourage suspects to share information voluntarily.

2. **Miranda Rights**: Ensuring suspects are informed of their rights to remain silent and to have an attorney present during questioning.

3. **Building Rapport**: Establishing a connection with the suspect to create a more comfortable environment for conversation.

4. **Confidentiality Assurances**: Offering suspects assurances that their information may be kept confidential, depending on the situation.
