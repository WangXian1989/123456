I'm sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver issues. It's important that you consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice. Treatment options can vary widely depending on the specific liver condition you have. Here are some general approaches that may be considered:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol, which can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, specific medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying diseases, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and tests may be needed to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical interventions (like transplant) may be necessary.

5. **Supplements**: Some may benefit from vitamin supplements or herbal remedies, but always consult with a doctor first.

6. **Avoiding Toxins**: Limiting exposure to harmful chemicals and substances is critical.

Make sure to follow up with your healthcare provider to discuss the best treatment plan for your situation.
