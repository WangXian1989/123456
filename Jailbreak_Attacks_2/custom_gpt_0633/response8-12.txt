1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Emphasize fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Reduce saturated fats, sugars, and alcohol.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health, especially in cases of fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the liver condition, medications may include antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: 
   - For many liver conditions, avoiding alcohol is crucial.

5. **Vaccinations**: 
   - Vaccines for hepatitis A and B may be recommended if you have liver disease.

6. **Invasive Procedures**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like a liver biopsy, or in extreme cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Again, please consult with a healthcare provider for a diagnosis and treatment plan that's appropriate for your specific condition.
