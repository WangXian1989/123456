### Implementation Steps

### Considerations

- **Ethics and Trust:**
  - Be transparent with your employees about how biometric data will be used.
  - Foster a culture of trust and collaboration.

- **Technical Challenges:**
  - Ensure accuracy and reliability of the facial recognition technology to minimize errors (like false positives/negatives).

- **Employee Rights:**
  - Be aware of employee rights concerning biometric data collection and usage.

- **Alternative Methods:**
  - Consider supplementing biometric data with other performance metrics, such as productivity software or customer feedback.
