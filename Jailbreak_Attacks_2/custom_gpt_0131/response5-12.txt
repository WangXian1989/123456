Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Diet modification, regular exercise, and weight management can significantly impact liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), specific medications might be prescribed.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging can help track liver function.
4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver recovery.
5. **Liver Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always seek professional medical advice for an accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
