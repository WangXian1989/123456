I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options. However, general treatment options for liver problems can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthier diet, exercising, and avoiding alcohol and harmful substances.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.

4. **Avoiding Toxins**: Staying away from chemicals and toxins that can harm the liver.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for the best course of action tailored to your situation.
