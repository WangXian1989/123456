1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eat a balanced diet low in saturated fats, sugar, and salt.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying issues (e.g., antivirals for hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function and condition.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - Some patients explore herbal remedies or supplements, but it's essential to discuss these with a healthcare provider.

Consulting a healthcare professional for a personalized plan is crucial.
