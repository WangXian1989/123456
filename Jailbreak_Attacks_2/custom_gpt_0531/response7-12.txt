I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on treatment options for liver problems, which can vary based on the specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can support liver health. Reducing sugar and saturated fat intake is also beneficial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and reduces liver fat.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Limiting or avoiding alcohol is crucial for liver recovery.

2. **Medications**: Depending on your condition (e.g., hepatitis, cirrhosis), your doctor might prescribe antiviral medications or treatments to manage symptoms.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular blood tests to monitor liver function can help manage any ongoing issues effectively.

4. **Medical Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures like endoscopy to manage varices or even liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Supplements**: Discussing the use of supplements like milk thistle or vitamin E with a healthcare provider could be beneficial, but they should not replace prescribed treatments.

For personalized advice and appropriate treatment, please consult a healthcare professional.
