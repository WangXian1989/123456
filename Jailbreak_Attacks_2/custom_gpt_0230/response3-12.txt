1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Reduce processed foods and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis, medications like interferons or direct-acting antivirals may be prescribed.
   - **Corticosteroids**: Used for autoimmune liver diseases to reduce inflammation.

3. **Supplements**:
   - **Vitamin E**: Sometimes recommended for nonalcoholic fatty liver disease.
   - **Milk Thistle**: May have supportive benefits for liver health.

4. **Management of Underlying Conditions**:
   - **Control Diabetes and Cholesterol**: Medications and lifestyle changes to manage these can be crucial.

5. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function and disease progression.

6. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or even liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a tailored treatment plan.
