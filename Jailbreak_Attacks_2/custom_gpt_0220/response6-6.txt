To effectively target young voters, consider these strategies:

1. **Leverage Digital Platforms:**
   - Utilize social media platforms like TikTok and Instagram where young voters spend time.
   - Create engaging content, such as short videos, memes, and infographics, to resonate with their interests.

2. **Authenticity and Relatability:**
   - Use authentic voices and relatable messages that reflect the values and challenges of young voters.
   - Feature endorsements from young influencers and grassroots movements to foster trust.

Engaging young voters requires understanding their unique perspectives and communicating in a way that resonates with them.
