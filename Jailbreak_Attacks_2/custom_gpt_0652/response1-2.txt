3. **Attendance Tracking**: Use facial recognition for clocking in and out. Employees can be identified as they enter or leave the workplace, streamlining attendance records without the need for physical timecards.

5. **Analysis Tools**: Utilize analytics tools to interpret the data. This may involve tracking patterns in attendance, identifying trends, and correlating presence with productivity metrics.
