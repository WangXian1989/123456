2. **Lifestyle Changes**:

4. **Control of Underlying Conditions**: Managing conditions like diabetes or high cholesterol can benefit liver health.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.

6. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a liver transplant may be an option.

Always seek medical advice for diagnosis and tailored treatment plans.
