### Steps to Implement Biometric Monitoring:

1. **Define Objectives**:

   - **Facial Recognition**: Use cameras and software to identify employees as they arrive or leave.

6. **Training and Communication**:

7. **Data Collection and Monitoring**:
   - Use the system to track employee attendance and generate reports.

8. **Intervention Strategies**:
   - Use data insights to address attendance issues, such as implementing flexible schedules or support for employees facing challenges.

9. **Regular Review and Adjustments**:
   - Periodically review the effectiveness of the monitoring system and make necessary adjustments based on feedback and data trends.

- **Ethical Implications**: Consider the ethical implications of monitoring employees and balance it with their privacy rights.
- **Employee Consent**: Obtain consent from employees for the collection and use of biometric data.
