I'm not a doctor, but I can offer some general information regarding common liver problems and their treatment options. If you have a specific liver issue such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, or cirrhosis, treatment can vary based on the condition's severity and underlying causes. 

Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and exercising regularly can significantly impact liver health. Reducing alcohol intake is also crucial.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, your doctor might prescribe medications to treat infections (like antivirals for hepatitis) or to manage symptoms.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: For chronic liver conditions, regular check-ups with blood tests and imaging studies help monitor liver function and progression of disease.

4. **Avoiding Toxins**: Limiting exposure to harmful substances, including certain medications and environmental toxins, can help protect your liver.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases of liver disease, a liver transplant may be necessary. This is usually considered when other treatment options are not effective.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
