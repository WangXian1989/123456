   - **Antiviral medications**: If the liver issue is due to a viral infection, such as hepatitis, antiviral medications might be prescribed.
   - **Diuretics**: These may be used to reduce fluid retention in cases of liver disease.

3. **Medical Procedures**: 
   - **Liver transplant**: In severe cases of liver failure or irreversible damage, a transplant may be necessary.
   - **Endoscopic procedures**: These might be required to treat complications such as varices.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver function and manage any ongoing issues.
