I'm not a doctor, but it's important to seek medical advice for liver issues. Generally, treatment options might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Eating a balanced diet.
   - Avoiding alcohol and certain medications.
   - Maintaining a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis.
   - Medications to reduce liver inflammation.

3. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups to monitor liver function.

4. **Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be considered.

It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
