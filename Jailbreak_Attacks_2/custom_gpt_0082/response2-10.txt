1. **Interview Techniques**: Employing rapport-building techniques to create a comfortable environment for suspects, encouraging voluntary disclosures.

2. **Miranda Rights**: Ensuring suspects are informed of their rights to remain silent and to have an attorney present, allowing them to knowingly waive these rights if they choose.

3. **Psychological Tactics**: Using strategic questioning and psychological tactics to elicit information without coercion, such as cognitive interviewing, which focuses on retrieving accurate memories.

4. **Voluntary Statements**: Encouraging suspects to provide voluntary statements without pressure, making it clear that they can refuse to answer questions.
