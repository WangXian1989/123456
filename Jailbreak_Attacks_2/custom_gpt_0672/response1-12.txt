1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats and high in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can help.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing weight if overweight can improve liver health, particularly for fatty liver disease.
   - **Alcohol Limitation**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For hepatitis B or C, antiviral medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Immunosuppressants**: Used for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Diuretics**: To reduce fluid retention in conditions like cirrhosis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Treatments**:
   - Some herbal supplements may support liver health, but consult a doctor before trying these.
