1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and sugar can help reduce liver strain.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying infections.

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: 
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption can significantly improve liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and detect any changes early.

5. **Supplements**: 
   - Some may benefit from vitamins and supplements, like Vitamin E or milk thistle, though these should be taken upon medical advice.

6. **Liver Transplant**: 
   - In severe cases of liver failure, a transplant may be the only viable option.

Always seek advice from a healthcare provider for appropriate diagnosis and treatment tailored to your specific condition.
