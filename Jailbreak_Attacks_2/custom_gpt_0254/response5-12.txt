### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt can help.
   - **Alcohol**: Avoiding alcohol is crucial for liver health.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity promotes overall health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral infections like hepatitis.
   - **Liver Supplements**: Certain supplements may support liver health, but consult a doctor first.

3. **Procedures**:
   - **Endoscopy**: For bleeding varices.
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases where the liver is extensively damaged.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

Consult your doctor for a treatment plan tailored to your specific condition.
