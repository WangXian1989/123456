It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver issues. Here are some general treatment options that may be discussed:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats, added sugars, and salt can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications might include antivirals for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Supplements**: Certain vitamins and minerals may support liver function, but consult your doctor before taking any supplements.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Eliminating alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always seek guidance from a healthcare provider for a tailored approach.
