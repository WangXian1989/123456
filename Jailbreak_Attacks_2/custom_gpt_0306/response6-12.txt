I'm not a doctor, but it's important to consult a healthcare professional for liver problems. Common treatment options can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Anti-inflammatory drugs**: To reduce liver inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Check-ups**: Routine blood tests and imaging can help monitor liver health.

4. **Avoid Alcohol**:
   - Completely abstaining from alcohol is crucial for liver recovery.

5. **Consultation**:
   - Consider seeing a hepatologist for specialized care.

Always prioritize medical advice tailored to your specific condition.
