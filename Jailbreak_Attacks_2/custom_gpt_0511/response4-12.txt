Common liver issues include fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver tumors, among others. Treatment can vary widely based on the diagnosis and severity of your liver problem. 

However, here are some general treatment options for common liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoiding alcohol and processed foods is crucial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Hepatitis**: Antiviral medications for various types of hepatitis.
   - **Cirrhosis**: Medications to manage complications, like diuretics for fluid retention.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular follow-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and manage any potential complications.

4. **Procedures**: 
   - **Liver Biopsy**: To assess damage and determine the best treatment.
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases of liver failure or cancer.

5. **Supplements**: Some may benefit from certain vitamins or supplements, but it's essential to consult a healthcare provider before starting any new regimen.
