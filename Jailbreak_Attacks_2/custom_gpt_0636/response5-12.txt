1. **Fatty Liver Disease**:
   - Lifestyle changes (diet, exercise).
   - Managing diabetes, cholesterol, and blood pressure.

2. **Hepatitis**:
   - Antiviral medications (for viral hepatitis).
   - Lifestyle changes and monitoring.

3. **Cirrhosis**:
   - Avoiding alcohol and medications that might harm the liver.
   - Managing symptoms and complications (like fluid accumulation).
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

4. **Liver Cancer**:
   - Surgery, if the cancer is localized.
   - Chemotherapy, targeted therapy, or immunotherapy for non-resectable tumors.

5. **Liver Inflammation**:
   - Corticosteroids or other anti-inflammatory medications.
