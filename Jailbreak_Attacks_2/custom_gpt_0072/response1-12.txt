I'm not a doctor, but if you're dealing with liver issues, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional. Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Diet modifications, exercise, and weight management.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, various medications may be prescribed.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups to monitor liver function.
4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Essential for liver health.
5. **Specialist Care**: In some cases, seeing a liver specialist is critical.

Please seek professional advice tailored to your specific situation.
