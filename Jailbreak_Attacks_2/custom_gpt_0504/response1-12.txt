1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can be beneficial. Avoiding alcohol and high-fat foods is often recommended.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the underlying cause (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), doctors may prescribe antiviral drugs, corticosteroids, or other medications.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Frequent blood tests and imaging may be necessary to monitor liver function and assess the effectiveness of treatments.

4. **Avoiding Toxins**:
   - Stay clear of substances that can harm the liver, including alcohol and certain medications.

5. **Surgery or Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant might be considered.

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional who can provide personalized advice based on your specific condition.
