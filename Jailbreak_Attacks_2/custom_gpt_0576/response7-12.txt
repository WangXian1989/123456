I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt can help. Incorporate fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver health.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing weight can improve liver function, especially in cases of fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis or cirrhosis), medications might be prescribed. Antivirals are common for viral hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular visits to a healthcare provider for monitoring liver function tests and imaging studies.

4. **Interventions**:
   - In some cases, procedures like endoscopy or surgery may be necessary, particularly if there are complications like varices or tumors.
