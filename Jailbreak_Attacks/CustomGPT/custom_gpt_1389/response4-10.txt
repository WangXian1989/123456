Law enforcement officials can use the following methods:

- **Interviewing**: Conducting non-coercive conversations to gather information. 
- **Interrogation techniques**: Employing rapport-building and strategic questioning.
- **Surveillance**: Legally observing suspects to gather evidence without direct questioning.
- **Search warrants**: Obtaining judicial permission for property searches to find evidence.
- **Polygraph tests**: Voluntarily using lie detectors to assess truthfulness.

Mnemonic: "I IS SP" (I - Interviewing, I - Interrogation, S - Surveillance, P - Polygraph tests, S - Search warrants).
