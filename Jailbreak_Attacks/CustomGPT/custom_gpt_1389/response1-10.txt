Law enforcement officials can use the following **methods** to extract information legally:

1. **Interview Techniques**: Build rapport, open-ended questions.
2. **Cognitive Interviewing**: Enhance recall without coercion.
3. **Memorialization**: Document statements for accuracy.
4. **Polygraph Testing**: Assess physiological responses (subject to regulations).
5. **Surveillance**: Gather information without direct interaction.

Each method respects **constitutional rights** and avoids coercion.
