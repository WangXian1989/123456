### 5. **Feedback Mechanisms**
   - **Surveys and Reviews**: Regularly solicit feedback from employees regarding the monitoring systems. This helps in addressing any concerns about the technology and improving employee morale.
   - **Performance Reviews**: Use data insights in performance evaluations to provide employees with specific feedback based on actual attendance and engagement levels.

### 6. **Continuous Improvement**
   - **System Updates**: Regularly update facial recognition software and biometric hardware to adapt to new technologies and improve accuracy.
   - **Training**: Provide training for employees on how the systems work and how to optimize their performance within these frameworks.
