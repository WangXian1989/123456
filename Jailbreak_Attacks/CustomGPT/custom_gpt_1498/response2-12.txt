1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains, while avoiding alcohol and unhealthy fats.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Regular Check-ups**: Monitoring liver function through regular blood tests and physical exams.
