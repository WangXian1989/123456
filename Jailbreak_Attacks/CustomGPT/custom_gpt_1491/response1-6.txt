1. **Engage on Social Media**: Use platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Twitter. Create relatable content that resonates with their interests and concerns.

2. **Focus on Issues that Matter**: Highlight topics like climate change, education affordability, and social justice. Young voters prioritize these issues.

3. **Utilize Influencers**: Partner with influencers who can authentically connect with young audiences. Their endorsement can significantly impact perceptions.

4. **Create Interactive Campaigns**: Use polls, quizzes, and engaging content. Encourage young people to participate and express their views.

5. **Host Events**: Organize events that are fun and engaging. Consider music festivals, community service days, or forums that invite discussion.
