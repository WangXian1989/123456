### General Treatment Options:

1. **Diet and Nutrition**:
   - **Low Fat Diet**: Reducing saturated fats can help manage liver diseases like fatty liver.
   - **Increased Fiber**: Fruits, vegetables, and whole grains support overall liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: Used for viral hepatitis (e.g., Hepatitis B and C).
   - **Corticosteroids**: To treat autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-Ups**:
   - Routine blood tests to check liver function and monitor disease progression.

4. **Surgical Options**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: Considered for chronic liver disease or liver failure when other treatments are ineffective.
