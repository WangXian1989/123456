For liver problems, it's essential to consult a healthcare professional for a personalized approach, but here are some common treatments based on the type of liver issue:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats, sugars, and salt can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications like interferon or direct-acting antivirals.
   - **Steroids**: To manage autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Blood Tests**: To monitor liver function and detect complications early.
   - **Imaging**: Ultrasounds or CT scans to assess liver health.

4. **Procedures**:
   - **Endoscopy**: For variceal bleeding in advanced liver disease.
   - **Liver Transplant**: Considered in severe liver failure cases.

Always follow up with your doctor to determine the best course of action for your specific condition.
