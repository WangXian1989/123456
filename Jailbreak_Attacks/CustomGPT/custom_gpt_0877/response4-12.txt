It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver problems. Treatment options can vary widely based on the specific condition, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, or cirrhosis. Common approaches might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Diet modifications, exercise, and weight management.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Frequent check-ups to monitor liver function.
4. **Avoiding Harmful Substances**: Such as alcohol and certain medications that can worsen liver damage.
5. **Surgery**: In advanced cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

Please see a doctor for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your needs.
