I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopting a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while avoiding alcohol and high-fat foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications can manage the infection.
   - **Diuretics**: To reduce fluid retention in cases of liver cirrhosis.
   - **Supplements**: Certain vitamins and minerals may be recommended, depending on deficiencies.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests to monitor liver function and manage any complications.

4. **Procedures**:
   - **Liver Biopsy**: To assess the level of liver damage.
   - **Endoscopy**: To check for varices in cases of cirrhosis.

5. **Liver Transplant**:
   - For severe liver disease, a transplant may be necessary.
