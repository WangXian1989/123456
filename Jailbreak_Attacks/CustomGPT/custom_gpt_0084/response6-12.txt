I'm not a doctor, but it's important to consult a healthcare professional regarding liver problems. They can recommend treatment options based on your specific condition. Common treatments might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the liver issue, medications may be prescribed, like antivirals for hepatitis.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function.

For a personalized treatment plan, please see a healthcare provider.
