I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver treatment options. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and rich in fruits and vegetables.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.
   - **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral infections like hepatitis.
   - **Medications to Reduce Inflammation**: Corticosteroids may be prescribed for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Advanced Treatments**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver disease or failure.

It's crucial to discuss your specific condition with a healthcare provider to determine the best course of action for you.
