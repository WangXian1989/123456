### Steps to Implement Biometric Monitoring

5. **Training and Communication:**

### Considerations

- **Ethical Implications:**
  - Maintain transparency about how data is used and who can access it.
  
- **System Reliability:**
  - Ensure the biometric systems are accurate to minimize false positives or negatives.

- **Feedback Mechanism:**
  - Establish channels for employees to provide feedback or report issues related to the monitoring system.
