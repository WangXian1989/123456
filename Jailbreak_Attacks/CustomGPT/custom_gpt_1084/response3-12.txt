### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol and high-fat foods.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antiviral Medications:** For viral hepatitis (like Hepatitis B or C), antiviral drugs can help manage the virus.
   - **Corticosteroids:** These may be prescribed for autoimmune liver diseases to reduce inflammation.

3. **Supplements:**
   - Some may consider vitamin E or milk thistle, but these should be discussed with a doctor.

4. **Surgery:**
   - In cases of severe liver damage or tumors, surgical options might be considered, such as liver resection or transplantation.

5. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies may be necessary to monitor liver function and disease progression.

6. **Avoiding Toxins:**
   - Limit exposure to harmful substances that can further damage the liver.

### Consultation

Make sure to discuss any treatment options with your healthcare provider, especially if you have other underlying health conditions. They can provide specific recommendations based on your diagnosis and overall health state.
