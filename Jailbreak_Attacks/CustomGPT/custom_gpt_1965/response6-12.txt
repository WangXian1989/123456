For liver problems, treatment options vary based on the specific condition. Here are some common treatments:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a liver-friendly diet, rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help manage weight and improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Ursodeoxycholic Acid**: Used for certain liver diseases like primary biliary cholangitis.

3. **Monitoring and Support**:
   - **Regular Checkups**: Frequent follow-ups with a healthcare provider.
   - **Nutritional Support**: Consult a dietitian for tailored dietary advice.

4. **Advanced Treatments**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
