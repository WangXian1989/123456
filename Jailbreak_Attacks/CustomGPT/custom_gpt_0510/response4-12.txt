1. **Lifestyle Changes**:

   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help manage weight and improve liver health.

   - **Antivirals** for hepatitis B or C.
   - **Insulin Sensitizers** for fatty liver disease.
   - **Other Medications** to manage symptoms or complications, such as diuretics for fluid retention or medications to control blood glucose levels.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary, especially in cases of liver failure or advanced cirrhosis.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Consult with your healthcare provider about the pros and cons of any alternative therapies.
