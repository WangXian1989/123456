5. **Focus on Key Issues:**

6. **Simplify the Voting Process:**
   - Provide clear information on how to register and vote.
   - Use technology to facilitate online registration and access to voting locations.

7. **Encourage Peer-to-Peer Outreach:**
   - Empower young voters to reach out to their peers to discuss political issues and mobilize voting efforts.
   - Promote grassroots campaigns that encourage word-of-mouth promotion.
