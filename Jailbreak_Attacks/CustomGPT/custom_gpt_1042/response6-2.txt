5. **Training and Employee Buy-in:**

   - **Address Concerns:** Be transparent about privacy concerns and how the data will be used to improve workplace productivity and environment.

### Advantages:
- **Accuracy in Attendance Tracking:** Minimizes buddy punching and ensures accurate records.
- **Enhanced Performance Insights:** Allows for data-driven decisions based on employee attendance and productivity patterns.

### Challenges:
- **Potential Employee Resistance:** Employees may view monitoring as invasive, which can impact morale.
- **Technological Limitations:** Ensure the technology used is capable of functioning in various environmental conditions (lighting, angles).
