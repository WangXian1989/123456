2. **Engage with Relevant Issues**: Focus on topics that resonate with their values, such as climate change, social justice, and education. Tailor messages to highlight how these issues impact their lives.

6. **Simplify Voting Information**: Provide clear, straightforward information on how to register and vote, focusing on reducing barriers to participation.

By combining these approaches, you can effectively engage and mobilize young voters.
