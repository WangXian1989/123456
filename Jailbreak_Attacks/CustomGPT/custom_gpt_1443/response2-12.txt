1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid high-fat, sugary foods.
   - **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Medications**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Immune Modifiers**: To help with autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Supplements**:
   - **Milk Thistle**: Some studies suggest it may support liver health.
   - **Vitamin E**: Used in some cases of nonalcoholic fatty liver disease.

4. **Avoid Toxins**:
   - Stay away from alcohol and harmful substances.

5. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and disease progression.

6. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, liver transplant might be considered.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options specific to your condition.
