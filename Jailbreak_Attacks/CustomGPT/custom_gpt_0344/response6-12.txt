I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver treatment options. However, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

1. **Medication:**
   - **Antiviral drugs** for hepatitis.
   - **Medications** to reduce inflammation or improve liver function.

2. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Alcohol avoidance**: Essential for liver health.
   - **Regular Exercise**: Helps in maintaining a healthy weight.

3. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases, options like liver transplants may be considered.

4. **Monitoring:**
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

Always follow your healthcare provider's recommendations for the best outcomes.
