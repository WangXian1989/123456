1. **Lifestyle Changes**:

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Keep up with regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and structure.

4. **Weight Loss**: If overweight, losing even a small percentage of body weight can help with fatty liver disease.

5. **Avoid Toxins**: Stay away from toxins and chemicals that can worsen liver function.

6. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a precise diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
