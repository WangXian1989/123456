### 1. **Lifestyle Changes**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol, fatty foods, and sugars.
   - **Weight Management:** Maintain a healthy weight to reduce liver strain.

### 2. **Medications**
   - **Antiviral Medications:** For viral hepatitis, medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Immune Modulators:** In autoimmune liver diseases, these medications can help regulate the immune response.

### 3. **Regular Monitoring**
   - **Routine Check-ups:** Regular visits to a healthcare provider assist in monitoring liver function and disease progression.

### 4. **Surgical Options**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In severe cases where liver function is severely compromised, transplantation may be necessary.

### 5. **Alternative Therapies**
   - **Herbal Remedies:** Some people explore herbal treatments, but it's vital to consult with a healthcare provider first.

### 6. **Manage Coexisting Conditions**
   - **Control Diabetes and Cholesterol:** Managing these conditions can help reduce liver stress.

### Consultation
Always consult with a healthcare professional for a tailored plan that fits your specific situation. They may refer you to a hepatologist, a liver specialist, for more specialized care.
