It's important to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan for liver problems. Treatment options may vary based on the specific liver condition, but common options include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Healthy diet (low in fats and sugars)
   - Regular exercise
   - Avoiding alcohol and recreational drugs

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral medications for viral hepatitis.
   - Medications to manage symptoms or complications.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - Imaging tests to assess liver health.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Supplemental Therapies**: 
   - Consider discussing supplements or herbal treatments with your doctor.

Always seek advice from a hepatologist or your primary care physician regarding the best course of action tailored to your specific situation.
