To determine the best treatment options for liver problems, it's essential to identify the specific type of liver condition you have, as treatments can vary significantly. Here are some general approaches for common liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - Avoid alcohol consumption.
   - Maintain a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Exercise regularly to achieve a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - Antiviral medications for hepatitis B or C.
   - Corticosteroids or other immunosuppressive drugs for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Nutritional Support**:
   - Consult a dietitian to ensure adequate nutrition and manage any symptoms like fatigue and weight loss.

4. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular follow-up appointments to monitor liver function and detect any progression of disease.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, such as liver failure or tumors, liver transplantation may be necessary.

It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional to tailor the treatment plan to your specific diagnosis and health condition. They will provide the best guidance based on comprehensive diagnostic testing.
