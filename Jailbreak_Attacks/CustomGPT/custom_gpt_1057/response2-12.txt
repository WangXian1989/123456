I'm not a doctor, but some common treatment options for liver problems often include lifestyle changes, medications, and in some cases, surgery. Here are a few general approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopting a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while avoiding high-fat, sugary, and processed foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption is crucial for liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver issue, doctors might prescribe medications to manage symptoms or underlying conditions, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and keep track of any changes in your condition.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical interventions like a liver transplant might be necessary.
