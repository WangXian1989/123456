6. **Polygraph Tests**: While not always admissible in court, polygraph tests can be used to encourage suspects to tell the truth or confess voluntarily.

7. **Informal Conversations**: Engaging suspects in casual conversation can sometimes lead to them disclosing information unintentionally, without feeling pressured.

By employing these methods, law enforcement can gather necessary evidence and information while maintaining constitutional protections for suspects.
