### 5. **Performance Monitoring**
   - Use biometric data to track how often employees are present and engaged in their work environments.

### 6. **Data Privacy and Compliance**
   - Ensure compliance with privacy laws (like GDPR or others applicable in your region) regarding the collection and use of biometric data.
   - Inform employees about how their data will be used and obtain their consent.

### 7. **Employee Feedback**
   - Collect feedback from employees on how they perceive the monitoring systems. It's essential for maintaining trust and morale.

### 8. **Performance Reviews**
   - Use attendance data as part of the overall performance review process. Consider attendance as a factor but ensure that other qualitative measures are also evaluated.

### 9. **Continuous Improvement**
