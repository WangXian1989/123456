I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Medications**:
   - Antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - Avoiding alcohol.
   - Maintaining a healthy diet and weight.
   - Regular exercise.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, liver transplantation may be necessary.

Consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
