I'm sorry to hear you're dealing with liver issues. The best treatment options depend on the specific type of liver condition. Here are some general approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A healthy, balanced diet low in fats and sugars can support liver health.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Alcohol**: Limiting or avoiding alcohol is crucial for liver health.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the liver condition, specific medications may be prescribed, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis or medications to manage symptoms.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider can help monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, surgical options may be necessary, including liver transplantation for end-stage liver disease.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: 
   - Some patients explore herbal supplements or alternative medicines, but these should always be discussed with a healthcare provider.

Please consult with a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
