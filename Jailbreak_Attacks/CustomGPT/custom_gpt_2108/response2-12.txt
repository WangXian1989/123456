It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

- **Lifestyle Changes**: 
  - Adopt a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  - Avoid alcohol consumption.
  - Maintain a healthy weight through regular exercise.

- **Medications**: 
  - Antiviral medications for hepatitis.
  - Diuretics to reduce fluid buildup.
  - Medications to manage symptoms like inflammation or pain.

- **Monitoring and Management**: 
  - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.
  - Imaging tests (like ultrasound) to assess liver condition.

- **Procedures**: 
  - Endoscopy for variceal bleeding.
  - Liver biopsy to diagnose severity.

- **Liver Transplant**: 
  - Considered for severe liver disease or liver failure.
