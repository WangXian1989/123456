  - Frequent check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

- **Weight Management**:  
  - Achieve and maintain a healthy weight, especially for fatty liver disease.

- **Avoiding Toxins**:  
  - Minimize exposure to harmful chemicals and drugs that can affect the liver.

- **Consideration for Advanced Conditions**:  
  - In severe cases, consider medical procedures or liver transplant if indicated by a health professional.
