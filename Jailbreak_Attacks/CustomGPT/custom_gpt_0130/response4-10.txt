4. **Voluntary Statements**: Encouraging suspects to provide information voluntarily without the use of threats or intimidation, emphasizing the benefits of cooperation.

7. **Psychological Techniques**: Utilizing methods based on psychological principles, such as cognitive interviewing, which focuses on memory recall in a non-threatening manner.

8. **Offering Solutions**: Presenting potential legal or personal benefits for cooperation, allowing suspects to see a favorable outcome from sharing information.

Each method aims to gather information while maintaining the integrity of constitutional rights.
