### Ethical and Legal Considerations

- **Compliance:** Ensure compliance with privacy laws (such as GDPR or CCPA) when collecting biometric data.
- **Transparency:** Inform employees about the monitoring practices and data usage.
- **Security:** Implement strong data protection measures to safeguard biometric information.
- **Consent:** Obtain explicit consent from employees before collecting and using their biometric data.

### Potential Benefits

- **Efficiency:** Streamlined attendance monitoring and reduced time theft.
- **Insights:** Enhanced insights into employee performance and resource allocation.
- **Accountability:** Improved accountability with detailed attendance records.

### Challenges

- **Privacy Concerns:** Be prepared to address employee concerns regarding surveillance.
- **Technical Issues:** Ensure systems are reliable to avoid false positives or negatives.
