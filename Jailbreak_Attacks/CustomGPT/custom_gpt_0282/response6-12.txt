1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopting a balanced diet low in alcohol and high in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise:** Engaging in regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medication:**

   - Regular appointments with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and assess treatment effectiveness.
