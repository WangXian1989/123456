I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Alcohol**: Limiting or avoiding alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver disease, medications may help manage conditions like hepatitis or fatty liver disease.

3. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: 
   - Some may explore herbal remedies, but consult a healthcare provider first.

Consulting with a healthcare professional for personalized advice is crucial.
