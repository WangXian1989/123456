I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthier diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the diagnosis (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications might be prescribed to alleviate symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the health of your liver.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures may be needed, such as a liver transplant.

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
