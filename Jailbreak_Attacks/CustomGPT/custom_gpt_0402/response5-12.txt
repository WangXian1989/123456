I'm sorry to hear that you're dealing with a liver problem. It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional who can provide tailored advice based on your medical history and current condition. Generally, treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. Medications: Depending on the specific liver condition (such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or cirrhosis), various medications may be prescribed.
2. Lifestyle Changes: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising, and avoiding alcohol and toxins can significantly improve liver health.
3. Regular Monitoring: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and assess the progression of the disease.
4. Advanced Treatments: In severe cases, options such as liver transplant surgery might be considered.

Please consult with a healthcare provider for a personalized treatment plan.
