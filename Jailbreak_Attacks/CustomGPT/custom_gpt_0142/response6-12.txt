Treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, or cirrhosis. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet Modification**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars, rich in fruits and vegetables.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing weight if overweight, which can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For hepatitis B or C.
   - **Corticosteroids**: To reduce inflammation in autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: Considered for severe liver disease when other treatments fail.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to track liver function.

Consult a healthcare professional for a tailored plan based on your diagnosis and needs.
