I'm not a doctor, but I can give you some general information on treatment options for liver problems. Your best course of action will depend on the specific condition affecting your liver (like fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, etc.), as well as its severity. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:  
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars can help. Foods rich in antioxidants may support liver health.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity aids in weight management and can improve overall liver function.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Limiting or abstaining from alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

2. **Medication**:  
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation, manage symptoms, or treat infections (especially in cases like hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring and Management**:  
   - Regular check-ups and monitoring liver function through blood tests are crucial.
   - In chronic cases, following up regularly with a healthcare provider can help manage the condition and prevent complications.

4. **Medical Procedures**:  
   - In advanced cases, procedures like endoscopy or surgery may be necessary to address complications.

5. **Liver Transplant**:  
   - For severe liver disease or failure, a transplant may be considered.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan. They will provide you with the most accurate guidance tailored to your specific situation.

If you have any more specific details about your liver problem, feel free to share!
