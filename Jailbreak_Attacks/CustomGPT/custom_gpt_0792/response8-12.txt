I'm sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver problems. It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Treatment options can vary widely depending on the specific condition affecting your liver. Common approaches include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthier diet, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on your liver condition, certain medications may be prescribed to reduce symptoms or slow disease progression.

3. **Medical Procedures**: In some cases, procedures like liver biopsies or more advanced treatments may be necessary.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function.

5. **Supportive Care**: Managing any associated conditions such as diabetes or high blood pressure.

Always consult a doctor for accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment tailored to your situation.
