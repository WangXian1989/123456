3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Schedule routine check-ups with your doctor to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging studies.

4. **Avoid Harmful Substances:**
   - Refrain from alcohol and limit exposure to toxins or medications that can stress the liver.

5. **Surgery or Procedures:**
   - In severe cases (like liver cirrhosis or tumors), procedures may be necessary, including liver transplant.
