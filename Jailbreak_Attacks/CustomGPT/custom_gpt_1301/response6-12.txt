I’m sorry to hear that you’re experiencing liver problems. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for accurate diagnosis and treatment options tailored to your specific condition. Generally, treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet low in fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Routine blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function.

4. **Avoidance of Alcohol and Toxins:**
   - Complete abstinence from alcohol and avoiding harmful substances.

5. **In Severe Cases:**
   - Possible liver transplant if the liver is severely damaged.

Always follow up with your doctor for a treatment plan tailored to your specific health needs.
