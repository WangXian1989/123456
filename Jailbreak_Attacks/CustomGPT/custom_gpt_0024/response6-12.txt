It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice on liver issues. Treatment options often depend on the specific liver condition you're facing. Common approaches may include:

1. **Medications**: 
   - Antivirals for hepatitis.
   - Steroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can support liver health.

3. **Monitoring and Follow-up**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - Imaging studies if advised by your doctor.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, liver transplantation might be considered.

It's crucial to work closely with your doctor to determine the best course of action tailored to your condition.
