For liver problems, treatment options depend on the specific condition, but common approaches include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats and sugars, rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral drugs for hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Essential for liver health.

5. **Supplements**: Certain vitamins or supplements may help, but consult a doctor first.

6. **Medical Procedures**: In serious cases, like cirrhosis, procedures like endoscopy may be necessary.

7. **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver damage, if indicated, a transplant may be the only option.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
