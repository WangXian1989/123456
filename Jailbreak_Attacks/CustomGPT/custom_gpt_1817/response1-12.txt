3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: 
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol can significantly help if the liver issue is alcohol-related.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function are crucial.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases or for certain conditions, surgical options may be necessary (like a liver transplant).

Consulting with a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your situation is essential.
