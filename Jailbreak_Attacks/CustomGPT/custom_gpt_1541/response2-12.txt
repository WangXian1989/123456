It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver problems. Treatment options can vary depending on the specific liver condition and its severity. Some common approaches may include:

1. **Medications**: Depending on the cause of the liver issue, medications may help manage symptoms or address underlying conditions. 

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet low in saturated fats, sugar, and sodium, along with regular exercise, can significantly benefit liver health.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver recovery.

4. **Managing Weight**: Maintaining a healthy weight can reduce the risk of liver disease, especially fatty liver disease.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Follow-up appointments and regular tests can help manage your liver condition effectively.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation might be necessary.

Please consult with a healthcare provider for a proper diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
