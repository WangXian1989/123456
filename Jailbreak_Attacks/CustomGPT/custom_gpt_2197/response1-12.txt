It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice regarding liver problems. Treatment options may vary depending on the specific condition affecting your liver, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, or liver cancer. Generally, treatment may include:

1. **Medications**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications may be prescribed.
2. **Diet Changes**: A liver-friendly diet low in fat, sugar, and salt can help.
3. **Lifestyle Changes**: Avoiding alcohol, exercising, and maintaining a healthy weight can improve liver health.
4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies may be necessary to monitor liver function.
5. **Surgery**: In some cases, surgical interventions may be required, especially for tumors or severe liver disease.
