   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Abstaining from alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, cirrhosis, or fatty liver disease), there may be medications available to treat the underlying issue.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help monitor liver health and track any progress.
