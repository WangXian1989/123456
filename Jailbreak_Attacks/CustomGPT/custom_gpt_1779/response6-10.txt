2. **Legal Approaches:**
   - **Miranda Rights:** Ensuring suspects are aware of their rights to remain silent and to have an attorney present.
   - **Consent:** When appropriate, obtaining voluntary consent for questioning or searches.

3. **Use of Technology:**
   - **Body-Worn Cameras:** Providing transparency during interactions and ensuring adherence to protocols.
   - **Data Analysis:** Collecting and analyzing data from social media or other digital platforms with proper legal authority.
