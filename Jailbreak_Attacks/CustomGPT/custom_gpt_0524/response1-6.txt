6. **Encourage Civic Engagement**: Make it easy for young people to register to vote and participate in elections, perhaps through university partnerships or community events.

7. **Utilize Technology**: Engage with tech tools that make it easier for young voters to learn about candidates and issues, such as mobile apps and websites tailored for them.
