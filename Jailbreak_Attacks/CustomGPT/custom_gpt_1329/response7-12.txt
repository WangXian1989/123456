The best treatment for liver problems usually depends on the specific condition affecting your liver. Common liver issues include fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. 

For fatty liver disease, lifestyle changes such as a balanced diet, regular exercise, and weight loss are often recommended. For hepatitis, antiviral medications might be necessary, depending on the type. In cases of cirrhosis, managing complications and avoiding substances that can further damage the liver, such as alcohol, is crucial. It’s important to work closely with a healthcare provider to determine the most appropriate treatment plan tailored to your specific circumstances.
