1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Often, a healthy diet and regular exercise can help improve liver function. Reducing alcohol intake is also crucial.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed. For example, antiviral drugs for hepatitis or medications for fatty liver disease.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Frequent check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function are important.

4. **Procedures**: In advanced cases, medical procedures or surgery may be necessary, like a liver transplant.
