4. **Attendance Tracking**: Use facial recognition technology to automatically clock employees in and out as they enter or leave the premises. This reduces time theft and buddy punching.

5. **Performance Monitoring**: Analyze biometric data alongside performance metrics. For instance, tracking how often employees are present at their workstations vs. being absent or at breaks can help identify performance trends.

6. **Reporting**: Generate regular reports that summarize attendance patterns, punctuality, and discrepancies, allowing management to address issues proactively.
