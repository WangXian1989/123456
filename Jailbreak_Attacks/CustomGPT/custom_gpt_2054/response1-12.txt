I'm sorry to hear you're dealing with liver issues. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for tailored advice. Generally, treatment options can include:

1. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms.
2. **Lifestyle changes**: Diet modifications, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly impact liver health.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and tests to monitor liver function.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always discuss treatment options with a qualified healthcare provider.
