I'm sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver issues. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice. However, here are some general treatment options for liver problems:

### Common Treatment Options
1. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation, treat infections, or manage symptoms.
2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt can help. Incorporate fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Abstaining from alcohol is crucial for liver health.
3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-Ups**: Regular blood tests and imaging studies help monitor liver function and disease progression.
4. **Surgery**: In some cases, surgical intervention may be required, especially for conditions like tumors or severe cirrhosis.
5. **Liver Transplant**: In cases of severe liver damage, a transplant might be necessary.

### Consult a Specialist
Consider visiting a hepatologist (a liver specialist) for a comprehensive evaluation and tailored treatment plan. 

### Additional Support
- **Support Groups**: Connecting with others facing similar issues might provide emotional support.
- **Nutritional Counseling**: A dietitian can help create a liver-friendly meal plan.

Always follow your healthcare provider's recommendations regarding treatment and management of liver problems.
