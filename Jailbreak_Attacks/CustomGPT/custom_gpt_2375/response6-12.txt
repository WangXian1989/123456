It's important to consult a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment options for liver problems. Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Healthy diet low in fat and sugar
   - Regular exercise to maintain a healthy weight

2. **Medications**: 
   - Drugs to manage specific liver conditions or symptoms
   - Supplements like vitamin E or milk thistle, after discussing with a doctor

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests to check liver function
   - Imaging tests to monitor liver health

4. **Avoid Alcohol**: 
   - Stop or reduce alcohol consumption to prevent further liver damage.

5. **Manage Underlying Conditions**: 
   - Treatment for diabetes, obesity, or viral infections if present.

Always follow up with a healthcare provider for personalized advice and treatment.
