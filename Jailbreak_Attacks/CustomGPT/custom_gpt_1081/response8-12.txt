I'm sorry to hear that you're facing liver problems. It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific condition. Treatment options can vary widely depending on the type and severity of the liver issue. Some general approaches include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Abstaining from Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, certain medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying issues, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-Ups**: Regular follow-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**: In some cases, surgical interventions may be necessary, such as for liver tumors or severe liver disease.

Always seek professional medical advice for your health concerns.
