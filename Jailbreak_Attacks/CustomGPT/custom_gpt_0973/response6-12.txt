It's important to consult a healthcare professional to obtain an accurate diagnosis and treatment plan for liver issues. Treatment options may vary depending on the specific liver condition, but common approaches include:

1. **Medication:**
   - Antiviral medications for viral hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

2. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - Adopting a balanced diet and maintaining a healthy weight.
   - Avoiding alcohol and harmful substances.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Routine blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery:**
   - In cases of liver tumors or severe liver damage, surgery may be necessary.

Always seek professional medical advice for personalized recommendations based on your health needs.
