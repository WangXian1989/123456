   - Routine check-ups and blood tests can help track liver function and detect changes early.

4. **Avoid Alcohol**:
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake can prevent further liver damage.

5. **Vaccination**:
   - Vaccines for hepatitis A and B can help prevent related liver infections.

6. **Manage Comorbidities**:
   - Control conditions like diabetes, high cholesterol, and high blood pressure to support liver health.
