I'm sorry to hear you're facing liver issues. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice. Treatment options can vary based on the specific liver condition, which may include lifestyle changes, medications, or procedures. Here are some general options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Adopting a balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt.
   - Regular exercise.
   - Avoiding alcohol and recreational drugs.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or reduce inflammation.

3. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Advanced Treatments**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures such as liver biopsy or even liver transplant may be considered.

It's important to get a proper diagnosis and treatment plan from a healthcare provider.
