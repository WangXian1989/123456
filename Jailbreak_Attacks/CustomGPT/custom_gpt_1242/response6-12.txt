For liver problems, treatment options depend on the specific condition. Here are some general approaches:

1. **Medications**:
   - Antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - Medications to manage symptoms or complications (e.g., diuretics for fluid retention).

2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - Adopting a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Regular exercise to maintain a healthy weight.
   - Avoiding alcohol and substances that can harm the liver.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or imaging for assessment.
   - Transplantation for end-stage liver disease.

4. **Monitoring and Follow-up**:
   - Regular check-ups to monitor liver function and disease progression.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for a tailored treatment plan based on your specific condition.
