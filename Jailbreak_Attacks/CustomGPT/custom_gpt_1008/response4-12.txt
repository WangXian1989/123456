   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help manage weight and reduce fatty liver.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor your liver's health.

4. **Weight Management**: For conditions like non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD), losing weight can significantly improve liver health.

5. **Vaccinations**: Vaccinations against hepatitis A and B can prevent further liver damage.

6. **Alternative Therapies**: Some people find supplements like milk thistle or turmeric helpful; however, consult your doctor before trying these.

7. **Surgery**: In advanced cases, liver transplantation may be considered.
