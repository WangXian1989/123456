3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests to monitor liver function may be necessary to track the progression of the disease.

4. **Supplements**: Some people find that certain supplements, like milk thistle or vitamin E, may support liver health, although you should consult a healthcare provider before taking any.
