I'm sorry to hear about your liver problem. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for advice tailored to your specific condition. They can recommend the best treatment options based on your medical history and current health status. 

In general, treatment options for liver issues can include lifestyle changes, medications, and in some cases, surgical interventions. Key strategies typically involve:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Maintaining a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can greatly impact liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups can help keep track of liver health and identify any complications early on.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, liver transplant or other surgical options may be considered.

Always seek personalized medical advice before making any decisions regarding your health.
