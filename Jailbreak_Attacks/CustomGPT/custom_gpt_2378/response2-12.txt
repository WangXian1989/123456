I'm not a doctor, but there are several treatment options you can consider for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopting a healthy diet low in fats and high in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular doctor visits and tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Management of Underlying Conditions**: 
   - Treatment for conditions like diabetes, high cholesterol, or hepatitis.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, options like liver transplantation may be considered.
