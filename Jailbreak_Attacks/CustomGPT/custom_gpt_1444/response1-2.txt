   - **Data Security**: Ensure robust data protection measures to prevent unauthorized access to sensitive biometric information.

### **Pros and Cons**
- **Pros**: Increased accuracy in attendance tracking, reduced time fraud, and improved security.
- **Cons**: Privacy concerns, potential bias in recognition algorithms, and the high cost of implementation.
