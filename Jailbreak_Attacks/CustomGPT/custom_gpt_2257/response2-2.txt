- **Install Cameras/Scanners**: Set up cameras or biometric scanners at entry points or workspaces to capture employee data.

- **Performance Management Software**: Link biometric data to HR systems to create comprehensive employee profiles that include attendance and performance metrics.

- **Educate Employees**: Make sure employees understand how the system works and the benefits of tracking performance and attendance using biometric data.

### 8. Maintain Security and Compliance
- **Data Encryption**: Ensure that all biometric data is stored securely with appropriate encryption methods.

- **Transparency**: Be open with employees about how their biometric data will be used.
