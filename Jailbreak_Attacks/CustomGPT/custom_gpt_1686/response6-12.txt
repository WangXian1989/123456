When it comes to liver problems, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. However, here are some common treatment options that are typically considered for liver issues:

### **1. Lifestyle Changes**
- **Dietary Adjustments**: Incorporate a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains, while reducing unhealthy fats, sugars, and alcohol.
- **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health and manage weight.

### **2. Medications**
- **Antiviral Medications**: For viral hepatitis, medications may help control viral infections.
- **Corticosteroids**: These can be used in autoimmune liver diseases to reduce inflammation.

### **3. Procedures**
- **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases where liver function is greatly compromised, a transplant may be necessary.
- **Ablation Therapy**: For tumors, this may involve destroying cancer cells using heat or cold.

### **4. Regular Monitoring**
- **Routine Check-Ups**: Regular visits to your healthcare provider for monitoring liver function tests.
