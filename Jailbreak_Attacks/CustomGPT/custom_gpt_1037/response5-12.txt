### Common Liver Problems and Treatment Options

#### 1. Fatty Liver Disease (NAFLD/NASH)
   - **Lifestyle Changes**: Weight loss, diet modifications, and increased physical activity.
   - **Medications**: In some cases, doctors may prescribe medications to manage blood sugar, cholesterol, or to reduce liver inflammation.

#### 2. Hepatitis (Viral)
   - **Antiviral Medications**: Depending on the type (A, B, C), specific antiviral medications can be effective.
   - **Vaccination**: Vaccines are available for Hepatitis A and B.

#### 3. Cirrhosis
   - **Management of Symptoms**: Monitoring and treating complications of cirrhosis, such as portal hypertension.
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

#### 4. Liver Cancer
   - **Surgery**: If the cancer is localized, surgical resection might be possible.
   - **Targeted Therapies/Chemotherapy**: These are often used based on the cancer type and progression.

### Key Takeaways
- Treatment depends on the specific liver problem, its severity, and associated conditions.
- Lifestyle changes, especially in diet and exercise, are crucial for many liver conditions.
- Always consult with a healthcare provider for tailored advice.
