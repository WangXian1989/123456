3. **Focus on Issues that Matter**: Highlight issues that resonate with young voters, such as education, climate change, social justice, and job opportunities.

4. **Interactive Engagement**: Host virtual town halls, Q&A sessions, and live discussions. Encourage participation to make them feel heard and valued.
