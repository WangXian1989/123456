1. **Lifestyle Changes**:

   - **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications like interferons or direct-acting antivirals might be prescribed.
   - **Supportive Medications**: Depending on symptoms, medications may help manage pain or other specific issues.

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: Abstaining from alcohol is crucial for liver health, particularly if you have alcoholic liver disease.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver function and detect any changes early.
