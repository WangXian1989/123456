For liver problems, treatment depends on the specific condition. Here are some common options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eat a balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Diuretics**: To reduce fluid retention.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Endoscopy**: To manage bleeding esophageal varices.
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases or end-stage liver disease.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Blood Tests**: To monitor liver function.
   - **Imaging Studies**: Such as ultrasounds to assess liver health.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
