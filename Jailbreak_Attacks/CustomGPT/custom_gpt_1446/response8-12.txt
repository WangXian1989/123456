I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Generally, treatment options for liver issues can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Such as adopting a healthy diet, reducing alcohol intake, and maintaining a healthy weight.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular visits to a healthcare provider can help monitor liver function and assess treatment effectiveness.
