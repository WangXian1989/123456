2. **Medications**: Depending on the underlying cause (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat infections.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging can help monitor liver function and detect any changes early.
