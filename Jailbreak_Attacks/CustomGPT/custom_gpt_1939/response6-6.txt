**Targeting Young Voters: Strategies for Engagement**

- **Use Social Media:** Engage through platforms like TikTok and Instagram with creative, shareable content. Collaborate with influencers who resonate with youth interests.
- **Focus on Relevant Issues:** Address topics that matter to young people, such as climate change, education debt, and job opportunities. Tailor messaging to highlight how policies affect their lives directly.

### Objective: Boost Engagement and Mobilization 
Implementing these strategies can create a resonance with young voters, encouraging them to participate in elections and advocacy initiatives.
