### Treatment Options for Liver Problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.
   - **Weight Management:** Maintaining a healthy weight can reduce liver inflammation.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals:** For viral hepatitis, antiviral drugs can help manage the infection.
   - **Anti-inflammatory Drugs:** Corticosteroids may be used for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Supplements:**
   - Certain supplements like milk thistle may support liver health, but consult your doctor first.

4. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function.

5. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always discuss these options with your healthcare provider to find the best approach for your specific condition.
