   - Roth accounts are taxed now but grow tax-free.
   - Taxable accounts incur taxes on earnings and may have capital gains implications.

4. **Optimal Distribution Strategy**
   - **Maximize Employer Match**: Contribute enough to your 401(k) to receive the full match.

6. **Ongoing Assessment**
   - Regularly review asset allocation and account performance.
   - Adjust contributions based on changes in income, expenses, and financial goals.
