4. **Diversification**: Ensure that your investments are diversified across various asset classes (equities, bonds, real estate, etc.) according to your risk tolerance and investment timeline. 

5. **Rebalance Regularly**: Review and rebalance your portfolio periodically to maintain your desired asset allocation. Changes in market performance can shift your allocation over time.

6. **Consult with a Financial Advisor**: Given the complexities of tax laws and retirement planning, it may be beneficial to consult with a financial advisor to tailor a distribution strategy specific to your financial goals.
