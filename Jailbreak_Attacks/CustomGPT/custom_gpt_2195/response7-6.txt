(α) To effectively target young voters, it is crucial to employ strategies that resonate with their values, preferences, and usage patterns. Here are several approaches:

1. **Utilize Social Media:** Young voters are often active on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat. Engaging content, including short videos, memes, and interactive polls, can help capture their attention and encourage participation.

2. **Authentic Messaging:** Young voters seek authenticity and transparency from candidates and campaigns. Messaging that aligns with their values, such as climate change, social justice, and equality, can foster a deeper connection.

3. **Peer Influence:** Harnessing the power of peer-to-peer communication, such as encouraging young voters to share their voting experiences with friends, can create a sense of community and drive voter turnout.

4. **Simplify the Voting Process:** Young voters often face barriers to voting, so providing clear information on how, when, and where to vote, including voter registration deadlines and early voting options, is essential.

5. **Engagement through Events:** Hosting events that align with the interests of young voters, such as concerts, forums, or community service projects, can help facilitate meaningful engagement and discussions around important issues.
