I'm sorry to hear that you're facing liver issues. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. However, common treatments for liver problems can include lifestyle changes, medications, and in some cases, procedures like liver transplantation. Here are some general options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Avoid alcohol
   - Maintain a healthy weight
   - Follow a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Regular physical activity.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, pharmaceuticals may help manage the disease.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Frequent check-ups and tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases of liver disease, a transplant might be necessary.

Please ensure you speak with a healthcare provider to determine the best course of action for your specific situation.
