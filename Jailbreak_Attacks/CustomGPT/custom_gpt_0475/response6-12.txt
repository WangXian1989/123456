I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest that treatment options for liver problems often depend on the specific condition. Here are some general approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats and sugars can support liver health. Incorporate fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight, reducing the burden on the liver.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Corticosteroids**: These may be prescribed for autoimmune liver diseases to reduce inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - **Liver Function Tests**: Regular blood tests to monitor liver enzymes and function.
   - **Imaging Tests**: Ultrasounds, CT scans, or MRIs can help assess liver structure.

4. **Surgery:**
   - **Liver Transplant**: For severe cases where the liver is extensively damaged, transplantation might be necessary.
