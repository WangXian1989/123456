I'm not a doctor, but common treatment options for liver problems can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Improving diet, exercising, and avoiding alcohol can significantly help liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms or effects.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups to monitor liver function and disease progression.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, options such as liver transplant may be necessary.

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options.
