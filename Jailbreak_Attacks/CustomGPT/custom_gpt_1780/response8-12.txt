I'm sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver issues. It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options tailored to your specific condition. Generally, treatment options for liver problems vary depending on the underlying cause, but they may include lifestyle changes, medications, and in some cases, surgery. 

Here are some common treatments:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.
2. **Medications**: There are medications to treat specific liver diseases, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.
3. **Therapeutic Procedures**: In certain cases, procedures might be needed to remove blockages or perform biopsies.
4. **Liver Transplant**: For severe conditions, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Make sure to discuss all your options with your doctor, who can provide the best guidance based on your medical history.
