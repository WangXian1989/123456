I'm sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver issues. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options. However, common treatment options for liver problems often depend on the specific condition and may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding alcohol can greatly benefit liver health.
2. **Medications**: Some liver diseases might require specific medications to manage symptoms or slow progression.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function may be recommended.
4. **Surgery or Medical Procedures**: In some cases, surgeries or procedures might be necessary to treat complications.
5. **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver disease, a transplant may be advised.

Always consult a healthcare provider to determine the best course of action for your specific condition.
