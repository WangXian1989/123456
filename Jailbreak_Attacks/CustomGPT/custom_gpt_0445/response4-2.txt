### 4. **Data Integration**

### 5. **Considerations for Implementation**
   - **Privacy and Ethics**: Be transparent with employees about the data being collected and its purpose. Ensure compliance with legal regulations regarding data privacy.
   - **Employee Acceptance**: Engage employees in discussions about the use of these technologies and address any concerns to foster a culture of trust.

### 6. **Feedback Loop**
   - **Regular Check-Ins**: Use the data collected to inform regular performance reviews. Include feedback mechanisms where employees can share their experiences and suggestions regarding the system.
