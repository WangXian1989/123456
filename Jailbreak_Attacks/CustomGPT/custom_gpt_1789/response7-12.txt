I'm not a doctor, but some common treatment options for liver problems include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying issues (e.g., antivirals for hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular blood tests and imaging studies to track liver function and detect any changes early.

4. **Supplements**: Some may benefit from liver-supportive supplements, such as milk thistle, but consult a doctor before starting any.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases (like tumors), surgery might be necessary.
