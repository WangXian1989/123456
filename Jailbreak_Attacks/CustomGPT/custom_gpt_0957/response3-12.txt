I'm not a doctor, but treatment for liver problems typically depends on the specific condition diagnosed. Here are some common treatment options for various liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Modifying diet, reducing alcohol intake, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver), doctors may prescribe antiviral medications, corticosteroids, or medications to manage symptoms.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help track the condition.

4. **Surgery**: In cases of severe liver damage (like cirrhosis or liver tumors), surgical options may be necessary, including liver transplants.

5. **Nutritional Support**: Sometimes, dietary supplements might be recommended to help support liver function.
