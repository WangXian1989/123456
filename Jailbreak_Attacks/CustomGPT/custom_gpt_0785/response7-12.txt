I'm really sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver issues. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice, as they can evaluate your specific situation and recommend the most appropriate treatment options based on your condition.

General treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: A healthier diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the underlying cause (like hepatitis, fatty liver, etc.), medications may be prescribed to help manage your condition.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**: Regular liver function tests and imaging studies can help track the progress of your liver health.

4. **Nutritional Support**: A dietitian can help create a tailored diet plan that supports liver health.

5. **Specialized Treatments**: In cases of severe liver disease, treatments like antiviral therapies for hepatitis or even liver transplantation may be necessary.
