2. **Medications:** Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes. For example, antiviral medications for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

4. **Regular Monitoring:** Regular check-ups and liver function tests are essential to monitor the condition's progression and adjust treatments as needed.

5. **Nutritional Support:** Working with a nutritionist can help in designing a diet that supports liver health.
