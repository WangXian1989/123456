1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet Adjustments:** A balanced diet, rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while limiting saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance:** Avoiding alcohol or significantly reducing intake can help prevent further liver damage.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antiviral Medications:** For liver diseases caused by viral infections, such as hepatitis.
   - **Anti-inflammatory Drugs:** Depending on the diagnosis, anti-inflammatory medications may be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - **Routine Blood Tests:** Monitoring liver function through regular blood tests to assess enzymes and other indicators.
   - **Imaging Tests:** Regular imaging to evaluate liver condition and detect any complications early.
