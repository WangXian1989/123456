Treatment options often depend on the specific liver condition, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, or liver cancer.

Common liver treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:** Adjusting diet (reducing alcohol intake, eating a balanced diet), maintaining a healthy weight, and regular exercise can significantly improve liver health.
   
2. **Medications:** Depending on the underlying cause, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat specific liver conditions (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Surgery:** In cases of liver cancer or severe liver disease, surgical options may include liver resection or liver transplantation.

4. **Regular Monitoring:** Regular check-ups and imaging studies to monitor liver function and disease progression.

Always consult your doctor for a proper diagnosis and tailored treatment plan that suits your specific needs.
