I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest that you consult a healthcare professional for proper advice tailored to your specific condition. Treatment options for liver problems can vary widely depending on the underlying issue, such as:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - Reduce alcohol consumption.
   - Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.

2. **Medications**:
   - Antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis.
   - Medications to manage symptoms or complications related to liver disease.

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

It's crucial to seek advice from a healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and effective treatment plan.
