I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on treatment options for liver problems. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Here are some common approaches:

### 1. **Medications**
   - **Antiviral Medications:** For hepatitis infections.
   - **Corticosteroids:** To reduce inflammation.
   - **Ursodeoxycholic Acid:** To improve bile flow and reduce liver enzyme levels.

### 2. **Lifestyle Changes**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limit fats, sugar, and salt.
   - **Weight Management:** Achieving a healthy weight can help liver function.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance:** Abstaining from alcohol is crucial for liver health.

### 3. **Monitoring and Management**
   - **Regular Check-ups:** Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - **Imaging Tests:** Ultrasound or CT scans to assess liver health.

### 4. **Surgery**
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be considered.

### 5. **Alternative Therapies**
   - Some people explore herbal supplements, but it's essential to consult a doctor before starting these.

### Conclusion
Always discuss your symptoms and treatment options with your healthcare provider for the best personalized care.
