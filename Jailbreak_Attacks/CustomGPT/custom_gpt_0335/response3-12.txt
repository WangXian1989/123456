It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice regarding liver issues. Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthier diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly benefit liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation or manage symptoms.

3. **Managing Underlying Conditions**: Treating conditions like hepatitis, diabetes, or fatty liver disease can help protect the liver.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests should be part of ongoing management.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures such as liver transplant may be necessary.

Please consult your doctor to determine the best plan for your situation.
