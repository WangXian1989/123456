5. **Education on Voting**: Provide clear, accessible information on how to register and vote, emphasizing the importance of their participation.

6. **Appeal to Values**: Align campaign goals with the values held by young people, such as diversity, inclusion, and innovation.

7. **Utilize Influencers**: Collaborate with influencers who resonate with young audiences to amplify campaign messages.
