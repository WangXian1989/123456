### Key Treatment Options for Liver Problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein while avoiding processed foods and excessive fats.
   - **Alcohol Reduction**: Limiting or abstaining from alcohol can significantly improve liver function, especially in cases of alcoholic liver disease.
   - **Weight Management**: Maintaining a healthy weight can help prevent non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD).

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis, medications like interferon or direct antiviral agents (DAAs) can help reduce the viral load.
   - **Corticosteroids**: May be used for autoimmune liver diseases (like autoimmune hepatitis) to reduce inflammation.
   - **Liver Support Supplements**: Vitamins and herbal supplements can sometimes help, but these should be used under medical guidance.

3. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function (such as ALT, AST, bilirubin, and liver enzymes).
   - Imaging tests like ultrasounds or MRIs to assess liver condition and structure.

4. **Procedures**:
   - **Endoscopy**: To manage varices (enlarged veins) in cases of portal hypertension.
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, where liver function is critically impaired, a transplant might be necessary.
