1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly impact liver health.
   
2. **Medications**: For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications may be prescribed. Supplements might also be suggested depending on the diagnosis.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests are crucial for managing chronic liver diseases.

4. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a transplant may be necessary.
