1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A healthy diet low in fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Alcohol Reduction**: Limiting or abstaining from alcohol.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids**: For autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Diuretics**: To reduce fluid buildup.

3. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - Regular check-ups to monitor liver function.
   - Ultrasound or other imaging tests.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
