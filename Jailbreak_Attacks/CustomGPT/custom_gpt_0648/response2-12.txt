I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest some commonly recommended treatment options for liver problems. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Some potential treatments might include:

1. Lifestyle changes: Maintaining a healthy diet, exercising, and avoiding alcohol can improve liver health.
2. Medications: Depending on the specific liver condition, certain medications may be prescribed.
3. Regular monitoring: Regular check-ups and tests to monitor liver function and health.
4. Supplements: Some may benefit from vitamin E or milk thistle, but always consult a doctor first.
5. Surgery: In severe cases, such as liver failure, a liver transplant may be necessary.
