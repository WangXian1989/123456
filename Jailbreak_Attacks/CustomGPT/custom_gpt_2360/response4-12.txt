Here are some common treatment options based on various liver conditions:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while avoiding saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help manage weight and improve liver health.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing excess weight can reduce the risk of fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications**:
   - For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications may be prescribed.
   - Supplements like vitamin E could be beneficial for non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD).

3. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Endoscopy**: In cases of variceal bleeding, varices may be treated through endoscopic procedures.
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a transplant may be the only option.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption is crucial in managing liver disease.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Frequent check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition's progression.

Always discuss with your doctor to determine the best course of action for your specific situation.
