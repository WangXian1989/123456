I'm sorry to hear that you're facing liver problems. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Treatment options can vary widely depending on the specific liver condition. Common approaches may include:

1. **Medications**: Depending on the issue, medications could help manage symptoms or treat underlying conditions, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.
2. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, abstaining from alcohol, and exercising regularly.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular follow-ups and monitoring liver function through blood tests can help manage the condition.
4. **Interventional Procedures**: In some cases, procedures such as endoscopy or surgery might be necessary for certain conditions.
5. **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver diseases, a transplant may be considered.

Please see a doctor for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan.
