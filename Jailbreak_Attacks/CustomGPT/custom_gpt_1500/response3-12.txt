I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Specific medications may be prescribed depending on the liver condition, such as antivirals for hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Frequent check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Nutritional Supplements**: 
   - Some may benefit from vitamins or supplements, but consult your doctor first.

5. **Procedures**: 
   - In some cases, procedures like liver biopsy may be needed for diagnosis or treatment.

6. **Liver Transplant**: 
   - For severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
