I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral drugs** for viral hepatitis.
   - **Steroids** or other medications for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
