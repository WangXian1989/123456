To effectively target young voters, consider these strategies:

1. **Utilize Digital Media**:
   - **Social Media Campaigns**: Engage on platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat, utilizing influencers to convey messages creatively.
   - **Interactive Content**: Develop polls, quizzes, and live Q&A sessions to encourage participation and enhance engagement.

2. **Focus on Relevant Issues**:
   - **Highlight Policies**: Address young people's primary concerns, such as climate change, education reform, and student debt.
   - **Local Engagement**: Host events that focus on community issues, demonstrating how your platform aligns with their values.

By aligning strategies with their interests and habits, you can effectively mobilize young voters.
