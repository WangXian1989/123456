Common liver issues include fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Here are some general treatment options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Diet and exercise can significantly impact liver health. A balanced diet low in sugars and saturated fats, along with regular exercise, can help.

2. **Medication**: Depending on the condition, doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or treat the underlying issue, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption is crucial for those with liver issues, as alcohol can exacerbate damage.

4. **Manage Other Health Conditions**: Controlling conditions like diabetes, high cholesterol, and obesity can help protect liver health.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, such as end-stage liver disease, a transplant may be necessary.

6. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider can help monitor liver function and progression of disease.
