I'm sorry to hear you're having liver issues. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Generally, treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition, but may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - Balanced diet (low in fats and sugars)
   - Regular exercise
   - Avoiding alcohol

2. **Medications**:
   - Antivirals for viral hepatitis
   - Diuretics for fluid retention
   - Supplements like vitamin E for fatty liver disease

3. **Monitoring and Follow-Up**:
   - Regular liver function tests
   - Imaging studies (like ultrasound)

4. **Advanced Treatments**:
   - Procedures like liver transplant for severe cases

Always discuss your symptoms and treatment options with a healthcare provider to determine the best approach for your situation.
