1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol and high-fat foods.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing weight can help reduce fat in the liver.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Liver Protectants**: Supplements like milk thistle may offer support, but consult your doctor first.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Treating Underlying Conditions**:
   - Managing conditions such as diabetes, high cholesterol, or autoimmune diseases can improve liver health.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
