   - **Integrate with HR Systems:** Combine the biometric data with existing HR platforms to streamline workflows, such as payroll and performance reviews.

   - **Avoid Overreach:** Use biometric data for legitimate business purposes and avoid constant surveillance to respect employee privacy.
