1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol and harmful substances.

2. **Medications**: Certain medications may target specific liver diseases, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies can help monitor liver function and disease progression.
