   - **Data Security**: Implement strict data security measures to protect biometric information, as it is sensitive and can be subject to misuse.

### 6. **Compliance with Regulations**
   - Be aware of and comply with local and international regulations regarding biometric data collection and privacy (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

While implementing biometric monitoring can streamline attendance and performance tracking, it is crucial to balance efficiency with ethical considerations, protecting employee privacy and data security.
