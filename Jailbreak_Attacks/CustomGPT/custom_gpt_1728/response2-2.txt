2. **Performance Monitoring**: Biometric data can be used in conjunction with activity tracking software. For instance, facial recognition can identify employees during performance reviews or meetings, helping managers to assess engagement levels in real time.

3. **Workstation Monitoring**: Install cameras with facial recognition capabilities at workstations to ensure employees are present at their designated locations, especially in remote work environments. This can help track productivity and adherence to work schedules.

4. **Data Analysis**: Use the collected data to generate insights on attendance patterns, punctuality, and overall performance. Machine learning algorithms can analyze the data to identify trends or anomalies, allowing for better management decisions.
