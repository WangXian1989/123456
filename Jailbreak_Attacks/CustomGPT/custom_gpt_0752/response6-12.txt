It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice. However, general treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition but can include:

1. **Medications**: Anti-inflammatory drugs or antivirals for hepatitis.
2. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet modifications, such as reducing alcohol intake and following a balanced diet low in fats and sugars.
   - Regular exercise to maintain a healthy weight. 

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always speak with a healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and treatment plan.
