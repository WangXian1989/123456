2. **Investment Goals**: Align your contributions based on your goals, such as retirement, short-term savings, or long-term growth.

3. **Diversification**: Consider spreading investments across different asset classes (stocks, bonds, etc.) to manage risk.

4. **Contribution Limits**: Be aware of the contribution limits for each account type and allocate accordingly.
