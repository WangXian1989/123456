To effectively target young voters, consider these strategies:

1. **Utilize Social Media Platforms**:
   - Engage on platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat, where young people are most active.
   - Create relatable content, including memes and short videos that resonate with their values.

2. **Focus on Issues They Care About**:
   - Highlight topics such as climate change, student debt, and social justice.
   - Use real stories and statistics to connect emotionally and demonstrate the impact of these issues.

3. **Promote Easy Engagement**:
   - Make registration and voting processes straightforward through clear, engaging campaigns.
   - Offer incentives for participation, like events or discounts for showing proof of voting.

4. **Collaborate with Influencers**:
   - Partner with influencers who can authentically promote your message.
   - Ensure that the influencers align with the values and interests of young voters. 

By combining these tactics, you can effectively engage and mobilize young voters.
