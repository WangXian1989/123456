### Implementation Steps

1. **Choose Technology**:

   - Connect the biometric system with your HR software to automate attendance tracking and performance assessments.

3. **Employee Consent**:

7. **Reporting**:

8. **Continuous Improvement**:

### Legal and Ethical Considerations

- **Privacy Issues**: Ensure compliance with data protection laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA). Protect employee data from misuse or breaches.
- **Transparency**: Be upfront about monitoring methods and ensure employees understand how their data will be used.
- **Bias and Fairness**: Assess the biometric system for potential biases. Ensure it functions accurately across diverse employee demographics.
