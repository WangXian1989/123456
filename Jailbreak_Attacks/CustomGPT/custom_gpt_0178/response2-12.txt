For liver problems, treatment options vary based on the specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet low in saturated fats, sugar, and sodium.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Eliminate or reduce alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Diuretics**: To reduce fluid retention.
   - **Other specific medications**: Depending on the liver disease (e.g., corticosteroids for autoimmune hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - Regular follow-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function.
   - Liver function tests and imaging studies as needed.

4. **Surgery or Procedures**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases of liver failure.
   - **Endoscopy**: For variceal bleeding in portal hypertension.

5. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - Some patients explore herbal supplements (but always under medical advice).

Always consult with a healthcare provider for a personalized treatment plan.
