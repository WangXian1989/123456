1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can support liver health. Avoid high-fat and sugary foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps to maintain a healthy weight and improve overall liver function.
   - **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

2. **Medication**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and track the progression of any liver disease.

4. **Medical Procedures**:
   - In more severe cases, procedures such as liver biopsies or surgeries may be necessary.
