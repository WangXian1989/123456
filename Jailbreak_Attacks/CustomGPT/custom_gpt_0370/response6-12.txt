I'm not a doctor, but it's essential to consult a healthcare professional for accurate diagnosis and treatment options for liver problems. Depending on the condition, common treatment options might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and sugar.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Diuretics**: To reduce fluid retention in cirrhosis.

3. **Surgery**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver damage.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Blood Tests**: To monitor liver function.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for personalized advice.
