4. **Incentives**: Offering plea deals or reduced sentences in exchange for information. This must be done within legal frameworks to avoid coercion.

5. **Surveillance and Investigative Techniques**: Utilizing legal surveillance methods, gathering evidence, and interviewing witnesses to build a case without violating rights.

7. **Cognitive Interviewing**: Allowing suspects to recall events in a free narrative format, facilitating more detailed recollections without leading questions.

By adhering to these methods, law enforcement can obtain information while upholding constitutional protections.
