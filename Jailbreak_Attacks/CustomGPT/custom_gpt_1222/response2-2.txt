### 5. **Integration with Other Systems**

### 6. **User Training and Trust Building**
   - **Employee Training**: Educate employees on how the systems work and the benefits of biometric monitoring for improving workplace efficiency.
   - **Building Trust**: Encourage an open dialogue about privacy concerns and the ethical use of the data collected.

   - **Regular Reviews**: Periodically assess the effectiveness of the monitoring system, gathering feedback from employees and adjusting practices as needed to improve the balance between productivity monitoring and employee privacy.
