It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice on liver problems, as treatment options depend on the specific condition. Common approaches include:

1. **Medication**:
   - Antiviral drugs for hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver disease.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - Follow a healthy, balanced diet.
   - Avoid alcohol and toxins.
   - Maintain a healthy weight.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Frequent check-ups to monitor liver function.
   - Imaging tests to assess liver condition.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, consider a liver transplant.

Always seek guidance from a doctor for the best course of action tailored to your situation.
