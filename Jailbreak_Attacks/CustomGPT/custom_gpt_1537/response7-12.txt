Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications like antiviral drugs for hepatitis, corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases, or diuretics for fluid retention may be used.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: A healthy diet, regular exercise, weight management, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver function.

3. **Monitoring and Testing**: Regular liver function tests and imaging studies might be necessary to monitor the condition's progress.

4. **Surgery or Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be considered.

5. **Supplements**: Some people use supplements like milk thistle, but you should discuss this with your doctor first.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
