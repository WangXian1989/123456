   - **Compliance**: Ensure adherence to local laws regarding data protection and privacy. Obtain employee consent where necessary.

### 5. **Use Cases**

   - **Performance Analysis**: Use attendance data to identify patterns (e.g., frequent absences) that may correlate with lower performance.

### 6. **Training and Adaptation**
   - **Staff Training**: Educate employees on how to use biometric systems and the benefits they bring.
   - **Culture Building**: Foster an environment where employees see these tools as beneficial rather than invasive.
