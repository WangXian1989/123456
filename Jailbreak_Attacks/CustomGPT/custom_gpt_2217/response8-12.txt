It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver problems. However, treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Modifications**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the condition, medications might help manage symptoms or treat underlying causes (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Supplements**: 
   - Some may benefit from specific vitamins or antioxidants, but always consult a doctor first.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function.

5. **Surgical Options**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always prioritize guidance from a healthcare professional tailored to your specific situation.
