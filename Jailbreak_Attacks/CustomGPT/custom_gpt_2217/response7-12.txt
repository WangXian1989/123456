1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet low in fats and sugars can help. Incorporate plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver function and aid in weight management.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Medication for Fatty Liver**: Some medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms and improve liver health.

3. **Limit Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake can prevent further liver damage.

4. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**: Regular appointments with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging as needed.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or cirrhosis, surgical options or a liver transplant may be considered.
