3. **Avoid Alcohol and Toxins**: 
   - Eliminating alcohol and avoiding environmental toxins is crucial for protecting liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver function and progression of liver disease.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, surgical interventions, including liver transplant, may be necessary.
