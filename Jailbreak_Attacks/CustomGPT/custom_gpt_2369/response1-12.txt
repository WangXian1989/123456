1. **Lifestyle Changes**:

4. **Avoid Alcohol**:
   - Abstaining from alcohol is crucial for liver recovery.

5. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

6. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary.

Always prioritize seeing a doctor for a definitive diagnosis and treatment plan!
