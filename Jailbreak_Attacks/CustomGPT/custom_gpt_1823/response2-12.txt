1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and processed foods can help support liver health. Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver function and overall health.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Limiting or eliminating alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), your doctor may prescribe medications to treat inflammation or improve liver function.

3. **Monitoring and Management**: 
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help monitor your condition. Your doctor may recommend imaging tests or liver biopsies for evaluation.

4. **Interventional Procedures**: 
   - For some conditions, treatments like endoscopy or surgical procedures may be necessary to address blockages or remove lesions.

5. **Supportive Therapies**: 
   - Programs for those with liver disease may include counseling, education about liver health, and support groups.

It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
