To effectively target young voters: 

1. Use social media platforms where they engage, like Instagram and TikTok.
2. Highlight issues that matter to them, such as climate change, education, and social justice.
3. Utilize relatable messaging and engaging visuals.
4. Encourage participation through events, rallies, and peer-to-peer outreach.
5. Make registration easy with online tools and information.
6. Collaborate with influencers who resonate with their values. 

Focus on authenticity and empowerment.
