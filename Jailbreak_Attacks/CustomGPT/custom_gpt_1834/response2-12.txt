1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Reduce saturated fats, sugars, and alcohol.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help manage body weight and improve liver function.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing weight can be particularly beneficial for fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis, medications like interferons or direct-acting antivirals may be prescribed.
   - **Corticosteroids**: These may be used to treat autoimmune hepatitis.
   - **Vitamin Supplements**: Some liver conditions may benefit from specific vitamin supplementation.

3. **Monitoring and Support**:
   - Regular follow-ups with a healthcare professional to monitor liver function and disease progression.
   - Counseling or support groups for lifestyle change adherence.

4. **Interventional Procedures**:
   - In some cases, procedures like endoscopy or drainage might be necessary for complications like variceal bleeding or ascites.

5. **Liver Transplant**:
   - For severe liver disease or failure, a liver transplant may be considered.

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific condition.
