1. **Medications**: Depending on your condition, medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation, manage symptoms, or treat underlying causes (like viral hepatitis).

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthier diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

3. **Monitoring and Management**: Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function and manage any developing issues.

4. **Surgery**: In some severe cases, surgical options, such as liver transplants, may be necessary.

5. **Avoiding Toxins**: Steer clear of substances that can exacerbate liver problems, like certain medications and pollutants.
