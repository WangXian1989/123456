1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limit alcohol, sugary foods, and saturated fats.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the diagnosis, medications may be offered to manage symptoms or complications, such as antivirals for hepatitis or drugs to reduce liver inflammation.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**:
   - Regular follow-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and assess disease progression.

4. **Advanced Treatments**:
   - In severe cases, treatments such as liver transplant or specialized procedures for conditions like cirrhosis may be necessary.
