I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice. In general, treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet modification: Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains.
   - Limiting alcohol intake: Reducing or eliminating alcohol can help prevent further liver damage.
   - Weight management: Achieving and maintaining a healthy weight can reduce stress on the liver.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or address the underlying cause (e.g., antiviral medications for hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests to monitor liver function and assess the effectiveness of treatment.

4. **Avoiding Toxins**: 
   - Staying away from substances that can cause liver damage, such as certain medications, chemicals, and illicit drugs.

5. **Supportive Therapies**: 
   - Depending on the condition, therapies such as physical therapy or counseling may be beneficial.

6. **Surgery or Procedures**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures such as a liver transplant may be considered.

Always consult with a healthcare provider to determine the best course of action tailored to your specific situation. If you have further questions or need more detailed information, feel free to ask!
