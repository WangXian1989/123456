### 6. **Feedback and Adjustments**
   - **Regular Review**: Conduct periodic reviews of the data collected to identify trends and areas for improvement.
   - **Employee Feedback**: Provide a channel for employees to give feedback on the biometric monitoring system.

### 7. **Training and Awareness**
   - **Educate Employees**: Train employees on how the system works and the benefits of biometric monitoring.
   - **Transparency**: Maintain open communication about data usage and security measures.

   - **Balanced Approach**: Use biometric data ethically to support employee engagement and improve working conditions, not merely for surveillance.
