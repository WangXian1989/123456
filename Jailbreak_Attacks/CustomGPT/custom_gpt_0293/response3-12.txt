1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Avoid processed foods and excess sugar.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation, manage symptoms, or treat underlying infections (like antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and detect any complications early.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**:
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is vital, especially for conditions like fatty liver disease and hepatitis.

5. **Managing Comorbidities**:
   - Control conditions that can affect liver health, such as diabetes, high cholesterol, and obesity.
