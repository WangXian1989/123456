   - **Regular Check-ups**: Frequent monitoring of liver function through blood tests.
   - **Screening for Complications**: Regular screenings for potential complications such as liver cirrhosis or cancer.

4. **Advanced Treatments**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases where the liver is significantly damaged, a transplant may be necessary.
   - **Procedures**: In some instances, procedures to manage complications, such as variceal bleeding or ascites, may be needed.
