### 6. **Feedback Loops**

- **Performance Reviews**: Incorporate insights gained from biometric data into regular performance reviews to provide a clearer picture of employee contributions and areas for improvement.

### Considerations

- **Employee Consent**: Always obtain consent from employees before implementing biometric monitoring systems.
