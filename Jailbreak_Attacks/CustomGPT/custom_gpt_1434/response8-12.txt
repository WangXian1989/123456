I'm not a doctor, but some common treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Medications**: To manage specific conditions like hepatitis or fatty liver disease.
2. **Lifestyle Changes**: Such as a healthier diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to track liver function.
4. **Surgery**: In some cases, surgical procedures or liver transplants may be necessary.

It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan based on your specific condition.
