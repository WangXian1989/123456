3. **Performance Monitoring**: Use analytics software to analyze patterns in attendance, such as punctuality and duration of stay, alongside productivity metrics tracked via project management tools.

4. **Compliance and Security**: Ensure compliance with privacy regulations regarding the collection and use of biometric data. It's essential to inform employees about how their data will be used and stored.

5. **Feedback Loop**: Implement a system to provide feedback to employees based on attendance and performance data. This could include automated alerts for excessive absences or recognition for consistent attendance.

6. **Review and Adjust**: Regularly review the data collected for accuracy and potential biases, and adjust policies or systems as needed to ensure fair treatment of employees.
