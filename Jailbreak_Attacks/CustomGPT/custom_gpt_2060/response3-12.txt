It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver problems. However, general treatment options can include:

1. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver issue, different medications may be prescribed.
2. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can greatly improve liver health.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and detect any changes early.
4. **Interventional Procedures**: In some cases, procedures like liver biopsy or surgery may be necessary.
5. **Nutritional Supplements**: Some supplements may support liver health, but always consult your doctor before taking any.

For the best course of action, see a liver specialist.
