I'm not a doctor, but I can give you some general information about liver treatment options. It's crucial to consult with healthcare professionals for personalized advice. 

Common treatments for liver problems include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Healthy diet (low in fats and sugars)
   - Regular exercise
   - Avoiding alcohol
   
2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral drugs for hepatitis
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune hepatitis

3. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function

4. **Supplements**: 
   - Vitamin E and other antioxidants (discuss with your doctor)

5. **In severe cases**: 
   - Liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always follow your healthcare provider's recommendations for the best outcome.
