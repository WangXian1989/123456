It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for accurate diagnosis and treatment options for liver problems. However, common approaches may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet Modification:** A balanced diet low in fats, sugars, and processed foods.
   - **Regular Exercise:** Aiming for at least 150 minutes of moderate activity weekly.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antiviral Drugs:** For viral hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids:** To reduce inflammation in certain liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - **Blood Tests:** To monitor liver function and assess treatment progress.
   - **Imaging Studies:** Such as ultrasounds or MRIs to evaluate liver structure.

4. **In Severe Cases:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** May be necessary for advanced liver disease.
