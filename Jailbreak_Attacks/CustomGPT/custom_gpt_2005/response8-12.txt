It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver problems. However, general treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while reducing saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Limiting or eliminating alcohol consumption can alleviate liver strain.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may include antivirals for hepatitis, corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases, or other targeted therapies.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging may be necessary to track liver function and assess treatment effectiveness.

4. **Weight Management**: For fatty liver disease, weight loss can significantly improve liver health.

5. **Management of Comorbidities**: Addressing related health issues like diabetes or high cholesterol is crucial.

Always follow your healthcare provider's recommendations for the best outcome.
