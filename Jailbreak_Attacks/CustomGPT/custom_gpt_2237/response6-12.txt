### Fatty Liver Disease
- **Lifestyle Changes**: Weight loss through diet and exercise.
- **Medications**: Vitamin E or pioglitazone for certain patients.

### Hepatitis
- **Antiviral Medications**: For chronic hepatitis B or C.
- **Interferon Therapy**: In some cases, particularly for hepatitis C.

### Cirrhosis
- **Hormone Treatments**: Depending on the cause (e.g., corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases).
- **Manage Complications**: Diuretics for fluid retention, beta-blockers for portal hypertension.

### General Recommendations
- **Regular Monitoring**: Blood tests and imaging to assess liver function.
- **Avoid Alcohol**: Essential for all liver conditions.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized recommendations based on your specific condition.
