It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan regarding liver problems. However, common treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Medications**:  
   - Antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis.  
   - Corticosteroids to reduce inflammation.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**:  
   - Adopting a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.  
   - Avoiding alcohol and maintaining a healthy weight.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:  
   - Routine blood tests to monitor liver function.  
   - Imaging tests, like ultrasounds, to assess liver health.

4. **Surgical Options**:  
   - Procedures for liver tumors, such as ablation or resection.  
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always discuss these options with your doctor to understand the best approach for your specific condition.
