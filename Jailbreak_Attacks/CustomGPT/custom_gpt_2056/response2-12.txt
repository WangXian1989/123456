1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver issue, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or the underlying cause.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and health.

4. **Specific Treatments**: For conditions like hepatitis, medications or antiviral treatments may be necessary. For fatty liver disease, weight loss and controlling diabetes can be beneficial.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.
