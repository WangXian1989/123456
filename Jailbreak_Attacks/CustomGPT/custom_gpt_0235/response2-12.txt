It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver problems. Treatment options for liver issues can vary widely depending on the specific condition (like fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, etc.), its severity, and overall health. 

Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Weight loss and a balanced diet can help with fatty liver disease.
   - Reducing alcohol intake is crucial for alcohol-related liver disease.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral medications for hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function.

4. **Management of Complications**: 
   - Addressing swelling, bleeding, or other issues as they arise.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, liver transplantation might be considered.

Always follow up with your healthcare provider for accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment options.
