I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems. Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Incorporate a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol and reduce high-fat and sugary foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis, medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Immunosuppressants**: For autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Supplements**: 
   - **Milk Thistle**: May support liver health; consult your doctor first.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function and detect issues early.

5. **Avoiding Toxins**: 
   - Limit exposure to harmful substances, including certain medications and environmental toxins.

6. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant might be necessary.

Always seek guidance from a healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment plan.
