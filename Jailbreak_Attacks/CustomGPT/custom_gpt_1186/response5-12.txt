Common treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This often involves dietary adjustments, such as reducing alcohol intake, eating a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables, and maintaining a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or address the underlying cause (e.g., antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and tests can help monitor liver function and detect any changes early.

4. **Surgery or Procedures**: In some cases, surgical interventions may be needed, such as procedures to remove lesions or treat complications.

5. **Nutritional Support**: Consulting a nutritionist specializing in liver disease can help create a diet plan suited to your needs.
