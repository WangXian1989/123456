1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol, saturated fats, and processed sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight, which is crucial for liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the liver issue, medications may help manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause, such as:
     - Antiviral drugs for hepatitis infections.
     - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and progression of disease.

4. **Surgical Options**:
   - In severe cases, procedures such as liver transplant may be necessary, especially for conditions like cirrhosis.

5. **Natural Supplements**:
   - Some individuals may consider milk thistle or other herbal supplements, but consult with a healthcare provider first to avoid interactions.
