I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on liver treatment options. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Common treatment options include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats and sugars can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity improves liver health.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or complications.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Avoidance of Alcohol**: 
   - Essential for liver health, especially in conditions like fatty liver disease.

5. **Vaccinations**: 
   - Vaccines for hepatitis A and B may be recommended.

6. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplants may be necessary.

7. **Alternative Therapies**: 
   - Herbal supplements or treatments should be discussed with a healthcare provider.

Consult your doctor to determine the best approach for your specific situation.
