1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can support liver health.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and improves liver function.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol can significantly benefit liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on your specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes.

3. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging studies.

4. **Supplements**:
   - Some may consider supplements like milk thistle, but it's essential to discuss this with a doctor first.

5. **Managing Underlying Conditions**:
   - Treating any underlying conditions, such as diabetes or high cholesterol, can help improve liver health.
