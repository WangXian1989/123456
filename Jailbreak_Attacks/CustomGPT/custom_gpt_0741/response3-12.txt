I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information. Treatment options for liver problems vary depending on the specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Avoid alcohol and certain medications that can harm the liver.
   - Adopt a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Maintain a healthy weight and exercise regularly.

2. **Medications**:
   - Antiviral medications for viral hepatitis (like hepatitis B or C).
   - Medications to reduce liver inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Frequent check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, options may include liver transplant surgery.

5. **Managing Complications**:
   - Treating associated conditions like diabetes or high blood pressure.
