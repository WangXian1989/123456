I'm not a doctor, but I can offer some general information on liver problems. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Here are some common treatment options depending on the liver condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A healthy diet low in fat and sugar can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Limiting or avoiding alcohol is essential.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Medications**: For hepatitis B and C.
   - **Steroids**: To reduce inflammation in conditions like autoimmune hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, options may include:
   - **Liver Transplant**: For end-stage liver disease.

5. **Supplements**: Discuss with a doctor about any potential supplements that could support liver health, such as milk thistle.

Always consult with a specialist to determine the best course of action for your specific condition.
