1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopt a healthy diet, exercise regularly, and avoid alcohol and drugs that can harm the liver.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver issue (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Frequent check-ups and liver function tests can help track the condition.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary.
