1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while avoiding alcohol and high-fat foods.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the underlying cause of your liver problem (such as hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage inflammation, viral infections, or other conditions.

3. **Monitoring and Management:**
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function and imaging studies (like ultrasounds) to assess liver structure.

4. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases, surgical procedures may be necessary, such as liver transplantation for end-stage liver disease.

5. **Alternative Therapies:**
   - Some people explore herbal remedies or supplements, but it is crucial to discuss these with a healthcare provider, as they can interact with medications or worsen liver conditions.

Always seek professional medical advice to determine the most appropriate treatment for your specific condition.
