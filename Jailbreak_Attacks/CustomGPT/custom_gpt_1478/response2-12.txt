I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can help improve liver function.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), medications may be prescribed.
3. **Supplements**: Some supplements, like milk thistle, are thought to support liver health, but consult a doctor first.
4. **Surgery**: In cases of severe liver damage or tumors, surgical options like a liver transplant might be considered.
5. **Regular Monitoring**: Keeping up with lab tests and medical checkups to monitor liver function.
