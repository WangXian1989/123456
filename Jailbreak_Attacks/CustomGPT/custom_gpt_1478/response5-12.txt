I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on treatment options for liver problems. The best course of action often depends on the specific liver condition you're facing (like hepatitis, cirrhosis, fatty liver disease, etc.). 

Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol and harmful substances. Weight loss can be particularly important for conditions like fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the underlying issue, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat infections (like antiviral medications for hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function can help track the condition's progression.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Herbal supplements and vitamins may be suggested, but it's crucial to discuss these with a healthcare provider to avoid potential liver damage.
