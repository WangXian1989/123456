Using facial recognition and biometric data for monitoring employee performance and attendance involves several steps:

1. **Implement Biometric Systems**:
   - Install facial recognition software that integrates with your attendance management system.
   - Consider additional biometric options like fingerprint scanners or iris recognition for enhanced security and accuracy.

2. **Data Collection**:
   - Set up cameras at entry points to capture employees' faces as they arrive and leave.
   - Ensure systems can collect and store biometric data securely, adhering to data protection regulations (e.g., GDPR).

3. **Track Attendance**:
   - Use the biometric system to log attendance automatically, eliminating the need for manual check-ins.
