   - **Treat underlying causes** (e.g., stop alcohol consumption, antiviral treatment for hepatitis).

4. **Liver Cancer**:
   - **Surgery** (resection or liver transplantation).
   - **Ablation therapies** or **chemotherapy** as appropriate.
