Common liver problems include fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver tumors, among others.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: For conditions like fatty liver disease, losing weight, eating a balanced diet, and exercising regularly can be highly effective.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, there may be specific medications available. For example, antiviral medications are used for hepatitis, while other drugs may be used to manage symptoms or complications.

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for most liver conditions to prevent further damage.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Work with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function and any changes in your condition.

5. **Surgery or Transplant**: In severe cases, such as advanced liver disease or liver cancer, surgery or a liver transplant may be necessary.
