It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice regarding liver problems. However, common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, cutting alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes.
3. **Vitamin and Mineral Supplements**: To support liver health, based on specific deficiencies.
4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups to monitor liver function and manage any changes.

For specific treatments, consult with a doctor who can provide a tailored plan based on your condition.
