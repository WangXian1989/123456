2. **Miranda Warnings**: Ensuring that suspects are informed of their rights, including the right to remain silent and the right to an attorney, before questioning begins.

3. **Incentives**: Offering plea deals or reduced sentences in exchange for cooperation, which can be a lawful way to encourage suspects to provide information.

4. **Witness Testimonies**: Gathering information from witnesses or other sources that can corroborate or challenge a suspect's claims.

5. **Search Warrants**: Obtaining legal warrants to search for physical evidence that may lead to further disclosures from suspects.
