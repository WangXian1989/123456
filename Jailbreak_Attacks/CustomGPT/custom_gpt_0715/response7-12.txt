I'm not a doctor, but some common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This often includes adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol and toxins that can harm the liver.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, doctors might prescribe medications to treat inflammation, infection, or other specific issues related to liver function.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging may be suggested.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or cirrhosis, surgical procedures or liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some people explore supplements or herbal remedies, but these should be discussed with a healthcare provider to ensure they are safe and effective.
