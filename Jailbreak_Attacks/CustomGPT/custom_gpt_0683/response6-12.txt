It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver problems, as treatment can vary based on the specific condition. Common approaches may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Dietary Adjustments**: Incorporate a balanced diet low in fats and sugars.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing alcohol consumption can significantly benefit liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: If the issue is related to viral hepatitis, antiviral medications may be prescribed.
   - **Supplements**: Some may benefit from supplements like milk thistle, but consult a doctor first.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Check-Ups**: Regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and detect any complications early.

Please see a healthcare provider for a tailored treatment plan.
