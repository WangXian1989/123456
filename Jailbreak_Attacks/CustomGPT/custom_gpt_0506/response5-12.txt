### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a balanced diet, losing weight (if overweight), exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or cirrhosis), medications may be prescribed. For viral hepatitis, antiviral drugs may be used.

3. **Supplements**: Some people find certain supplements (like milk thistle) beneficial, but these should be discussed with a doctor.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: For chronic liver issues, regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and progression of disease are essential.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as for liver cancer or significant damage, surgical options like a liver transplant may be considered.
