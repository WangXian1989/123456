2. **Non-Accusatory Approaches**: Employing a neutral tone and open-ended questions to gather information without confrontation.

3. **Good Cop/Bad Cop**: This traditional technique can create pressure but must avoid coercion.

4. **Miranda Rights**: Ensuring suspects are informed of their rights before questioning, allowing them to understand their options.

5. **Psychological Tactics**: Utilizing behavioral cues and body language analysis to encourage suspects to open up.
