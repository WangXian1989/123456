It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver problems, as they can assess your specific condition and recommend appropriate treatments. General treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Adopting a healthy diet low in saturated fats and high in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Maintaining a healthy weight and exercising regularly.
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and progression of any liver disease.

4. **Avoiding Toxins**: Staying away from substances that can harm the liver, such as certain drugs and toxic chemicals.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures such as a liver transplant might be necessary.

Always seek medical advice for the best course of action tailored to your needs.
