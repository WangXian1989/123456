It's important to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific liver condition. However, common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Medications**:
   - Antiviral drugs for hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - Medications to reduce liver inflammation.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - Adopting a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.
   - Avoiding alcohol and reducing intake of harmful substances.
   - Regular exercise to maintain a healthy weight.

3. **Monitoring and Follow-Up**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - Follow-up imaging studies as needed.

Always seek personalized advice and treatment from a qualified healthcare provider.
