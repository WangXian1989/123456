   - Depending on the condition, doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes, such as antiviral medications for hepatitis.

   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies may be necessary to monitor liver function and assess the progression of the disease.

4. **Surgery or Procedures**: 
   - In severe cases or for conditions like liver tumors, surgical options may be considered.

5. **Transplant**: 
   - For end-stage liver disease, a liver transplant may be the only viable option.
