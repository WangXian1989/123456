### Treatment Options for Liver Problems

- **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), antiviral medications or corticosteroids may be prescribed.
- **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and weight management can significantly impact liver health. Avoiding alcohol and reducing fat intake are also crucial.
- **Dietary Supplements**: Some people may benefit from supplements like milk thistle, though it's important to consult a healthcare provider first.
- **Monitoring and Management**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests help monitor the condition and adjust treatment as necessary.
- **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or cirrhosis, surgical options or liver transplantation might be considered.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
