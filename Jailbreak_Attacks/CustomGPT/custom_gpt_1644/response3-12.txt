1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a healthy, balanced diet. Reduce saturated fats, sugar, and salt intake.

   - For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications may be prescribed.
   - In cases of fatty liver disease, medications to lower cholesterol and blood sugar might be suggested.

3. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function.
   - Blood tests to assess liver enzymes and function.

4. **Surgery**:
