To provide you with accurate recommendations for liver treatment, please consult a healthcare professional. They can help determine the specific condition affecting your liver (such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, etc.) and recommend the best treatment options tailored to your individual needs. 

Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and reducing alcohol intake.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, options may include antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular follow-ups with blood tests and imaging studies to assess liver function.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be considered.

Always prioritize professional medical advice for personalized treatment.
