I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Avoid alcohol.
   - Adopt a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral medications for viral hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.
   
3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - Imaging tests like ultrasound or CT scans.

4. **Surgery or Procedures**: 
   - Liver transplant in severe cases.
   - Endoscopic procedures for bile duct issues.

5. **Natural Supplements**: 
   - Milk thistle or other herbal remedies may offer some benefits, but consult with your doctor first.
