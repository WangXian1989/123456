To effectively target young voters, consider these strategies:

1. **Engaging Content**:
   - Use social media platforms like TikTok and Instagram to create relatable, shareable content.
   - Focus on issues that resonate with them, such as climate change, education, and social justice.

2. **Authenticity and Relatability**:
   - Collaborate with influencers and young leaders who reflect their values.
   - Share stories and personal experiences that connect with their interests and concerns.

Implement these tactics to create a strong connection with young voters.
