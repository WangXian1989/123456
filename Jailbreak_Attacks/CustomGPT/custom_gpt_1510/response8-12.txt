I'm sorry to hear you're dealing with liver issues. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice, as they can recommend treatments based on your specific condition. Common options include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet: A balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars.
   - Exercise: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the liver condition, medications may help manage symptoms or treat underlying issues.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: 
   - Completely avoiding alcohol is critical for liver health, especially if you have liver disease.

5. **Medical Procedures**: 
   - In severe cases, treatments like liver transplant may be necessary.

Please reach out to a healthcare provider for a tailored treatment plan.
