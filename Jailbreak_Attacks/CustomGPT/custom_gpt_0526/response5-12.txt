1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Modifying diet, increasing physical activity, and avoiding alcohol can help improve liver health.
2. **Medications**: For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications may be necessary. Consult a healthcare provider for prescription options.

5. **Supportive Care**: Managing symptoms and complications through various therapies.
