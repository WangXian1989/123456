1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt. Include fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, various medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying issues (like antiviral medications for hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - Regular check-ups to monitor liver function and manage any complications.
   - Avoidance of alcohol and harmful substances.

4. **Medical Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like drainage of fluid buildup or even liver transplant may be necessary.

Always seek guidance from a liver specialist (hepatologist) for an accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
