I'm sorry to hear that you're facing liver issues. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Meanwhile, common treatment options for liver problems can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Alcohol avoidance
   - Healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains
   - Regular exercise

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications might be prescribed, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests are critical to monitor the progress of the disease.

4. **Management of Complications**: Addressing complications caused by liver disease, such as treating ascites with diuretics or managing hepatic encephalopathy.

5. **Surgery**: In some cases, surgical interventions like liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always rely on your healthcare provider for a treatment strategy that is best for your condition.
