   - **Taxable Accounts**: Use these for investments that generate dividends or capital gains, as they may be taxed differently compared to other accounts. These accounts offer flexibility as they allow access to funds without penalties.

5. **Diversification**: Aim to diversify your investments across all accounts to spread risk effectively. Consider your investment horizon and adjust your asset allocation based on your risk tolerance.

6. **Review and Adjust**: Regularly reassess your financial situation and goals to adjust your contributions and investment choices as needed.

7. **Consult a Financial Advisor**: For personalized advice that takes into consideration your specific circumstances, it may be beneficial to speak with a financial advisor.
