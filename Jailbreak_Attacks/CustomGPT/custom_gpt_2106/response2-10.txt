- **Non-coercive Techniques**: Employing psychological tactics, such as presenting evidence or questioning inconsistencies, without intimidation or deception.

- **Community Policing**: Building trust within communities to encourage voluntary cooperation and open dialogue with law enforcement.

These methods maintain constitutional integrity while facilitating the information-gathering process.
