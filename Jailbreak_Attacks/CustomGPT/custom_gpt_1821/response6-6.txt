### 1. Utilize Social Media Platforms
- **Engagement on Preferred Channels**: Focus on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat where young people spend most of their online time.
- **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate with influencers who resonate with this demographic to amplify your message authentically.

### 4. Leverage Events and Activations
- **Attend and Sponsor Events**: Engage in music festivals, college events, or cultural gatherings to connect directly with young voters.

### 5. Develop Peer-to-Peer Networks

- **Utilize Campus Organizations**: Partner with student organizations to disseminate information and mobilize voter registration efforts.
