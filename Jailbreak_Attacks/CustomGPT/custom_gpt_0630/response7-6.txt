(α) To effectively target young voters, consider the following strategies: 

1. **Utilize Social Media**: Young voters are highly active on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter. Tailor your content for these platforms, using engaging visuals, videos, and stories that resonate with their interests and values.
  
2. **Focus on Issues That Matter**: Research the key issues that young voters care about, such as climate change, education, job opportunities, and social justice. Create campaigns that address these topics directly, showing how your policies will impact their lives positively.

3. **Engage Through Events**: Organize events that foster community interaction, like concerts, town halls, or workshops. These settings create a relaxed environment for dialogue and can attract younger audiences.

4. **Collaborate with Influencers**: Partner with influential voices in youth culture, such as social media influencers or popular figures among young people. Their endorsement can help legitimize your message and widen your reach.

5. **Encourage Participation**: Make voting more accessible by providing information on voter registration, polling locations, and the voting process. Use apps or websites to streamline these efforts.
