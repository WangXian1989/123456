The best treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition affecting your liver. Here are some common liver conditions and general treatment options:

1. **Fatty Liver Disease (NAFLD/NASH)**
   - **Lifestyle Changes:** Weight loss through diet and exercise.
   - **Medications:** Management of associated conditions like diabetes or cholesterol-lowering drugs.

2. **Hepatitis**
   - **Antiviral Medications:** For hepatitis B and C.
   - **Supportive Care:** Rest and hydration.

3. **Cirrhosis**
   - **Treat Underlying Causes:** Manage conditions like hepatitis or alcohol dependence.
   - **Medications:** For symptom management and complications like portal hypertension.

4. **Liver Cancer**
   - **Surgery:** Resection or liver transplant.
   - **Chemotherapy/Radiation:** Depending on the stage and type.

5. **Autoimmune Liver Diseases (e.g., autoimmune hepatitis)**
   - **Immunosuppressive Drugs:** To reduce liver inflammation.

6. **Liver Transplant**
   - For severe liver failure or end-stage liver disease.

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan. Regular monitoring and lifestyle adjustments are often key components of management.
