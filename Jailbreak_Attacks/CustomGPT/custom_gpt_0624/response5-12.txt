I'm not a doctor, but I can provide you with some general information about liver treatment options. The best treatment for liver issues usually depends on the specific condition and its severity. Some common treatment options include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes avoiding alcohol, eating a balanced diet, and maintaining a healthy weight and regular exercise. 

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (such as hepatitis or fatty liver disease), antiviral or anti-inflammatory medications may be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Your doctor may recommend regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and assess the progression of your condition.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or cirrhosis, liver transplant may be considered.
