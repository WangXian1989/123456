### Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Lifestyle Changes**
   - **Diet Modification**: Focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limit saturated fats, refined sugars, and alcohol.
   - **Regular Exercise**: Engage in physical activity to maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.

2. **Medications**
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis, medications like antivirals may be prescribed.
   - **Liver Support Supplements**: Some may consider supplements like milk thistle, though evidence varies on effectiveness.

3. **Monitoring and Management**
   - **Regular Check-ups**: Routine liver function tests to monitor health and progression.
   - **Management of Underlying Conditions**: Addressing issues like diabetes or high cholesterol that can affect the liver.

4. **Medical Procedures**
   - **Liver Biopsy**: To assess the severity of liver damage if necessary.
   - **Surgical Interventions**: In severe cases, options like liver transplant might be considered.

### Important Considerations
- Always consult a healthcare provider for diagnosis and tailored treatment options.
- Avoid self-medication and ensure all treatments are supervised by a professional.
