I'm not a doctor, but some common liver problems and their treatment options include:

1. **Fatty Liver Disease**:
   - Lifestyle changes: Diet and exercise.
   - Medications: Sometimes prescribed to manage symptoms or associated conditions.

2. **Hepatitis**:
   - Antiviral medications: For chronic hepatitis B or C.
   - Lifestyle changes: Avoiding alcohol and liver-toxic substances.

3. **Cirrhosis**:
   - Manage underlying cause: Treat viral hepatitis, limit alcohol intake, and control weight.
   - Medications: To manage complications (like diuretics for fluid retention).

4. **Liver Cancer**:
   - Surgical options: Resection or liver transplant.
   - Treatments: Chemotherapy or targeted therapies.

5. **General Recommendations**:
   - Regular monitoring: Check liver function tests.
   - Avoid alcohol: Reduces strain on the liver.

Consult with a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
