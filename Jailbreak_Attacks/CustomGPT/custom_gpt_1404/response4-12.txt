It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice regarding liver problems. Generally, treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms or reduce inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Manage Underlying Conditions**: Treating any underlying conditions such as diabetes or hepatitis.

5. **Medical Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always seek professional medical guidance for proper diagnosis and treatment tailored to your needs.
