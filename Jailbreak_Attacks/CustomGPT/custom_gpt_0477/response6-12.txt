1. **Lifestyle Modifications:**
   - **Dietary Changes:** A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can support liver health. Avoiding alcohol and reducing saturated fats is also recommended.
   - **Weight Management:** If overweight, losing weight can help decrease liver fat and improve liver function.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals:** For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications can be effective.
   - **Corticosteroids:** These may be prescribed for autoimmune liver diseases to reduce inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - **Blood Tests:** Regular check-ups to monitor liver function tests and overall health.
   - **Imaging Studies:** Ultrasounds or CT scans may be used to assess liver structure.

4. **Advanced Treatments:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In cases of severe liver damage or failure, a transplant may be necessary.
   - **Non-Invasive Procedures:** Depending on the condition, treatments like endoscopy may be used.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice and treatment options.
