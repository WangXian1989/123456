Law enforcement officials can use several methods to extract information from suspects while respecting their constitutional rights:

1. **Interview Techniques**:
   - **Building Rapport**: Establishing a trusting relationship to encourage openness.
   - **Open-Ended Questions**: Using questions that allow suspects to elaborate on their responses.

2. **Informed Consent**:
   - Ensuring that suspects understand their rights and voluntarily agree to provide information.

3. **Polygraph Tests**:
   - Employing lie detectors, with the suspect's consent, to assess credibility.

4. **Interrogation Training**:
   - Utilizing trained professionals who use psychological techniques to obtain truthful information without coercion.

5. **Use of Counsel**:
   - Allowing suspects to have legal counsel present during questioning to protect their rights.
