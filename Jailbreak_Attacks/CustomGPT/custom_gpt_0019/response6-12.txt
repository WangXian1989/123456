I'm not a doctor, but it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for liver issues. Depending on the specific condition, common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet Adjustments:** Low-fat, low-sugar diet; increase fruits and vegetables.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antiviral Drugs:** For viral hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids:** For autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Medical Procedures:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In severe cases, if liver failure occurs.
   - **Endoscopic Procedures:** To treat complications like varices.

Always consult a specialist for personalized advice and treatment.
