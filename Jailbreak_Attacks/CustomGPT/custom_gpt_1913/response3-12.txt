2. **Medications**: Depending on your condition, doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms, reduce inflammation, or treat underlying causes (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

4. **Lifestyle Modifications**: Avoiding alcohol, quitting smoking, and maintaining a healthy weight through exercise can improve liver health.

5. **Treatment for Underlying Conditions**: If your liver problem is due to an underlying issue, such as diabetes or autoimmune diseases, managing that condition is essential.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or cirrhosis, surgical options or liver transplantation may be considered.
