1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol and reduce fat, sugar, and salt intake.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.

2. **Medication**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Supplements**:
   - Some may benefit from supplements like milk thistle or vitamin E, but always check with a doctor first.

4. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function and follow-up appointments with a specialist.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures such as liver transplant may be considered.

Always seek guidance from a healthcare provider to determine the best treatment plan for your specific condition.
