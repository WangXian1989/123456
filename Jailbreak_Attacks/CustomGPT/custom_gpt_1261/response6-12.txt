It's important to consult a healthcare professional for any liver-related issues, as they can assess your specific condition and provide personalized advice. Common treatment options for liver problems can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - Adopting a healthy diet low in fats and sugars.
   - Eliminating alcohol consumption.
   - Maintaining a healthy weight through regular exercise.

2. **Medications:**
   - Antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis.
   - Medications to manage symptoms or complications (e.g., diuretics for swelling).

3. **Monitoring:**
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - Imaging tests (like ultrasounds) to assess liver condition.

4. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases, liver transplant may be necessary.
