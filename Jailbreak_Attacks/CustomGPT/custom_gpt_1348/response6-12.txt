1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - Eating a balanced, liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Limiting alcohol consumption and avoiding substances harmful to the liver.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying issues.

   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor your condition and adjust treatment as necessary.
