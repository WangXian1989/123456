Law enforcement officials can utilize various techniques to extract information from suspects while adhering to constitutional rights. Here are some effective methods:

### 1. **Building Rapport**
   - **Establish Trust:** Officers can develop a relationship with the suspect to create a comfortable environment, making them more willing to share information.
   - **Active Listening:** By showing genuine interest in the suspect's perspective, officers can encourage openness.

### 2. **Interview Techniques**
   - **Open-Ended Questions:** Asking questions that require more than yes or no answers can elicit detailed responses.
   - **Cognitive Interviewing:** This technique encourages the suspect to visualize and recount their memories in a structured manner, enhancing recall.

### 3. **Legal Knowledge and Rights Awareness**
