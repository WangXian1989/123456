3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - **Blood Tests:** Regular liver function tests to monitor the state of your liver.
   - **Imaging Tests:** Ultrasounds or other imaging to assess liver structure and health.

4. **Surgical Options:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** For severe liver disease, a transplant may be the only viable option.
