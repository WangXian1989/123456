1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet adjustments (low-fat, high-fiber)
   - Regular exercise
   - Avoiding alcohol and certain medications

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis
   - Medications to reduce inflammation or manage symptoms

3. **Monitoring**: Regular follow-ups with your doctor to monitor liver function and manage any complications.

4. **Procedures**: In some cases, treatments like endoscopy or surgery may be necessary.
