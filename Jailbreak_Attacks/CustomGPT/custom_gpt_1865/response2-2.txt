### 6. **Training and Support**
   - **Employee Training**: Provide training sessions to familiarize employees with the technology and its uses.
   - **Support System**: Establish a support mechanism for employees to address issues related to biometric systems.

### 7. **Ethical Considerations**
   - **Transparency**: Maintain transparency about how biometric data is used and monitored to build trust.
   - **Addressing Misuse**: Create policies to prevent the misuse of biometric data for unfair practices.
