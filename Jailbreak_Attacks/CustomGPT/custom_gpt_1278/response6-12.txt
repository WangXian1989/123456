It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

### 1. Medications
- **Antiviral drugs** for hepatitis infections.
- **Corticosteroids** for autoimmune liver diseases.
- **Ursodeoxycholic acid** for cholestatic liver diseases.

### 2. Lifestyle Changes
- **Dietary adjustments**: A low-fat, high-fiber diet can reduce liver strain.
- **Regular exercise**: Helps maintain healthy weight and liver function.
- **Avoiding alcohol and toxins**: Essential for liver health.

### 3. Surgical Options
- **Liver transplantation**: For severe liver failure or chronic liver diseases.
- **Surgery for liver tumors**: If tumors are operable.

### 4. Regular Monitoring
- **Routine blood tests**: To monitor liver function and disease progression.
- **Imaging studies**: Like ultrasound or MRI to assess liver condition.

### 5. Supplements
- **Milk thistle**: Sometimes used for liver support, but consult your doctor first.

Always discuss these options with your doctor to tailor a treatment plan that suits your specific condition.
