Common treatment options for liver problems include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can support liver health.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids**: For autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Diuretics**: To reduce fluid buildup.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - **Blood Tests**: To check liver function.
   - **Imaging**: Ultrasounds or CT scans to monitor liver health.

4. **Surgery**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases of liver failure.

5. **Supplementation**: 
   - Talk to your doctor about potential vitamins or supplements that could support liver health.

Make sure to follow up with your healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
