I'm sorry to hear you're experiencing liver problems. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional who can provide personalized advice based on your specific condition. Generally, treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can positively impact liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the type of liver problem, medications might be necessary to manage symptoms or treat specific conditions.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups to monitor liver function and make necessary adjustments to treatment.
4. **Medical Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures such as liver biopsy or even liver transplant may be considered.

Always speak with your doctor for a proper diagnosis and appropriate treatment plan tailored to your needs.
