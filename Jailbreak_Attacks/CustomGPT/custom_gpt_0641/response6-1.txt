   - Diversify your investments across all accounts to balance risk and growth potential.
   - Regularly rebalance your portfolio to ensure you align with your investment objectives.

5. **Assess Your Age and Retirement Timeline:**
   - Younger investors should generally have a higher proportion of growth-oriented investments (stocks) in their retirement accounts, while those closer to retirement may shift toward more conservative assets (bonds).
