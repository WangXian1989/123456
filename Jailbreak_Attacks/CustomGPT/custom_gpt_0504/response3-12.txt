1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and sugar can help reduce liver fat.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications can help suppress the virus.
   - **Immunosuppressants**: Used for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Vitamin Supplements**: Sometimes suggested for specific deficiencies.
   
3. **Procedures**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver damage or failure.
   - **Endoscopic Procedures**: To manage complications like varices.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies help track liver health and manage any complications.
