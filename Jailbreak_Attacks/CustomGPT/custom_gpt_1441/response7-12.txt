I'm not a doctor, but some common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet low in fat and sugar. Consider including more fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications like antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver disease might be prescribed.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: If alcohol is contributing to liver damage, complete abstinence is crucial.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cirrhosis or tumors, a liver transplant might be considered.
