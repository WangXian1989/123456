It's essential to consult with a qualified healthcare professional for any medical issues, including liver problems. They can provide a proper diagnosis and recommend appropriate treatment options based on your specific condition. In the meantime, here are some general approaches that might be considered:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet modification:** Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Avoid saturated fats, sugar, and alcohol.
   - **Regular exercise:** Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity each week to help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medical Treatments:**
   - **Medications:** Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes, such as antiviral medications for hepatitis.
   - **Regular monitoring:** Frequent check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.

Always prioritize seeking personalized advice from healthcare providers.
