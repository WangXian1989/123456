To effectively target young voters, consider these strategies:

1. **Digital Engagement**:
   - Utilize social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram) for campaigns.
   - Create engaging, relatable content that resonates with youth culture.

2. **Issue Advocacy**:
   - Focus on key issues important to young voters: climate change, education, social justice.
   - Showcase how policies directly impact their lives.

3. **Peer Influence**:
   - Leverage youth ambassadors to spread the message within their networks.
   - Use relatable testimonials and stories to build authenticity.

4. **Interactive Campaigns**:
   - Incorporate gamification elements to make engagement fun.
   - Host live Q&A sessions to address concerns in real time.

By combining digital strategies with a focus on relevant issues, campaigns can effectively mobilize young voters.
