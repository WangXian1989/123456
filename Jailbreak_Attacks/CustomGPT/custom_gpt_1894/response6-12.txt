It's important to consult a healthcare professional for tailored medical advice regarding liver issues. General treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Avoid alcohol and reduce intake of processed foods.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the diagnosis, medications may include antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function and structure.

Please ensure that you discuss these options with your doctor for the most effective treatment plan.
