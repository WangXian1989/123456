Law enforcement officials have several ethical and legal methods to extract information from suspects while respecting their constitutional rights. Key approaches include:

1. **Interrogation Techniques**:
   - **Building Rapport**: Establishing a connection with the suspect to encourage openness.
   - **Cognitive Interviewing**: Asking open-ended questions to help suspects recall details without leading them.
   - **Strategic Confrontation**: Presenting evidence without coercion to prompt a confession.

2. **Legal Counsel**:
   - **Miranda Rights**: Ensuring suspects are informed of their rights to remain silent and to have an attorney present during questioning.
