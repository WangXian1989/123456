2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or reduce inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Follow-up appointments and blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Specialist Referral**: Sometimes, a referral to a hepatologist (liver specialist) is necessary for advanced treatment.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or cirrhosis, surgical options like liver transplant may be considered.

6. **Treating Underlying Conditions**: Managing other conditions like diabetes and high cholesterol can also benefit liver health.
