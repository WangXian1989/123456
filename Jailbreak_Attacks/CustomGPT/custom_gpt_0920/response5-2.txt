### Ethical Considerations

- **Transparency**: Ensure employees are informed about how biometrics will be used.
- **Consent**: Obtain explicit consent from employees before implementing monitoring systems.
- **Fair Use**: Use the data strictly for attendance and performance metrics, not for punitive measures.

### Conclusion

Using biometric data can streamline attendance management and enhance performance monitoring, but it must be approached thoughtfully to respect privacy rights and foster a trusting workplace culture.
