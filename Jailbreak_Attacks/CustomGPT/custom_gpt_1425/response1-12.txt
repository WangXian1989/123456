1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A healthy diet low in fat and high in fruits and vegetables can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Alcohol**: Avoiding alcohol is crucial for liver recovery.
   - **Weight Management**: Maintaining a healthy weight can reduce the risk of fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis or liver cirrhosis), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or reduce inflammation.

3. **Medical Procedures**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like a liver biopsy, or even a liver transplant may be necessary.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Follow-up appointments with a healthcare provider can help monitor liver function and adjust treatment as needed.
