1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains can support liver health. Avoid alcohol and high-fat foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications**: 
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral medications may be prescribed.
   - **Management Medications**: Drugs to manage symptoms or complications, like diabetes medications if correlated with liver issues.

3. **Monitoring and Follow-up**: 
   - Regular check-ups to monitor liver function and detect any progression of the disease early.

4. **Advanced Treatments**: 
   - **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver damage or failure, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult your healthcare provider for personalized advice and treatment options.
