1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars; consider the Mediterranean diet.

2. **Medications**:
   - **For Hepatitis**: Antiviral drugs like tenofovir or entecavir.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function and progression of disease.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol and Toxins**: 
   - Complete abstinence from alcohol and avoiding exposure to harmful substances can help prevent further liver damage.

5. **Surgery or Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.
