1. **Social Media Engagement**: Utilize platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat, where young people spend time. Create engaging, relatable content that addresses their concerns.

4. **Interactive Campaigns**: Use interactive tools like polls, Q&A sessions, and challenges to engage them actively. Participation can foster a sense of ownership and commitment.

5. **Accessibility**: Ensure that voting information is accessible and easy to understand. Simplifying the registration and voting process can encourage higher turnout.

6. **Community Involvement**: Engage in local events and community service projects that align with young people's interests, creating a presence that feels organic and supportive.
