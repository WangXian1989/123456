I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on treatment options for liver problems. The best treatment largely depends on the specific condition affecting your liver. Here are some common options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats, sugars, and salt can help. Foods rich in antioxidants and fiber are beneficial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver function and overall health.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis, cirrhosis, or fatty liver disease), doctors may prescribe medications to reduce inflammation, manage symptoms, or treat infections.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary, especially for chronic liver disease or liver failure.
