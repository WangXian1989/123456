### 5. **Data Analysis**
   - Use analytics tools to analyze attendance patterns and performance trends.

### 6. **Maintain Privacy and Compliance**

### 7. **Provide Feedback and Training**
   - Use the data collected to provide constructive feedback to employees.
   - Offer training programs based on performance data to help employees improve.

### 8. **Monitor and Adjust**
   - Continuously monitor the effectiveness of the system and make adjustments as needed.
   - Gather employee feedback to ensure the system is perceived as fair and useful.

### 9. **Consider Ethical Implications**
   - Establish clear policies regarding the use of biometric data to prevent misuse.
   - Ensure transparency with employees about how their data will be handled.
