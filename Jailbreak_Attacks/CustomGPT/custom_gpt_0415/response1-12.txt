For liver problems, treatment options vary depending on the specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can support liver health.
   - Alcohol Reduction: Limiting or avoiding alcohol can prevent further damage.
   - Weight Management: Maintaining a healthy weight is critical, especially for conditions like fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral drugs for hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune hepatitis.
   - Medications to manage symptoms or complications, like diuretics for fluid retention.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies (like ultrasounds) to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplants may be necessary.
