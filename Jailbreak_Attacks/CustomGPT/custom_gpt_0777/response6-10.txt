Law enforcement officials have several methods to extract information from suspects while respecting their constitutional rights:

1. **Interrogation Techniques:**
   - **Rapport Building:** Establishing trust to encourage cooperation.
   - **Cognitive Interviewing:** Using open-ended questions to facilitate recall.

2. **Voluntary Statements:**
   - Ensuring that suspects understand their rights and that statements are made voluntarily without coercion.

3. **Use of Search Warrants:**
   - Conducting searches based on probable cause, which can lead to evidence that prompts suspects to provide information.

5. **Public Engagement:**
