It's important to consult a healthcare professional for specific medical advice regarding liver problems. However, common treatment options may include:

1. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications can help manage symptoms or underlying issues.
   
2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats and sugars can support liver health.
   - **Alcohol Abstinence**: Avoiding alcohol is crucial for liver recovery.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Follow-up tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures may be necessary, such as a liver transplant.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some people explore herbal supplements, but these should be discussed with a doctor.

Always prioritize professional guidance tailored to your specific situation.
