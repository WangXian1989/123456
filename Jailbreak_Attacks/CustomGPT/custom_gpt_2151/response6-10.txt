Law enforcement officials can use several methods to extract information from suspects while ensuring they respect constitutional rights. Here are some key techniques:

1. **Building Rapport:**
   - **Establish Trust:** Engaging in conversation to create a comfortable environment.
   - **Active Listening:** Showing genuine interest in the suspect's story can lead to more cooperative responses.

2. **Interrogation Techniques:**
   - **Cognitive Interviewing:** Encouraging suspects to recall details by asking open-ended questions.
   - **Strategic Use of Silence:** Allowing pauses to prompt suspects to fill the gaps, often leading to more information.

3. **Legal Standards:**
   - **Miranda Warnings:** Ensuring suspects are informed of their rights before interrogation.
   - **Voluntary Statements:** Encouraging suspects to provide information voluntarily without coercion.
