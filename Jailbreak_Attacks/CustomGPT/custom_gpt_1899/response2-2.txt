4. **Integration with HR Systems**:

5. **Employee Privacy and Consent**:
   - **Policy Development**: Draft clear policies regarding the use of biometric data, ensuring that employees are informed about the monitoring and its purposes.
   - **Collect Consent**: Obtain explicit consent from employees before implementing biometric systems to maintain compliance with privacy laws and regulations.

6. **Feedback and Adjustment**:
   - **Regular Reviews**: Continually assess the effectiveness of the systems in place and solicit employee feedback to address any concerns regarding privacy or fairness.
   - **Adjustments**: Be open to changing policies and systems based on the feedback and evolving technology.
