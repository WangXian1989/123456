2. **Building Rapport**: Building a connection with suspects through rapport can encourage cooperation. This technique relies on trust, empathy, and psychological strategies to elicit information without coercion.

5. **Polygraph Tests (Lie Detector)**: In some circumstances, law enforcement may offer polygraph testing as a way for suspects to prove their truthfulness regarding their involvement in a crime, thereby encouraging them to share information voluntarily.
