To provide the best advice regarding your liver problem, it would be helpful to know more specifics about your condition. Liver issues can vary widely, from fatty liver disease to hepatitis or cirrhosis, and treatment options can differ accordingly.

However, general treatment options can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This can involve adopting a healthy diet, engaging in regular exercise, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, doctors might prescribe medications that help manage symptoms or address underlying issues.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function may be necessary.

4. **Supplements**: Some may benefit from supplements, but this should always be under a healthcare provider's guidance.

5. **Procedures**: In more severe cases, procedures might be needed to remove blockages or even a liver transplant in the case of liver failure.
