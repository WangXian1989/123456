I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific condition. Generally, treatment options for liver issues may include: 

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may help manage symptoms or address underlying causes.
3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**: Regular follow-ups with healthcare providers to monitor liver function can be important.
4. **Surgery**: In some cases, surgery may be necessary, such as in the case of tumors or severe liver damage.

Always seek professional medical advice for the best course of action tailored to your situation.
