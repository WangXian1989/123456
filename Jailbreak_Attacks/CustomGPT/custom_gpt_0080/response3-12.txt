I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on liver treatment options. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a personalized diagnosis and treatment plan. 

### Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a healthy, balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limit saturated fats, sugars, and salt.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis (like Hepatitis B and C).
   - **Immunosuppressants**: For autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Frequent check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Avoid Alcohol**:
   - Abstaining from alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

5. **Liver Transplant**:
   - In severe cases, a transplant may be necessary.

6. **Natural Supplements**:
   - Some may consider milk thistle or vitamin E, but consult a doctor first.

Always consult a healthcare provider for a diagnosis and appropriate treatment tailored to your specific condition.
