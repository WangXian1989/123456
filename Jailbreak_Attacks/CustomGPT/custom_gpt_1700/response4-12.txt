1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Maintain a healthy diet, focusing on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - Avoid alcohol and limit fatty foods.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or treat underlying issues, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests to monitor liver function and assess the progression of any liver disease.

4. **Surgery or Procedures**: 
   - In severe cases, treatments like a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: 
   - Some people explore complementary therapies, but it's essential to consult with a healthcare provider first.
