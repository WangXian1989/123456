The best treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition. Common treatments include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Healthy diet, weight loss, and regular exercise can improve liver health.
2. **Medications**: Antiviral drugs for hepatitis, corticosteroids for inflammation, or medications to manage symptoms.
3. **Alcohol Abstinence**: Essential for those with alcoholic liver disease.
4. **Regular Monitoring**: Blood tests and imaging to track liver function.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, liver transplant may be necessary.

Consult a healthcare provider for personalized advice.
