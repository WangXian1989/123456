1. **Lifestyle Changes:** 
   - **Diet:** Adopt a liver-friendly diet, focusing on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while limiting saturated fats, sugars, and salt.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.
   - **Avoid Alcohol:** Reducing or eliminating alcohol is crucial, especially for conditions like alcoholic liver disease.

2. **Medications:** Depending on the underlying cause, medications might include antiviral drugs for hepatitis or medications to manage symptoms and complications.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-Ups:** Regular blood tests to monitor liver function can help track your liver health.

4. **Surgery or Procedures:** In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsies or even a liver transplant might be necessary.

5. **Supplements:** Some people consider supplements like milk thistle, but consult with a healthcare provider first.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options!
