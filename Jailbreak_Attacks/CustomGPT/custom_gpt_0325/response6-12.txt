It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver issues. Treatment options can vary based on the specific condition, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, or cirrhosis. 

### Common Treatment Options Include:
- **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthier diet and exercise routine.
- **Medications**: Managing symptoms or underlying conditions, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

Always seek guidance from a medical professional for an accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
