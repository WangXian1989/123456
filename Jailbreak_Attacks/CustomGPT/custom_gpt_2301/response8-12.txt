I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing a liver problem. It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options. They may recommend lifestyle changes, medications, or procedures depending on the specific liver condition. Here are some general treatment options that might be discussed with your doctor:

1. **Lifestyle Modifications**: This can include a healthy diet low in fats and sugars, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, several medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Dietary Supplements**: Certain vitamins and supplements may support liver health, but it's crucial to discuss these with a doctor.

4. **Surgery or Procedures**: In more severe cases, procedures such as gallbladder removal or even a liver transplant might be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare provider who can provide a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan specific to your situation.
