1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, losing weight if overweight, and avoiding alcohol can be essential.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the issue, doctors may prescribe antiviral medications (for viral hepatitis) or other specific treatments.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular blood tests and imaging can help monitor liver function and detect any changes.
