   - **Installation**: Set up cameras and biometric devices at entry points and important work areas.
   - **Data Collection**: Start collecting data on attendance by recording entry and exit times through facial recognition or other biometrics.

### 5. **Monitoring Performance**
   - **Analysis**: Use the data collected to analyze attendance patterns and correlate them with performance metrics.
   - **Reporting**: Generate reports that summarize attendance and performance data for management review.

### 6. **Feedback and Support**

   - **Support**: Provide assistance and training for any new systems introduced.

### 7. **Continuous Improvement**
   - **Review Policies**: Regularly assess the effectiveness of surveillance strategies and make adjustments as necessary.
