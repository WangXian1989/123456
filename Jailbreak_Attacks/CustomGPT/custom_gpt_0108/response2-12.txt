I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems based on general medical knowledge:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats, sugar, and salt can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Medications**: For hepatitis B or C.
   - **Corticosteroids**: To reduce inflammation in autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Supplements**:
   - **Milk Thistle**: May support liver health.
   - **Vitamin E**: Sometimes used for non-alcoholic fatty liver disease.

4. **Surgical Options**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver disease or failure.

5. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - Regular check-ups and imaging tests to monitor liver function.

It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
