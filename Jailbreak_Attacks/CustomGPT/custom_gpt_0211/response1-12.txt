For liver issues, treatment options depend on the specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Limit Alcohol:** Reduce or eliminate alcohol intake.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antivirals:** For viral hepatitis, medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Anti-inflammatory Drugs:** To reduce inflammation in the liver.

3. **Monitoring:**
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests to track the condition.

4. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplants may be necessary.

5. **Nutritional Support:**
   - Consult a dietitian for tailored dietary recommendations.

Always consult your healthcare provider for a treatment plan that addresses your specific needs.
