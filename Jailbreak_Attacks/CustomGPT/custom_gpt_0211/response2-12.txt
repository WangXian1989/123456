2. **Medications**:

4. **Vaccinations**:
   - Vaccines for hepatitis A and B can prevent further liver damage.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

6. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - Some people explore herbal supplements or therapies, but consult your doctor before starting any.

For a precise diagnosis and tailored treatment, it's essential to consult a healthcare professional.
