I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about treatment options for liver problems. The best treatment will depend on the specific condition affecting your liver. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eating a healthy diet low in saturated fats, refined sugars, and salt can support liver health.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: If liver disease is related to alcohol, complete abstinence is crucial.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the diagnosis, medications can help manage conditions like hepatitis or fatty liver disease.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**:
   - Regular follow-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function tests and overall health.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Complementary Therapies**: 
   - Some people explore herbal remedies or supplements, but these should be discussed with a healthcare provider.
