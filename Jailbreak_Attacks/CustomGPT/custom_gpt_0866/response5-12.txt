1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Abstaining from alcohol, following a balanced diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and exercising regularly.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, antiviral medications for viral hepatitis, corticosteroids for autoimmune liver disease, or medications to manage symptoms may be recommended.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular follow-ups with healthcare providers to monitor liver function and any progression of the disease.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cirrhosis, liver transplant may be necessary.
