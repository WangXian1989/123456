Law enforcement officials can employ several techniques to extract information from suspects while respecting constitutional rights:

1. **Interview Techniques:**
   - **Building Rapport:** Establishing a connection can encourage suspects to share information voluntarily.
   - **Open-Ended Questions:** Asking broad questions allows suspects to provide more context without feeling cornered.

2. **Surveillance and Observational Methods:**
   - **Behavioral Analysis:** Observing body language and facial expressions can provide insights into a suspect's truthfulness.
   - **Background Research:** Gathering information on a suspect's history can inform questioning strategies.

3. **Legal Interrogation Strategies:**
   - **Miranda Warnings:** Ensuring suspects understand their rights to remain silent and have legal representation before questioning.
   - **Cognitive Interviewing:** Using memory-enhancing techniques to help suspects recall events without coercion.

4. **Use of Technology:**
   - **Digital Evidence:** Analyzing phone records, emails, and social media can provide context and support investigations.
