I'm not a doctor, but it's important to get a proper diagnosis for your liver condition. Generally, treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver problem, medications may help manage symptoms or fight infections.
3. **Nutritional Support**: Sometimes, dietary supplements or specific diets can assist liver function.
4. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, liver transplants may be necessary.

It's crucial to consult your healthcare provider for personalized advice and treatment options.
