1. **Medication**: Depending on the condition, medications like antiviral drugs for hepatitis or drugs to manage inflammation may be prescribed.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats, sugars, and salt can help. Foods high in fiber, such as fruits and vegetables, are beneficial.

3. **Alcohol Avoidance**: Avoiding alcohol is crucial, especially for those with alcoholic liver disease.

4. **Monitoring and Follow-up**: Regular check-ups for liver function tests to monitor liver health.

5. **Alternative Treatments**: Some may benefit from supplements like milk thistle, but these should be discussed with a doctor.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or cirrhosis, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional to determine the best course of action for your specific situation.
