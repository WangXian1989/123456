1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Avoid high-fat, sugary, and processed foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.
   - **Weight Management**: Achieving a healthy weight can reduce liver fat and inflammation.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the underlying cause (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), your doctor may prescribe specific medications.

3. **Limit Alcohol**:
   - Avoid or significantly reduce alcohol intake, as it can worsen liver conditions.

4. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help monitor your condition.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for the best treatment tailored to your specific condition.
