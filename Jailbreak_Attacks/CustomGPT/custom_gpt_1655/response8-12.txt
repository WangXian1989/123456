I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver issues. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan. Generally, treatment options for liver problems can vary based on the specific condition and severity, but they may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes, such as antiviral medications for hepatitis.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some may consider herbal supplements, but it's crucial to discuss these with a doctor to avoid potential liver damage.

Always seek professional medical advice for personalized recommendations.
