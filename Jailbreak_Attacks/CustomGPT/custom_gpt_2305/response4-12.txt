1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, reducing alcohol consumption, and maintaining a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on your condition, medications to manage symptoms or underlying issues may be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.
