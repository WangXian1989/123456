1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Incorporating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while avoiding fatty and processed foods.
   - **Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing weight if overweight to reduce liver stress.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), doctors may prescribe medications like antiviral drugs or corticosteroids.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and the progression of the disease.

4. **Managing Underlying Conditions**:
   - Controlling diabetes, high cholesterol, or other related health issues.

5. **Liver Transplant**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant might be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options.
