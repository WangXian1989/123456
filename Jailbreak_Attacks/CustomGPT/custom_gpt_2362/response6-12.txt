1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol and limit high-fat foods.

   - **Weight Management:** Losing excess weight can be beneficial, especially for fatty liver disease.

   - **Antivirals:** For viral hepatitis, medications like lamivudine, entecavir, or tenofovir can be effective.
   - **Steroids:** In autoimmune liver diseases, corticosteroids may be prescribed.

3. **Interventional Procedures:**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In advanced liver disease or cirrhosis, a transplant might be necessary.
   - **Endoscopic Procedures:** For issues like variceal bleeding associated with cirrhosis, endoscopic treatments may be performed.

4. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - **Follow-Up Care:** Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.
