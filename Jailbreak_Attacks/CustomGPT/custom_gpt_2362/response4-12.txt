1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoiding alcohol and processed foods can be crucial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Certain medications may be prescribed to manage specific liver conditions, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis or medications to reduce inflammation.

3. **Supplements**: 
   - Some people benefit from supplements like milk thistle, though you should consult with a healthcare provider before starting any.

4. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and detect any changes early.

5. **Procedures**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or endoscopy may be necessary.

6. **Liver Transplant**: 
   - If the liver is severely damaged, a transplant may be considered.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
