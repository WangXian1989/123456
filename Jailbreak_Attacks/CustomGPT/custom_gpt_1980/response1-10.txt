1. **Voluntary Interviews**: Engaging suspects in conversations where they willingly share information without coercion.

3. **Cognitive Interview Techniques**: Using strategies that enhance memory recall without pressure, helping suspects to provide information freely.

7. **Background Research**: Gathering information before interacting with the suspect, allowing for more informed and relevant questions.
