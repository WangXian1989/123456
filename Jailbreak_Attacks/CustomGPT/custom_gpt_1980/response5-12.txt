1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, avoiding alcohol, and exercising regularly can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed. For example, antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests can help manage and monitor liver health.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical options like liver transplant may be considered.
