1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limit fats, sugars, and alcohol.
   - **Exercise**: Aim for regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Medications**: For viral hepatitis, antiviral drugs may be prescribed.
   - **Corticosteroids**: For autoimmune liver diseases to reduce inflammation.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Avoid Toxins**:
   - Minimize exposure to harmful substances and medications that can affect the liver.

5. **Supplements**:
   - Some may benefit from supplements like milk thistle, but consult your doctor first.

Always seek guidance from a healthcare provider for the best course of action tailored to your specific condition and needs.
