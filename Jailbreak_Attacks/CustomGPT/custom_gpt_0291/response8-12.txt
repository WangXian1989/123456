I'm sorry to hear you're having liver problems. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. However, common treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Maintaining a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the type of liver disease, medications may help manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.
4. **Medical Procedures**: In some cases, procedures like endoscopy or surgery might be necessary.

Always follow your doctor's recommendations for the best course of action.
