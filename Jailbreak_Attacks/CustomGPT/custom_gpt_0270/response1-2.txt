3. **Integration**: Integrate the biometric system with your attendance management software to automate clock-ins and clock-outs.

4. **Performance Monitoring**: Use facial recognition for real-time monitoring to assess engagement during meetings or training sessions.

5. **Analysis**: Analyze the collected data to identify patterns in attendance and performance, addressing any discrepancies or trends.

6. **Privacy Policies**: Establish clear privacy policies and inform employees about data usage to maintain transparency.

7. **Feedback Loop**: Regularly review the system's effectiveness and gather employee feedback for improvements.
