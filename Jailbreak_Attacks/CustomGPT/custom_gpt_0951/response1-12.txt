2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the condition, medications might be prescribed to reduce inflammation, manage symptoms, or address specific diseases like hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and detect any progression of disease.
