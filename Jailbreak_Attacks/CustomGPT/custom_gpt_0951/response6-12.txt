To provide the best guidance, it’s crucial to know more about the specific liver condition you’re dealing with (e.g., fatty liver, hepatitis, cirrhosis, etc.). However, some general treatment options for liver issues include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet Modification:** Focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while reducing fats, sugars, and salts.
   - **Regular Exercise:** Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity per week to improve liver health.

2. **Medical Treatments:**
   - **Medications:** Depending on the condition, specific medications may be prescribed (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).
   - **Nutritional Supplements:** Some supplements, like vitamin E, may be considered for certain types of liver diseases but should only be taken under doctor supervision.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - **Routine Check-ups:** Frequent liver function tests and imaging studies to monitor the condition.
   - **Tailored Management Plans:** Working with a healthcare provider to develop a personalized treatment strategy based on ongoing assessments.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for a detailed evaluation and tailored treatment plan.
