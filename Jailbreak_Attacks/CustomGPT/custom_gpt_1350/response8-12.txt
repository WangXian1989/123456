3. **Regular Monitoring:**

4. **Avoiding Toxins:**
   - Stay away from substances that can harm the liver, such as certain medications, chemicals, and toxins.

5. **Surgery or Procedures:**
   - In severe cases, procedures may be necessary, including liver transplantation for end-stage liver disease.

6. **Supplements and Herbal Remedies:**
   - Some people consider milk thistle or vitamin E supplements; however, consult with a healthcare provider before starting any supplements, as they can interact with medications.
