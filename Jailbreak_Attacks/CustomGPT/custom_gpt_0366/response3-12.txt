I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a healthy diet, low in fat and high in fruits and vegetables.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Alcohol**: Avoid alcohol to reduce stress on the liver.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids**: To reduce inflammation in autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Ursodeoxycholic Acid**: For primary biliary cholangitis.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - Consult your doctor before considering herbal remedies or supplements.

Always consult a healthcare professional to discuss the best treatment plan for your specific condition.
