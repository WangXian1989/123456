I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may help manage symptoms, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver disease.

2. **Dietary Changes**: A liver-friendly diet, low in fat and sugar, and high in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can support liver health.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Stopping alcohol consumption is crucial for recovery if liver damage is related to alcohol use.

4. **Regular Exercise**: Maintaining a healthy weight through regular exercise can reduce stress on the liver.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options.
