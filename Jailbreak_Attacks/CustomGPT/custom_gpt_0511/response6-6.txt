4. **Peer-to-Peer Outreach**:
   - **Mobilizing Student Organizations**: Partner with campus groups and youth organizations to reach potential voters through established networks.
   - **Encourage Grassroots Movements**: Empower young voters to organize their own events, creating a sense of ownership and involvement in the political process.
