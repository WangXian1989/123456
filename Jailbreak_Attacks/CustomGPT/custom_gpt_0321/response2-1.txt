   - **Taxable Accounts:**

### 3. **Diversify Investments:**
   - **Asset Allocation:**
     - Diverse your portfolio across stocks, bonds, and cash based on your risk tolerance. Generally, equities are favored for long-term growth in tax-advantaged accounts.

### 4. **Consider Your Time Frame:**
   - **Short-Term Needs:** 
     - Use taxable accounts for money you may need in the next few years, as they can provide more liquidity without penalties.

   - **Long-Term Goals:** 
     - Keep long-term investments in 401(k) and IRA accounts to benefit from compounding without immediate tax implications.

### 5. **Regularly Reassess Your Strategy:**
   - Reevaluate your allocation based on changes in your income, expenses, and market conditions. Adjust contributions as necessary to stay aligned with your goals.

### 6. **Seek Professional Guidance:**
   - Consider consulting a financial advisor for personalized advice tailored to your specific financial situation.
