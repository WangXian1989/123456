8. **Address Misinformation**: Combat misinformation by providing accurate, straightforward information about the voting process and policies.

9. **Create a Community**: Foster a sense of belonging by encouraging peer conversations and community involvement around important issues.

10. **Feedback Mechanism**: Create opportunities for young voters to voice their opinions and concerns, making them feel invested in the political process.
