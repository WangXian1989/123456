#### 3. **Performance Monitoring**
   - **Usage of Biometric Data**: Biometric data can help analyze productivity levels by integrating with performance management systems.
   - **Implementation**: Systems can track time spent on tasks, reducing idle time by monitoring breaks and activity levels through movement detection.
   - **Benefits**:
     - Helps managers assess productivity related to specific tasks or projects.
     - Provides insights for performance reviews.

#### 4. **Data Privacy and Compliance**
   - **Legal Considerations**: Ensure compliance with data protection regulations (like GDPR, CCPA) when collecting biometric data.
   - **Transparency**: Communicate clearly with employees about what data is being collected, for what purposes, and how it will be stored and protected.
